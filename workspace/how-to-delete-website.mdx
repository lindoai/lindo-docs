---
title: "How to Delete a Website"
description: "Removing a website permanently"
---

Permanently remove a website from your workspace.

## Warning

**Deletion is permanent.** You cannot recover a deleted website. Consider exporting first.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Website">
    Locate the website in your Websites Grid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Actions Menu">
    Hover over the website card to reveal actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Click **Delete** from the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    A confirmation dialog appears. Confirm to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Website Removed">
    The website is permanently deleted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Gets Deleted

- All pages
- All content
- All settings
- Analytics data
- CRM data
- Custom domain settings

## What Doesn't Get Deleted

- Your account
- Other websites
- Workspace settings
- Credit balance

## Before Deleting

Consider these alternatives:

### Export First

Download a backup:
1. Click **Export** instead of Delete
2. Save the ZIP file
3. Then delete if needed

### Unpublish Instead

Make it private without deleting:
1. Open the website
2. Unpublish all pages
3. Website exists but isn't public

### Downgrade

If hitting website limits:
1. Downgrade less important sites
2. Keep them as drafts
3. Delete only if necessary

## Recovering Deleted Websites

**Not possible.** Once deleted, websites cannot be recovered.

If you accidentally deleted:
- Check if you exported recently
- Contact support (no guarantees)
- Recreate from scratch

## Tips

- **Export before deleting** - Always have a backup
- **Double-check** - Make sure it's the right website
- **Consider alternatives** - Unpublish or downgrade first
