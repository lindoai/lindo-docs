---
title: "What is Purchase History"
description: "Record of your credit purchases"
---

Purchase History shows all the credit purchases you've made for your workspace.

## What It Shows

The purchase history displays:

- Purchase date
- Credit amount
- Price paid
- Payment method

## Accessing History

1. Open **Settings** in sidebar
2. Go to **Credits** tab
3. View purchase history section

## Understanding Purchases

Each entry shows:

- When you bought credits
- How many credits
- Total cost
- Transaction status

## Using the History

The history helps you:

- Track spending on credits
- Plan future purchases
- Reconcile with accounting
- Understand usage patterns

## Tips

- Review purchases monthly
- Budget for credit needs
- Consider bulk purchases for savings
- Keep records for accounting
