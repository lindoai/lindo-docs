---
title: "How to View Invoice Details"
description: "Check detailed information about a client invoice"
---

View complete details about any invoice you've sent to clients.

## Accessing Invoice Details

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Go to **Billing** tab (for all invoices)
3. Or select a client → **Billing** tab
4. Click on any invoice

## Invoice Information

Each invoice shows:

- **Invoice Number**: Unique identifier
- **Client Name**: Who it's billed to
- **Service Name**: What was charged
- **Amount**: Price and currency
- **Status**: Open, Paid, or Refunded
- **Date**: When it was created
- **Due Date**: Payment deadline

## Invoice Actions

From the details view:

- **Copy Link**: Get payment URL
- **Download**: Save as PDF
- **Refund**: Process refund (if paid)

## Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Open | Awaiting payment |
| Paid | Payment received |
| Refunded | Payment returned |

## Tips

- Monitor open invoices regularly
- Follow up on overdue payments
- Keep records of all transactions
