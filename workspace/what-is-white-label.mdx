---
title: "What is White Label"
description: "Brand Lindo.ai as your own platform"
---

White Label lets you brand Lindo.ai as your own platform. Your clients see your logo, your domain, and your brand—they never see "Lindo.ai."

<Note>
Available on Business, Elite, and Reseller plans.
</Note>

## What You Can Customize

**Branding**
- Full Logo (200x50px) — Your logo in the header
- Favicon (32x32px) — Browser tab icon
- Login Background (1920x1080px) — Custom login page image

**Domain**
- WebApp Domain — app.youragency.com
- Website Domain — sites.youragency.com
- Email Domain — Transactional emails from your domain

**Settings**
- Workspace Name — Your agency name
- Default Language — Interface language for clients
- Client Registration — Allow self-signup

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure branding">
    Upload your logo, favicon, and optionally a login background image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up domain">
    Connect your custom domain so clients access app.youragency.com instead of lindo.ai.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite clients">
    Add clients to your workspace. They see your branded platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clients create">
    Clients build websites under your brand, never knowing about Lindo.ai.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## White Label Tabs

Settings → White Label has five tabs:

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| Workspace | Name, client settings, agentic mode |
| Appearance | Logo, favicon, login background |
| Domain | Custom domain setup |
| Integrations | Stripe, analytics, chat tools |
| Custom Code | Header/body code injection |

## Benefits

- **Brand consistency** — Everything matches your brand
- **Professional image** — Clients see a polished platform
- **Client trust** — Your brand, your relationship
- **Pricing control** — Set your own prices
