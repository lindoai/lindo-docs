Customize the background image on your login page.

## What It Does

The login background:
- Appears behind the login form
- Sets the visual tone
- Reinforces your brand
- Creates professional impression

## Requirements

- Size: 1920x1080 pixels (recommended)
- Format: PNG or JPG
- High quality
- Not too busy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to White Label">
    Select **White Label** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Appearance Tab">
    Navigate to the Appearance tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Background Upload">
    Locate the login background area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upload">
    Click upload or drag and drop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select File">
    Choose your background image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** or **Update**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Design Tips

### Good Backgrounds

- Subtle gradients
- Abstract patterns
- Blurred photography
- Brand-colored overlays

### Avoid

- Busy images
- Distracting patterns
- Low resolution
- Text-heavy images

## Ensuring Readability

The login form appears over the background:
- Use darker or lighter images
- Add overlay if needed
- Test form visibility

## Testing

After upload:
- Sign out
- View login page
- Check form readability
- Test on different screens
