Add Intercom for customer messaging.

## What is Intercom

Intercom provides:
- In-app messaging
- Customer support
- User engagement
- Help center

## Prerequisites

- Intercom account
- Intercom App ID

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your App ID">
    1. Log into app.intercom.com
    2. Go to Settings → Installation
    3. Copy your App ID
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to Settings → White Label → Integrations tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Intercom">
    Locate Intercom card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter App ID">
    Paste your Intercom App ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click Save or Connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You Get

After connecting:
- Messenger widget appears
- In-app messaging
- User tracking
- Support inbox

## Where It Appears

Intercom shows on:
- Workspace login page
- Client dashboard
- Workspace pages

## Tips

- Set up product tours
- Create help articles
- Use automated messages
- Track user events
