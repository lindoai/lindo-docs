Reseller Tiers let you create subscription plans that your clients can subscribe to. Each tier defines what your clients get — website limits, monthly credits, and pricing.

## What It Is

Tiers are subscription plans you define in your workspace settings. When a client subscribes to a tier, they get:

- A set number of websites they can create
- Monthly AI credits that reset each billing cycle
- An active subscription managed through Stripe

## How Tiers Work

### Creating Tiers

1. Go to **Settings → Tiers** tab
2. Click **Add Tier** (up to 4 tiers)
3. Set the tier name, website limit, monthly credits, price, and currency
4. Click **Save**

Tiers are automatically assigned IDs based on their order: `tier1`, `tier2`, `tier3`, `tier4`.

### Supported Currencies

You can price tiers in:
- USD ($)
- EUR (€)
- GBP (£)
- AUD (A$)
- CAD (C$)

### Client Subscription Flow

1. Client opens the **Plans & Billing** modal
2. Available tiers are displayed as cards
3. Client clicks **Subscribe** on their chosen tier
4. They're redirected to a Stripe Checkout page
5. After payment, the subscription is activated
6. Monthly credits are allocated automatically

### Upgrade & Downgrade

Clients with an active subscription can:
- **Upgrade** to a higher tier — prorated charges apply
- **Downgrade** to a lower tier — prorated credit applied
- **Cancel** their current subscription

The tier cards show contextual buttons based on the client's current plan.

## Example Setup

| Tier | Websites | Credits/mo | Price |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| Starter | 1 | 500 | $29/mo |
| Pro | 5 | 1,300 | $50/mo |
| Business | 15 | 5,000 | $99/mo |
| Enterprise | 50 | 20,000 | $199/mo |

## Requirements

- Reseller (Whitelabel) plan
- Stripe connected in workspace integrations
