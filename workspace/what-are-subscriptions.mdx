---
title: "What are Subscriptions"
description: "Recurring billing explained"
---

Subscriptions are recurring invoices that bill automatically.

## How They Work

1. Create subscription for client
2. Set frequency (monthly, yearly, weekly)
3. Client pays first invoice
4. Automatic billing continues
5. Cancel anytime

## Subscription Types

| Frequency | Billing |
|-----------|---------|
| Monthly | Every month |
| Yearly | Every year |
| Weekly | Every week |

## Benefits

### For You

- Predictable revenue
- Automatic billing
- Less manual work
- Recurring income

### For Clients

- Convenient payments
- No manual invoices
- Consistent service

## Managing Subscriptions

- View active subscriptions
- Cancel when needed
- Track payment history
- Update amounts

## Tips

- Use for ongoing services
- Set clear expectations
- Monitor for failures
- Communicate changes
