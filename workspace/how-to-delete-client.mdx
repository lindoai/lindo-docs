---
title: "How to Delete a Client"
description: "Removing client from workspace"
---

Remove a client from your Workspace CRM.

## Before Deleting

Consider:
- Client's websites (will be affected)
- Outstanding invoices
- Active subscriptions
- Data preservation needs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace CRM">
    Click **CRM** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Client">
    Click the client in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Info Tab">
    Ensure you're on the Info tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Click the Delete button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge and confirm deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Happens

When you delete a client:
- Client account removed
- Access revoked
- Websites may be affected
- Billing history preserved

## Important Notes

- Action cannot be undone
- Communicate with client first
- Handle outstanding billing
- Export data if needed

## Tips

- Settle billing first
- Export important data
- Communicate clearly
- Consider archiving instead
