description: "Configuring service name, price, currency"
---

Configure and send an invoice to a client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Create Invoice">
    From client's Billing tab, click Create Invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Service Name">
    Enter what you're billing for:
    - "Website Design"
    - "Monthly Maintenance"
    - "SEO Services"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Price">
    Enter the amount to charge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Currency">
    Choose currency:
    - USD
    - EUR
    - GBP
    - And more
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Frequency">
    Choose billing type:
    - One-time
    - Monthly
    - Yearly
    - Weekly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click Send Invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Invoice Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Service Name | What you're billing for |
| Price | Amount to charge |
| Currency | Payment currency |
| Frequency | One-time or recurring |

## Tips

- Clear service names
- Accurate pricing
- Correct currency
- Appropriate frequency
