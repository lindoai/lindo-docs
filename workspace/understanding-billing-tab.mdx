---
title: "Understanding the Billing Tab"
description: "All invoices across clients"
---

The Billing tab in Workspace CRM shows all invoices across all clients.

## What It Shows

- All invoices in one place
- Filter and search
- Invoice details
- Quick actions

## Invoice List

Each invoice shows:
- Invoice number
- Client name
- Amount
- Status
- Date

## Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Open | Awaiting payment |
| Paid | Payment received |
| Refunded | Payment returned |

## Filtering

Filter invoices by:
- Status (open, paid, refunded)
- Date range
- Client

## Invoice Actions

- View details
- Copy payment link
- Download PDF
- Refund

## Tips

- Review regularly
- Follow up on open invoices
- Track payment patterns
