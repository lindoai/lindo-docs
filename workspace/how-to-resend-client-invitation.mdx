---
title: "How to Resend Client Invitation"
description: "Send another invitation email to a client"
---

Resend an invitation email if a client didn't receive or lost their original invitation.

## When to Resend

Resend invitations when:

- Client didn't receive email
- Email went to spam
- Invitation link expired
- Client lost the email

## Resending Invitation

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Find the client
3. Look for pending invitation status
4. Click **Resend Invitation**
5. New email is sent

## What the Client Receives

The invitation email includes:

- Welcome message
- Link to set up account
- Your workspace branding (if white label)

## Tips

- Ask client to check spam folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Consider calling to confirm receipt
- Update email if bouncing
