---
title: "Understanding the Clients Tab"
description: "Client list and management"
---

The Clients tab in Workspace CRM shows all your agency clients.

## What It Shows

- Client list on left
- Client details on right
- Search functionality
- Add client button

## Client List

Each client shows:
- Avatar (initials)
- Name
- Email
- Status indicator

## Searching Clients

Use the search input to:
- Filter by name
- Filter by email
- Find specific clients

## Adding Clients

Click "Add Client" to:
- Create new client
- Enter details
- Send invitation

## Selecting a Client

Click a client to see:
- Full details
- Websites
- Credits
- Billing
- Subscriptions

## Tips

- Use search for large lists
- Keep client info updated
- Review regularly
