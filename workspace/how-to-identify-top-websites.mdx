---
title: "How to Identify Top Websites"
description: "Find your best performing websites"
---

Identify which websites in your workspace receive the most traffic.

## Finding Top Websites

1. Open **Analytics** from sidebar
2. Look at **Top Websites** section
3. View ranked list by traffic

## What You See

The top websites section shows:

- Website name
- Visitor count
- Page views
- Ranking

## Using This Data

Top websites data helps you:

- Identify successful sites
- Learn from best performers
- Prioritize optimization
- Report to clients

## Tips

- Monitor changes over time
- Analyze what makes top sites successful
- Apply learnings to other sites
- Share insights with clients
