Some templates in the Templates panel have an "AI Agent" badge, indicating special features.

## What is AI Agent Badge?

Templates with the AI Agent badge:

- Were created using AI agents
- May have advanced features
- Showcase AI capabilities
- Demonstrate best practices

## How They're Different

AI Agent templates:

- Generated by AI
- Modern design patterns
- Optimized structure
- Ready for AI editing

## Using AI Agent Templates

1. Browse templates panel
2. Look for AI Agent badge
3. Preview the template
4. Click to use

## Benefits

- High-quality starting point
- AI-optimized structure
- Easy to customize with AI
- Modern design patterns

## Tips

- Great for learning AI capabilities
- Customize with AI chat
- Use as inspiration
- Combine with your content
