Add Google Analytics to track workspace usage.

## What is Google Analytics

Google Analytics provides:
- Visitor tracking
- Traffic sources
- User behavior
- Conversion tracking

## Prerequisites

- Google Analytics account
- GA4 Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Measurement ID">
    1. Log into analytics.google.com
    2. Go to Admin → Data Streams
    3. Select your stream
    4. Copy Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to Settings → White Label → Integrations tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Google Analytics">
    Locate Google Analytics card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Measurement ID">
    Paste your GA4 Measurement ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click Save or Connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Verification

After connecting:
- Check GA4 Realtime report
- Visit your workspace
- Verify data appears

## What Gets Tracked

GA tracks:
- Workspace login page
- Client dashboard
- Workspace pages

Does NOT track:
- Individual websites (use website settings)

## Tips

- Use GA4 (not Universal Analytics)
- Set up conversions
- Create custom reports
- Monitor regularly
