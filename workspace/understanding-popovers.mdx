title: "Understanding Popovers"
description: "Dropdown menus and tooltips"
---

Popovers are small overlay elements that provide additional options or information.

## Types of Popovers

### Dropdown Menus

Click-triggered menus showing:

- Action options
- Selection choices
- Navigation items

### Tooltips

Hover-triggered hints showing:

- Feature explanations
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Additional context

### Context Menus

Right-click or action menus showing:

- Available actions
- Quick options
- Contextual choices

## Popover Behavior

Popovers:

- Appear on click or hover
- Close when clicking elsewhere
- Position near trigger element
- May contain interactive elements

## Tips

- Hover for tooltips
- Click for menus
- Click outside to close
- Look for action icons
