The user menu provides quick access to account actions and support options.

## Accessing the Menu

1. Look at the top-right corner of the workspace
2. Click your avatar or profile icon
3. The user menu dropdown appears

## Menu Options

### Support

Opens support options:
- Help documentation
- Contact support
- Submit a ticket

### Feedback

Opens the feedback form to:
- Report bugs
- Suggest features
- Share ideas

### Community

Links to the Lindo.ai Slack community where you can:
- Connect with other users
- Get tips and advice
- Share your work

### Hire an Expert

Opens the expert hiring form to:
- Get professional help
- Find certified partners
- Request custom work

### Sign Out

Logs you out of your account.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|--------------|
| Support | Get help |
| Feedback | Share ideas |
| Community | Join Slack |
| Hire Expert | Get pro help |
| Sign Out | Log out |

## Tips

- Use feedback for feature requests
- Join community for tips
- Sign out on shared computers
