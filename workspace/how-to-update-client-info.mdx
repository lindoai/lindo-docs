---
title: "How to Update Client Info"
description: "Editing name, email, company, notes"
---

Edit client information in Workspace CRM.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace CRM">
    Click **CRM** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Client">
    Click the client in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Info Tab">
    Ensure you're on the Info tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Fields">
    Update any field:
    - Name
    - Email
    - Company
    - Notes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Update">
    Save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Editable Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Name | Client's display name |
| Email | Contact email |
| Company | Business name |
| Notes | Internal notes (client doesn't see) |

## Notes Field

Use notes for:
- Project details
- Communication history
- Special requirements
- Internal reminders

## Tips

- Keep info current
- Use notes liberally
- Update after meetings
- Track important details
