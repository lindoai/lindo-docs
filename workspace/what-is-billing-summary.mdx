The Billing Summary provides a quick overview of a client's financial status.

## What It Shows

The billing summary displays:

- **Total Billed**: Sum of all invoices sent
- **Outstanding**: Unpaid invoice amounts
- **Paid**: Completed payments

## Where to Find It

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Select a client
3. Go to **Billing** tab
4. View summary at top

## Understanding the Numbers

### Total Billed

All invoices ever sent to this client, regardless of status.

### Outstanding

Invoices that are still open and awaiting payment.

### Paid

Successfully completed payments.

## Using the Summary

The summary helps you:

- Quickly assess client status
- Identify payment issues
- Track revenue per client
- Make business decisions

## Tips

- Monitor outstanding amounts
- Follow up on overdue payments
- Track high-value clients
- Review before sending new invoices
