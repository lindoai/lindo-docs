title: "What are Add-ons"
description: "Additional features like Embed Webapp"
---

Add-ons are additional features you can purchase separately.

## Available Add-ons

### Embed Webapp

Embed your workspace in external websites:
- iframe integration
- Seamless experience
- Custom branding

### Additional Credits

Purchase extra AI credits:
- One-time purchase
- Added to balance
- No expiration

### Additional Websites

Increase website limit:
- Per-website pricing
- Flexible scaling

## How Add-ons Work

1. Go to Settings → Plans & Billing
2. Find Add-ons section
3. Select add-on
4. Complete purchase
5. Feature activated

## Add-on vs Plan Upgrade

| Add-on | Plan Upgrade |
|--------|--------------|
| Single feature | All features |
| One-time or monthly | Monthly subscription |
| Specific need | General upgrade |

## When to Use Add-ons

- Need one specific feature
- Don't need full plan upgrade
- Temporary increase
- Testing features

## Tips

- Compare add-on vs upgrade cost
- Consider long-term needs
- Add-ons can be removed
