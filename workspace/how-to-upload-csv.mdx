---
title: "How to Upload CSV for Batch Import"
description: "Importing multiple workflows"
---

Create multiple workflows from a CSV file.

## Prerequisites

- CSV file prepared
- Following correct format
- Workflows modal open

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workflows">
    Click **Workflows** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Upload Button">
    Look for "Import CSV" or upload button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your File">
    Choose your prepared CSV file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Import">
    Preview the workflows to be created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Import">
    Click to start all workflows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Progress">
    Watch workflows process in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Test with small batch first
- Verify CSV format
- Enable notifications
- Monitor for errors

## Common Issues

- Wrong format: Check columns
- Empty rows: Remove blank lines
- Special characters: Use UTF-8 encoding
