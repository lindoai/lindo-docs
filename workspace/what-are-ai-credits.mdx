---

AI credits are the currency for AI-powered features in Lindo.ai.

## How They Work

- Each AI action costs credits
- Credits refresh monthly
- Can purchase additional credits
- Different actions cost different amounts

## Credit Costs

There's no fixed per-agent price. Credits are consumed based on how much work
the agent does and how complex the task is—small edits use far fewer credits
than generating new sections, full pages, or an entire website.

## Monthly Allocation

Credits by plan:
- Pro: 500 credits
- Business: 1,500 credits
- Elite: 3,000 credits
- Reseller: 300,000 credits per year

## Credit Reset

- Credits reset monthly
- Based on billing date
- Unused credits don't roll over

## Checking Balance

View credits in:
- Toolbar (quick view)
- Settings → Credits (detailed)

## Running Low

If credits run low:
- Purchase additional credits
- Wait for monthly reset
- Upgrade plan for more

## Tips

- Monitor usage
- Plan large projects
- Purchase extra if needed
- Upgrade for more credits
