---
title: "How to Enable Client Registration"
description: "Allow clients to self-register for your workspace"
---

Enable client registration so potential clients can sign up for your workspace without manual invitation.

## What is Client Registration?

When enabled, clients can:

- Visit your workspace URL
- Create their own account
- Access their dashboard immediately

## Enabling Registration

1. Open **Settings** in sidebar
2. Go to **Workspace** tab
3. Find **Client Register** section
4. Toggle **Enable** to on
5. Click **Save**

## How It Works

1. Client visits your workspace URL
2. They see a registration form
3. They enter their details
4. Account is created automatically
5. They can start using the platform

## Client Defaults

New self-registered clients get:

- Default website limit (configurable)
- Default credit allocation (configurable)
- Standard client permissions

## Managing Registered Clients

Self-registered clients appear in your CRM like any other client. You can:

- Update their limits
- Allocate additional credits
- View their websites
- Send invoices

## Tips

- Set appropriate default limits
- Monitor new registrations
- Follow up with new clients
- Consider onboarding workflow
