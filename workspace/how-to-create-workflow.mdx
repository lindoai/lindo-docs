Create a website in the background using a workflow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workflows">
    Click **Workflows** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Workflow">
    Click the **Create Workflow** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Prompt">
    Describe the website you want to create:

    ```
    Modern landing page for a fitness app called FitTrack.
    Hero with app screenshot, features section, pricing,
    testimonials, and download CTA. Dark mode with green accent.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add AI Tools (Optional)">
    Configure additional settings:
    - **Language** - Content language
    - **Mode** - Dark or Light
    - **Color Palette** - Brand colors
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Notifications (Optional)">
    Toggle **Email Notifications** to receive an email when the workflow completes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule or Start">
    **Start immediately** — Click **Start** or **Create** to begin processing now.

    **Schedule for later** — Click the **Schedule** date picker, select a future date and time, then click **Schedule**. The workflow will run automatically at the scheduled time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Progress">
    The workflow appears in your list with "Running" or "Scheduled" status. It updates to "Completed" when done.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## When to Use Workflows

**Use workflows when:**
- You want to continue working on other things
- Creating multiple websites
- Running batch operations

**Use direct generation when:**
- You want to see results immediately
- Making quick iterations
- Single website creation

## Workflow Duration

Typical times:
- Simple landing page: 1-2 minutes
- Multi-page website: 2-5 minutes
- Complex site: 5-10 minutes

## After Completion

When a workflow completes:
1. Status changes to "Completed"
2. Email notification (if enabled)
3. Website appears in your Websites Grid
4. Click to open and edit

## Tips

- **Write clear prompts** - Better prompts = better results
- **Enable notifications** - Don't miss completions
- **Check for errors** - Review failed workflows
