title: "Understanding Modals"
description: "Full-screen and popup modals in Lindo.ai"
---

Modals are overlay windows that appear for specific tasks and settings.

## Types of Modals

### Full-Screen Modals

Large modals that cover most of the screen:

- Settings modal
- CRM modal
- Analytics modal
- Workflows modal

### Popup Modals

Smaller modals for quick actions:

- Confirmation dialogs
- Quick forms
- Alerts

## Modal Behavior

Modals:

- Overlay the main content
- Focus on specific task
- Close with X button or Escape
- May have multiple tabs

## Navigating Modals

- Use tabs to switch sections
- Scroll for more content
- Click outside to close (some modals)
- Press Escape to close

## Tips

- Complete actions before closing
- Save changes when prompted
- Use keyboard shortcuts
- Check all tabs for options
