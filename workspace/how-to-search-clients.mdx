---
title: "How to Search Clients"
description: "Filtering client list"
---

Find specific clients quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace CRM">
    Click **CRM** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Search Input">
    Locate the search field above the client list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Your Search">
    Enter client name or email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Results">
    List filters to matching clients.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Search Tips

- Search by name
- Search by email
- Partial matches work
- Case insensitive

## Examples

| Search | Finds |
|--------|-------|
| "john" | John Smith, Johnny Doe |
| "@gmail" | All Gmail clients |
| "acme" | Acme Corp clients |

## Clearing Search

- Delete search text
- Or click clear button
- Full list returns

## Tips

- Use specific terms
- Search by company
- Clear to see all
