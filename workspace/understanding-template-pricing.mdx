---
title: "Understanding Template Pricing"
description: "FREE templates vs premium templates"
---

Templates in Lindo.ai come in different pricing tiers.

## FREE Templates

Most templates are free:

- No additional cost
- Available to all plans
- Full functionality
- Unlimited use

## Premium Templates

Some templates may be premium:

- Higher quality designs
- More complex layouts
- Special features
- May require specific plan

## Identifying Template Type

In the templates panel:

- FREE templates show "FREE" badge
- Premium templates show price or plan requirement

## Using Templates

1. Browse available templates
2. Check pricing/requirements
3. Preview before using
4. Click to start with template

## Tips

- Start with FREE templates
- Premium templates worth it for specific needs
- All templates customizable
- Templates save time
