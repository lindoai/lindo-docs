---
title: "How to Set Website Limit"
description: "Configuring max websites for client"
---

Control how many websites a client can create.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace CRM">
    Click **CRM** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Client">
    Click the client in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Info Tab">
    Ensure you're on the Info tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Website Limit">
    Locate the website limit field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the Limit">
    Enter the maximum number of websites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Update">
    Save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Limit Options

- **0** - No websites allowed
- **1-10** - Limited access
- **Unlimited** - No restrictions

## Use Cases

| Limit | Use Case |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | Single project client |
| 5 | Small business client |
| 10 | Agency client |
| Unlimited | Premium client |

## Tips

- Match to client's plan
- Increase as needed
- Use for upselling
- Track usage
