title: "How to Setup White Label Domain"
description: "Complete domain setup wizard walkthrough"
---

Connect your own domain to your Lindo.ai workspace.

## Prerequisites

- Elite or Reseller plan
- Access to your domain's DNS settings
- A domain you own

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Domain Settings">
    1. Go to Settings → White Label
    2. Click the **Domain** tab
    3. Click **Setup Domain**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Domain">
    Enter your main domain (e.g., `youragency.com`).

    Click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Subdomains">
    Set up three subdomains:

    | Subdomain | Purpose | Example |
    |-----------|---------|---------|
    | WebApp | Your workspace | app.youragency.com |
    | Website | Client websites | sites.youragency.com |
    | Email | Transactional emails | mail.youragency.com |

    Default suggestions work for most cases. Click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add DNS Records">
    You'll see a list of DNS records to add. Go to your domain registrar:

    **Required Records:**

    | Type | Name | Value |
    |------|------|-------|
    | CNAME | app | cname.lindo.ai |
    | CNAME | sites | cname.lindo.ai |
    | TXT | _lindo | (verification code shown) |

    **How to add:**
    1. Log in to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap, etc.)
    2. Find DNS settings
    3. Add each record with the exact values shown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    1. Return to Lindo.ai
    2. Click **Check Status** for each record
    3. Wait for green checkmarks (5-30 minutes)
    4. Once all verified, click **Complete Setup**
  </Step>
</Steps>
## DNS Propagation

DNS changes take time to propagate:
- Initial: 5-30 minutes
- Full global: Up to 48 hours

Be patient if status doesn't update immediately.

## Common Issues

**Record not connecting:**
- Double-check values match exactly
- Remove trailing dots if present
- Check for conflicting A/AAAA records

**Cloudflare users:**
- Disable proxy (orange cloud → gray cloud)
- Use DNS-only mode for verification

## After Setup

Once connected:
- Share `app.youragency.com` with clients
- Client websites publish to `sites.youragency.com`
- Emails come from your domain
