Lindo.ai offers four workspace plans, each designed for different needs and scales.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Pro | Business | Elite | Reseller |
|---------|-----|----------|-------|----------|
| Price | $30/mo | $150/mo | $300/mo | $2,000/mo |
| Active Websites | 1 | 10 | 20 | 200 |
| AI Credits | 500/mo | 1,500/mo | 3,000/mo | 25,000/mo |
| Team Members | Unlimited | 3 workspace | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| White Label | - | - | ✓ | ✓ |
| Custom Domain | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Client CRM | - | - | ✓ | ✓ |
| API Access | - | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

## Pro Plan — $30/month

Best for business owners.

**Includes:**
- 1 active website
- 500 AI credits/month
- Custom domain support
- Unlimited team members
- No Lindo branding

**Best for:**
- Business owners
- Personal websites
- Small portfolios

## Business Plan — $150/month

Best for freelancers.

**Includes:**
- 10 active websites
- 1,500 AI credits/month
- 3 workspace team members
- API access
- Custom domains

**Best for:**
- Freelancers
- Small teams
- Multiple client projects

## Elite Plan — $300/month

Best for agencies.

**Includes:**
- 20 active websites
- 3,000 AI credits/month
- Unlimited team members
- White label branding
- Client CRM
- API access

**Best for:**
- Agencies
- High-volume production
- White label needs

## Reseller Plan — $2,000/month (billed yearly)

Best for white label resellers and SaaS builders.

**Includes:**
- 200 active websites
- 300,000 AI credits/year (25,000/mo)
- Unlimited team members
- Full white label
- Reseller website
- Embed workspace

**Best for:**
- SaaS builders
- Platform resellers
- Large-scale operations

## Choosing a Plan

| If you need... | Choose |
|----------------|--------|
| Personal website | Pro |
| Client work | Business |
| Agency scale | Elite |
| White label SaaS | Reseller |

## Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings** → **Plans & Billing**
2. Compare plans
3. Click **Upgrade** on desired plan
4. Complete payment
