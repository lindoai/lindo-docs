---
title: "How to Use a Template"
description: "Starting a new website from a template"
---

Create a website by starting from a pre-built template.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Template">
    Browse the Templates Panel on the right side of your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview (Optional)">
    Hover over the template to see a larger preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Template">
    Click the template card you want to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Template Loads">
    The template opens in the Website Editor with all its content and design.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Use AI Chat to make it yours:

    **Change business name:**
    ```
    Update all instances of the business name to "Your Company"
    ```

    **Update content:**
    ```
    Rewrite the hero section for a dental clinic in Chicago
    ```

    **Change colors:**
    ```
    Change the color scheme to match my brand (blue and white)
    ```

    **Add sections:**
    ```
    Add a testimonials section after the features
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    When ready, click **Publish** to make your site live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You Get

When you use a template:
- Complete page structure
- Pre-designed sections
- Placeholder content
- Styling and animations
- Responsive design

## Customization Ideas

- Replace placeholder text with your content
- Swap images for your own
- Adjust colors to match your brand
- Add or remove sections
- Change fonts in Theme settings

## Tips

- **Don't keep placeholder content** - Replace everything
- **Maintain design consistency** - Keep the template's style
- **Use AI for bulk changes** - Faster than manual editing
- **Save as your own template** - After customizing
