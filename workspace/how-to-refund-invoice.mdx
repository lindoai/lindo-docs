---
title: "How to Refund an Invoice"
description: "Processing refund for client"
---

Return payment to a client.

## Prerequisites

- Invoice must be paid
- Stripe connected
- Funds available

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Invoice">
    In client's Billing tab, locate the paid invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Refund">
    Click the refund button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge and confirm the refund.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refund Processed">
    - Client receives refund
    - Invoice status changes to "Refunded"
    - Funds returned via Stripe
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Refund Timeline

- Processing: Immediate
- Client receives: 5-10 business days
- Depends on client's bank

## Important Notes

- Cannot be undone
- Full refund only
- Stripe fees may not be refunded
- Keep records

## Tips

- Communicate with client
- Document reason
- Process promptly
- Verify before confirming
