Switch between websites in your workspace to manage different projects.

## Switching Websites

From the workspace:

1. View your websites grid
2. Click on any website card
3. Website opens in editor

From the editor:

1. Click the website dropdown in header
2. Select "Back to Workspace"
3. Choose a different website

## Tips

- Use search to find websites quickly
- Recent websites may be highlighted
- Each website opens in the editor
- Changes save automatically
