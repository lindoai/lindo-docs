---
title: "How to Analyze Traffic Sources"
description: "Understand where your visitors come from"
---

Analyze traffic sources to understand how visitors find your websites.

## Accessing Traffic Sources

1. Open **Analytics** from sidebar
2. Scroll to **Referrers** section
3. View traffic source breakdown

## Traffic Source Types

### Direct

- Typed URL
- Bookmarks
- No referrer

### Search

- Google
- Bing
- Other search engines

### Social

- Facebook
- Twitter
- LinkedIn
- Other social platforms

### Referral

- Links from other websites
- Partner sites
- Directories

## Using This Data

Traffic source analysis helps you:

- Identify best channels
- Optimize marketing spend
- Focus promotion efforts
- Track campaign success

## Tips

- Invest in top-performing sources
- Improve underperforming channels
- Track UTM campaigns
- Monitor changes over time
