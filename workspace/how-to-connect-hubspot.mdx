Add HubSpot chat for CRM-integrated support.

## What is HubSpot Chat

HubSpot Chat provides:
- Live chat widget
- CRM integration
- Contact tracking
- Chatbot flows

## Prerequisites

- HubSpot account
- HubSpot tracking code ID

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Tracking Code">
    1. Log into app.hubspot.com
    2. Go to Settings → Tracking Code
    3. Copy your Hub ID
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to Settings → White Label → Integrations tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find HubSpot">
    Locate HubSpot Chat card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Hub ID">
    Paste your HubSpot Hub ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click Save or Connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You Get

After connecting:
- Chat widget appears
- Contacts sync to CRM
- Chat history in HubSpot
- Lead capture

## Where It Appears

HubSpot shows on:
- Workspace login page
- Client dashboard
- Workspace pages
