Add a favicon that appears in browser tabs.

## What is a Favicon

A favicon is the small icon that appears:
- In browser tabs
- In bookmarks
- In browser history
- On mobile home screens

## Requirements

- Size: 32x32 pixels
- Format: PNG or ICO
- Square shape
- Simple design

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to White Label">
    Select **White Label** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Appearance Tab">
    Navigate to the Appearance tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Favicon Upload">
    Locate the favicon upload area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upload">
    Click the upload button or drag and drop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select File">
    Choose your favicon file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** or **Update**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Design Tips

- Keep it simple
- Use your logo mark
- Ensure visibility at small size
- Test on different backgrounds

## Good Favicon Examples

- Single letter
- Simple icon
- Logo mark
- Recognizable symbol

## Bad Favicon Examples

- Full logo with text
- Complex images
- Too much detail
- Hard to see colors

## Testing

After upload:
- Refresh the page
- Check browser tab
- Test in different browsers
