description: "Accessing the CRM modal for client management"
---

The Workspace CRM is where you manage your agency clients, their credits, and billing.

## Opening the CRM

1. Look for **CRM** in the sidebar navigation
2. Click to open the CRM modal
3. The modal displays your client list

## CRM Tabs

### Clients Tab
View and manage all your clients. Search, add new clients, and access client details.

### Billing Tab
View all invoices across all clients. Filter by status (open, paid, refunded).

## What You Can Do

- Add new clients
- View client details and websites
- Allocate credits to clients
- Create and send invoices
- Manage subscriptions
- Track billing history

## Requirements

The CRM is available on Elite and Reseller plans. Pro and Business plan users don't have access to client management features.
