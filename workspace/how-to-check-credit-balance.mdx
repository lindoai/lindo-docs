Monitor your AI credits to manage usage.

## Quick Check

Your credit balance is displayed in the toolbar:
- Look below the prompt input
- Credits shown as a number
- Updates after each AI action

## Detailed View

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Credits Tab">
    Click the **Credits** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Details">
    See your credit information:
    - Current balance
    - Monthly allocation
    - Usage this month
    - Reset date
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Credit Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Available | Credits you can use now |
| Monthly | Credits you get each month |
| Used | Credits consumed this period |
| Reset Date | When credits refresh |

## Understanding Usage

Credits are consumed by AI agents:
- Website Builder: 10x (10 per page) credits
- Block Creator: 10 credits
- Block Editor: 10 credits
- Element Editor: 10 credits
- Blog Writer: 10 credits

## Low Credit Warning

When credits are low:
- Warning appears in toolbar
- Consider purchasing more
- Or wait for monthly reset

## Purchasing Credits

If you need more:
1. Go to Settings → Credits
2. Click "Purchase Credits"
3. Select amount
4. Complete payment

## Tips

- Check before large projects
- Plan usage around reset dates
- Purchase extra for busy periods
