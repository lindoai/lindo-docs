description: "Resolve credit balance problems"
---

If you're having issues with credits, try these solutions.

## Common Issues

### Credits Not Showing

**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and back in
3. Check Settings → Credits

### Credits Not Deducting

**Solution:**
- Credits deduct after AI operations complete
- Wait for operation to finish
- Check balance after completion

### Unexpected Credit Usage

**Solution:**
1. Review recent AI operations
2. Check agent credit costs
3. Monitor usage patterns

### Purchase Not Reflected

**Solution:**
1. Wait a few minutes
2. Check purchase history
3. Verify payment completed
4. Contact support if needed

## Tips

- Monitor credit usage
- Plan operations based on balance
- Purchase before running low
- Contact support for discrepancies
