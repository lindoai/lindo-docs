If Stripe verification is pending or failed, follow these steps.

## Common Issues

### Missing Information

**Solution:**
1. Log into Stripe dashboard
2. Check for pending requirements
3. Provide requested information
4. Submit for review

### Document Issues

**Solution:**
1. Ensure documents are clear
2. Use accepted formats
3. Resubmit if rejected
4. Contact Stripe support

### Business Information

**Solution:**
1. Verify business details are accurate
2. Update any incorrect information
3. Ensure consistency across documents

## Checking Status

1. Open Settings → Integrations
2. View Stripe connection status
3. Click to open Stripe dashboard

## Tips

- Respond promptly to requests
- Use accurate information
- Keep documents ready
- Contact Stripe for specific issues
