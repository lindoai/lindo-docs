Check your credit balance to know how many AI credits you have available.

## Checking Credits

### In Workspace

1. Look at the toolbar area
2. Credit balance is displayed
3. Shows current available credits

### In Settings

1. Open **Settings** from sidebar
2. Go to **Credits** tab
3. View detailed credit information

## Credit Information

You can see:

- Current balance
- Monthly allocation
- Usage this month
- Purchase history

## Understanding Credits

- Credits are used for AI operations
- Different agents cost different amounts
- Credits reset monthly (for subscription)
- Can purchase additional credits

## Tips

- Check before large operations
- Monitor usage patterns
- Purchase more if needed
- Plan batch operations
