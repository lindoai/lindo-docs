Track the status of your background workflows.

## Statistics Overview

At the top of the Workflows modal:
- **Total** - All workflows created
- **Running** - Currently processing
- **Completed** - Successfully finished
- **Errored** - Failed workflows

## Understanding Each Stat

### Total

All workflows ever created in your workspace.

### Running

Workflows currently being processed:
- Website generation in progress
- May take several minutes
- Check back for completion

### Completed

Successfully finished workflows:
- Website created
- Ready to use
- No issues

### Errored

Workflows that failed:
- Check error message
- May need to retry
- Contact support if persistent

## Tips

- Monitor running workflows
- Check errored for issues
- Completed means ready
- Refresh to update stats
