---
title: "How to Revoke Access"
description: "Removing team member access"
---

Remove a team member's access to your workspace.

## When to Revoke

- Team member leaves
- Project completed
- Security concerns
- Role no longer needed

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Members Settings">
    Go to Settings → Members tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Member">
    Locate the team member in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Remove">
    Click the remove or revoke button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge and confirm removal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Happens

When you revoke access:
- Immediate effect
- Member can't log in
- No access to workspace
- Their work remains

## Important Notes

- Cannot revoke owner
- Action is immediate
- Can re-invite later
- Work is preserved

## Tips

- Communicate before revoking
- Review access regularly
- Document handoff if needed
- Can re-invite if needed
