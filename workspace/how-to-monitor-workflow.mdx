---
title: "How to Monitor Workflow Progress"
description: "Checking workflow status"
---

Track the progress of your background workflows.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workflows">
    Click **Workflows** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Statistics">
    Check the stats at top:
    - Running count
    - Completed count
    - Error count
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Your Workflow">
    Locate your workflow in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Status">
    See the current status:
    - Running (in progress)
    - Completed (done)
    - Error (failed)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh">
    Click refresh to update the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Status Indicators

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Running | Processing |
| Completed | Success |
| Error | Failed |

## Tips

- Refresh periodically
- Enable email notifications
- Check errors promptly
- Be patient with running
