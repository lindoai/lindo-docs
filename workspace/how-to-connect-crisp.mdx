Add Crisp live chat to your workspace.

## What is Crisp

Crisp provides:
- Live chat widget
- Customer support
- Chat history
- Team inbox

## Prerequisites

- Crisp account
- Crisp Website ID

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Website ID">
    1. Log into crisp.chat
    2. Go to Settings → Website Settings
    3. Copy your Website ID
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to Settings → White Label → Integrations tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Crisp">
    Locate Crisp card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Website ID">
    Paste your Crisp Website ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click Save or Connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You Get

After connecting:
- Chat widget appears
- Clients can message you
- Real-time support
- Chat history saved

## Where It Appears

Crisp shows on:
- Workspace login page
- Client dashboard
- Workspace pages

## Tips

- Set up auto-responses
- Configure business hours
- Use canned responses
- Monitor chat queue
