---
title: "How to Copy Invoice Link"
description: "Get the payment link for a client invoice"
---

Copy the invoice payment link to share with clients for easy payment.

## Copying Invoice Link

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Select the client
3. Go to **Billing** tab
4. Find the invoice
5. Click the **Copy Link** icon
6. Link is copied to clipboard

## Using the Link

Share the link with clients via:

- Email
- Chat
- SMS
- Any messaging platform

## What Clients See

When clients click the link:

- Invoice details display
- Payment form appears
- They can pay with card
- Confirmation on success

## Tips

- Send link promptly
- Include in follow-up emails
- Track if link was used
- Resend if needed
