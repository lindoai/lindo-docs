Enter your domain as the first step in setting up white label.

## Entering Your Domain

1. Open **Settings** from sidebar
2. Go to **Domain** tab
3. Click **Setup Domain**
4. Enter your main domain

## Domain Format

Enter your domain without protocol:

```
yourdomain.com
```

Not:
```
https://yourdomain.com
www.yourdomain.com
```

## Requirements

Your domain should be:

- Registered and active
- Under your control
- Not already in use elsewhere
