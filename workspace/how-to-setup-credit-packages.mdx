---
title: "How to Set Up Credit Packages"
description: "Step-by-step guide to creating one-time credit packages for your clients"
---

Set up credit packages so your clients can purchase additional AI credits as one-time payments.

## Prerequisites

- Reseller (Whitelabel) plan active
- Stripe connected in **Settings → Integrations**

## Steps

### 1. Open Tiers Settings

Navigate to **Settings → Tiers** tab. The credit packages section is below the tiers section.

### 2. Add a Package

Click the **Add Package** button. A new row appears with fields for:

- **Name** — Display name (e.g., "Starter Pack", "Pro Pack")
- **Credits** — Number of AI credits in the package
- **Price** — One-time purchase price
- **Currency** — USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, or CAD

### 3. Configure Multiple Packages

Add up to 4 packages. They'll be displayed as cards in the client's purchase modal.

### 4. Save

Click **Save Packages**. The packages are now available for your clients.

### 5. Client Experience

When a client opens their **Plans & Billing** modal:
1. They see a **Buy Credits** button
2. Clicking it opens a modal with available packages
3. They select a package and complete payment via Stripe Checkout
4. Purchased credits are immediately added to their balance

## Purchased vs Monthly Credits

- **Monthly credits** come from tier subscriptions and reset each billing cycle
- **Purchased credits** come from credit packages and don't expire
- Both count toward the client's total available balance
