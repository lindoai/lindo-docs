---
title: "What is Allocation History"
description: "Track credit allocations made to clients"
---

Allocation History shows a record of all credit allocations made to a client over time.

## What It Shows

The allocation history displays:

- Date of allocation
- Amount allocated
- Type (monthly or one-time)
- Running balance

## Accessing History

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Select a client
3. Go to **Credits** tab
4. View allocation history section

## Types of Allocations

### Monthly Allocations

- Recurring credits each month
- Reset on billing cycle
- Shown as "Monthly" type

### One-Time Allocations

- Single credit additions
- Don't reset
- Shown as "One-time" type

## Using the History

The history helps you:

- Track credit distribution
- Audit allocations
- Understand usage patterns
- Plan future allocations

## Tips

- Review history periodically
- Note patterns in usage
- Adjust allocations based on history
- Document reasons for one-time allocations
