description: "Accessing the analytics modal"
---

View aggregated analytics for all your websites.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Analytics in Sidebar">
    Look for the **Analytics** item in the left sidebar (chart icon).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Analytics">
    Click to open the Analytics modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Dashboard">
    The analytics dashboard displays with:
    - Statistics cards at top
    - Traffic chart
    - Breakdown sections
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Default View

When you open analytics:
- Date range: Last 7 days (default)
- All websites included
- All metrics visible

## Navigating the Dashboard

### Scroll to See All Data

The modal contains multiple sections. Scroll down to see:
1. Statistics cards
2. Traffic chart
3. Top websites
4. Operating systems
5. Browsers
6. Devices
7. Countries
8. Referrers
9. UTM campaigns

### Change Date Range

Click the date picker to adjust the time period.

### Refresh Data

Click the refresh button to update with latest data.

## Quick Insights

Look for these key metrics:
- **Visitors trending up?** - Growth indicator
- **Top website** - Your best performer
- **Mobile vs Desktop** - Audience device preference
- **Top referrer** - Best traffic source
