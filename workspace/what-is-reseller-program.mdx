The Reseller Program lets you white label and resell Lindo.ai as your own platform.

## What It Is

As a reseller, you can:
- Brand the platform as yours
- Set your own pricing
- Manage unlimited clients
- Keep the revenue

## Reseller Features

### Full White Label

- Your logo and branding
- Custom domain
- No Lindo.ai mentions
- Complete customization

### Unlimited Scale

- Unlimited websites
- Unlimited clients
- Unlimited team members
- No caps

### Reseller Website

- Your own landing page
- Showcase your platform
- Capture leads
- Professional presence

### Workspace Embed

- Embed in your website
- Seamless integration
- iframe support
- Custom experience

## Who It's For

- SaaS entrepreneurs
- Large agencies
- Platform builders
- White label resellers

## Pricing Model

You control pricing:
- Set your own rates
- Keep the difference
- Flexible billing
- Your payment processor

## Getting Started

1. Upgrade to Reseller plan
2. Configure white label
3. Set up your domain
4. Create reseller website
5. Start selling

## Tips

- Build your brand
- Create support resources
- Set competitive pricing
- Market your platform
