Workspace CRM is the client management system for agencies and resellers who manage multiple clients.

## Purpose

If you run a web design agency or resell Lindo.ai services, Workspace CRM helps you:
- Organize client information
- Track client websites
- Manage credit allocation
- Handle billing and invoices

## Availability

Workspace CRM is available on:
- Elite plan ($300/mo)
- Reseller plan ($2,000/mo)

## Accessing Workspace CRM

1. Click **CRM** in the sidebar
2. The CRM modal opens

## CRM Structure

### Clients Tab

Left panel shows your client list:
- Client avatar (initials)
- Client email
- Search input to filter

Right panel shows selected client details.

### Billing Tab

View all invoices across all clients:
- Invoice list on left
- Invoice details on right

## Client Details

When you select a client, you see five tabs:

### Info Tab

- Name, email, company
- Notes (internal comments)
- Website limit setting
- Agentic Mode toggle
- Delete and Update buttons

### Websites Tab

- All websites owned by this client
- Quick access to edit each site

### Credits Tab

- Monthly credit allocation
- Usage progress bar
- Reset date
- Allocation history
- Add/update credits buttons

### Billing Tab

- Billing summary (total, outstanding, paid)
- Create Invoice button
- Invoice history table

### Subscriptions Tab

- Active subscriptions
- Create Subscription button
- Subscription management

## Workspace CRM vs Website CRM

| Workspace CRM | Website CRM |
|---------------|-------------|
| Manage YOUR clients | Manage WEBSITE customers |
| Agency clients you bill | Visitors who buy products |
| In workspace sidebar | In website editor |
| For agencies/resellers | For e-commerce sites |
