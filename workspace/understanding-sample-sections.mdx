Sample sections are pre-defined page components you can add to your prompt.

## What Are Sample Sections

Sample sections represent common website components:
- Hero sections
- Feature grids
- About sections
- Testimonials
- CTAs
- And more

## Available Sections

### Hero
The main banner at the top of a page:
- Headline
- Subheadline
- CTA button
- Background image

### Features
Showcase product or service features:
- Feature grid
- Icon cards
- Benefit lists

### About
Tell your story:
- Company history
- Team introduction
- Mission statement

### Testimonials
Social proof from customers:
- Quote cards
- Star ratings
- Customer photos

### Pricing
Display pricing options:
- Pricing tables
- Plan comparison
- Feature lists

### FAQ
Answer common questions:
- Accordion style
- Question/answer pairs

### Contact
Get in touch:
- Contact form
- Map
- Contact details

### CTA (Call to Action)
Drive conversions:
- Action buttons
- Sign-up forms
- Download prompts

### Footer
Page footer:
- Navigation links
- Social icons
- Copyright

## Using Sample Sections

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Prompt Builder">
    Click the expand button on the prompt input.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Sections Tab">
    Navigate to the sections area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Sections">
    Click sections to add them to your prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange Order">
    Sections are added in the order you select them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Create your website with selected sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Start with Hero
- End with CTA and Footer
- Mix sections for variety
- Don't overload—5-7 sections is ideal
