---
title: "How to View Purchase History"
description: "Checking past credit purchases"
---

See all your credit purchases.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Credits Tab">
    Select the **Credits** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Purchase History">
    Scroll to the purchase history section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Transactions">
    See all past purchases with:
    - Date
    - Amount
    - Credits
    - Status
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Transaction Details

Each entry shows:
- Purchase date and time
- Number of credits
- Amount paid
- Payment status

## Uses

- Track spending
- Expense reporting
- Budget planning
- Receipt reference

## Tips

- Review monthly
- Export for accounting
- Track patterns
