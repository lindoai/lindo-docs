Templates are organized into categories for easy browsing.

## Available Categories

- **All**: View all templates
- **Agency Consulting**: Business service templates
- **Automotive**: Car dealership, repair shops
- **Beauty & Wellness**: Spas, salons, fitness
- **E-commerce**: Online stores
- **Education**: Schools, courses
- **Food & Beverage**: Restaurants, cafes
- **Healthcare**: Medical, dental
- **Real Estate**: Property listings
- **Technology**: SaaS, apps
- **And more...**

## Browsing by Category

1. Open templates panel
2. Click category dropdown
3. Select a category
4. Browse filtered templates

## Tips

- Start with your industry
- Check "All" for more options
- Preview before selecting
- Categories help narrow choices
