Sample prompts are pre-written templates that help you get started quickly.

## What Are Sample Prompts

Sample prompts are professionally crafted descriptions for common website types. They include:
- Business description
- Target audience
- Key sections
- Style preferences

## Available Categories

### Agency Consulting
Prompts for service-based businesses:
- Marketing agencies
- Consulting firms
- Professional services

### SaaS
Prompts for software products:
- App landing pages
- Product showcases
- Feature highlights

### Restaurant
Prompts for food service:
- Restaurant websites
- Cafe pages
- Menu displays

### Real Estate
Prompts for property businesses:
- Listing pages
- Agent portfolios
- Property showcases

### Fitness
Prompts for health businesses:
- Gym websites
- Personal trainers
- Wellness studios

### Portfolio
Prompts for creative professionals:
- Photographers
- Designers
- Artists

## Using Sample Prompts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Prompt Builder">
    Click the expand button on the prompt input.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Sample Prompts">
    Scroll through available categories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Prompt">
    Click a sample prompt to use it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit the prompt to match your specific needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click send to create your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Customizing Sample Prompts

Sample prompts are starting points. Customize by:
- Changing business name
- Updating services
- Adjusting style preferences
- Adding specific sections

## Tips

- Use samples as inspiration
- Always customize for your brand
- Combine with other tools (colors, mode)
