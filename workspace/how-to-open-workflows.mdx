---
title: "How to Open Workflows"
description: "Accessing the workflows modal"
---

Access the Workflows panel to manage background website creation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Workflows in Sidebar">
    Look for **Workflows** in the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Workflows">
    Click to open the Workflows modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Workflows">
    See all your workflows:
    - Statistics at top
    - Workflow list below
    - Create new button
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You See

- **Statistics** - Total, running, completed, errored
- **Workflow List** - All workflows with status
- **Actions** - Create, refresh, batch import

## Tips

- Check regularly for progress
- Use refresh to update
- Monitor for errors
