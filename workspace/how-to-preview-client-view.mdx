---
title: "How to Preview Client View"
description: "Seeing workspace as clients see it"
---

See your workspace exactly as clients see it.

## Why Preview

Preview helps you:
- Verify branding looks correct
- Check client experience
- Test before launching
- Ensure quality

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to White Label">
    Select **White Label** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Preview Button">
    Look for "Preview Client View" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Preview">
    Opens a new tab showing client view.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You See

The preview shows:
- Login page with your branding
- Your logo and favicon
- Custom background
- Client dashboard experience

## What to Check

### Login Page

- Logo displays correctly
- Background looks good
- Form is readable
- Colors match brand

### Dashboard

- Navigation works
- Features accessible
- Branding consistent

## Tips

- Preview after each change
- Test on different devices
- Check mobile view
- Verify all branding elements
