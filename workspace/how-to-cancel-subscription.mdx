---
title: "How to Cancel a Subscription"
description: "Stopping recurring billing"
---

Stop automatic recurring billing for a client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Client Details">
    Go to CRM → Select client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Subscriptions Tab">
    Click the Subscriptions tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Subscription">
    Locate the active subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Click the cancel button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge and confirm cancellation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Happens

When you cancel:
- No more automatic billing
- Current period may complete
- Client notified
- Subscription ends

## Important Notes

- Cannot be undone
- May complete current period
- Communicate with client
- Can create new subscription later

## Tips

- Communicate before cancelling
- Document reason
- Offer alternatives if appropriate
- Keep records
