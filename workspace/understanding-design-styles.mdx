Design styles are presets that influence the visual direction of your generated website.

## Available Styles

### Modern

Clean, contemporary design with:
- Balanced whitespace
- Subtle shadows
- Rounded corners
- Professional typography
- Smooth animations

**Best for:** SaaS, agencies, professional services

### Minimal

Simple, whitespace-focused design with:
- Maximum whitespace
- Limited color palette
- Simple typography
- Few decorative elements
- Focus on content

**Best for:** Portfolios, luxury brands, art galleries

### Brutalist

Bold, raw aesthetic with:
- High contrast
- Unconventional layouts
- Bold typography
- Raw, unpolished feel
- Strong visual impact

**Best for:** Creative agencies, art projects, bold brands

### Glass

Glassmorphism effects with:
- Frosted glass backgrounds
- Blur effects
- Transparency
- Soft shadows
- Layered elements

**Best for:** Tech products, modern apps, futuristic brands

### Playful

Fun, colorful design with:
- Bright colors
- Rounded shapes
- Playful illustrations
- Bouncy animations
- Friendly feel

**Best for:** Kids products, games, casual brands

### Elegant

Sophisticated, refined design with:
- Serif typography
- Muted colors
- Generous spacing
- Subtle details
- Premium feel

**Best for:** Luxury brands, high-end services, fashion

## How to Apply a Style

### In Prompt Builder Modal

1. Click **✨ Prompt Builder**
2. Scroll to Design Styles section
3. Select your preferred style
4. Click **Apply to Prompt**

### In Your Prompt

Mention the style directly:
```
Modern SaaS landing page for...
```
```
Minimal portfolio website for...
```
```
Elegant restaurant website for...
```

## Combining Styles

You can blend styles:
```
Modern design with minimal elements and glass effects
```

The AI interprets and combines the influences.

## Style vs Content

Design style affects:
- Visual appearance
- Layout patterns
- Typography choices
- Animation styles

It doesn't affect:
- Content/copy
- Section structure
- Functionality

## Tips

- **Match your brand** - Choose a style that fits your identity
- **Consider audience** - Playful for casual, Elegant for premium
- **Be consistent** - Stick to one primary style per site
