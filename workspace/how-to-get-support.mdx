When you need help with Lindo.ai, there are several ways to get assistance.

## Accessing Support

1. Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Support** from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be directed to the support resources

## Support Options

### Help Documentation
Browse the comprehensive documentation covering all features and common questions.

### Support Ticket
Submit a support ticket for technical issues or account-related questions. Include:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Screenshots if helpful

### Community
Join the Slack community to connect with other users and get help from the community.

## Response Times

Support tickets are typically answered within 24-48 hours during business days. The community is available 24/7 with responses from other users.
