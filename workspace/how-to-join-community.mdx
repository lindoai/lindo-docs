Connect with other Lindo.ai users in the Slack community.

## Joining the Community

1. Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Community** from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be directed to the Slack community invitation

## Community Benefits

### Get Help
Ask questions and get answers from experienced users and the Lindo.ai team.

### Share Ideas
Discuss feature requests and vote on what matters most.

### Learn Tips
Discover prompts, workflows, and techniques from other users.

### Stay Updated
Get early announcements about new features and updates.

## Community Guidelines

- Be respectful and helpful
- Search before asking (your question may already be answered)
- Share your wins and learnings
- Report bugs through proper support channels
