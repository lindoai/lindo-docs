Workspace CRM helps agencies manage their clients.

## Key Difference

- **Workspace CRM**: For agency clients (your customers)
- **Website CRM**: For website visitors (your clients' customers)

## Features

### Clients Tab

- Client list
- Contact information
- Website limits
- Credit allocations

### Billing Tab

- All invoices across clients
- Payment tracking
- Invoice management

## What You Can Do

- Add and manage clients
- Set website limits
- Allocate credits
- Send invoices
- Create subscriptions

## Tips

- Keep client info updated
- Monitor credit usage
- Track billing status
- Communicate proactively
