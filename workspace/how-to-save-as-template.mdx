---
title: "How to Save Website as Template"
description: "Creating reusable template from website"
---

Turn your website into a reusable template.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Website">
    Locate the website you want to save as a template in the Websites Grid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Actions Menu">
    Hover over the website card to reveal actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Save as Template">
    Click **Save as Template** from the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The website is saved to your templates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Your Template">
    Your template appears in the Templates Panel under your saved templates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Gets Saved

- Page structure
- All sections and content
- Design and styling
- Theme settings
- Layout and animations

## What Doesn't Get Saved

- Custom domain settings
- Analytics data
- Customer data
- Products
- SEO meta data (reset to defaults)

## Use Cases

### Reuse Great Designs

Created something you love?
1. Save it as a template
2. Use it for future projects
3. Maintain consistency

### Client Templates

Build templates for client types:
1. Create a restaurant template
2. Save as template
3. Use for all restaurant clients

### Team Standards

Share design standards:
1. Create a standard layout
2. Save as template
3. Team uses for consistency

### Backup Designs

Preserve your best work:
1. Save as template before changes
2. Always have the original available

## Tips

- **Clean up first** - Remove placeholder content
- **Use generic content** - Makes customization easier
- **Name clearly** - "Restaurant - Modern Dark"
- **Create variations** - Light/dark, different industries

## Managing Templates

Your saved templates appear in the Templates Panel. To use one:
1. Open Templates Panel
2. Find your saved template
3. Click to use it
