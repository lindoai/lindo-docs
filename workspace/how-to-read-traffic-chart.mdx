Understand the traffic chart to analyze your website performance trends.

## Chart Elements

The traffic chart shows:

- **Page Views**: Total page loads (blue line)
- **Visitors**: Unique visitors (green line)
- **Time**: X-axis shows date range

## Reading the Chart

- Higher lines = more traffic
- Upward trends = growth
- Spikes = traffic events
- Dips = lower activity

## Interpreting Data

### Page Views vs Visitors

- If page views >> visitors: Good engagement
- If similar: Visitors viewing few pages
- Ratio indicates engagement level

### Trends

- Consistent growth: Healthy site
- Sudden spikes: Viral content or campaigns
- Drops: Issues or seasonal changes

## Tips

- Compare to previous periods
- Note significant events
- Track campaign impact
- Monitor for anomalies
