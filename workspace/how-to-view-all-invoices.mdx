---
title: "How to View All Invoices"
description: "See all invoices across all clients"
---

View a complete list of all invoices sent to all clients in your workspace.

## Accessing All Invoices

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Click **Billing** tab
3. View the complete invoice list

## Invoice List

The billing tab shows:

- All invoices from all clients
- Invoice number
- Client name
- Amount
- Status
- Date

## Filtering Invoices

You can filter by:

- Status (Open, Paid, Refunded)
- Date range
- Client name

## Invoice Actions

From the list, you can:

- Click to view details
- Copy payment link
- Download as PDF
- Process refunds

## Tips

- Review invoices regularly
- Follow up on unpaid invoices
- Track payment patterns
- Export for accounting
