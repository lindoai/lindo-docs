The Business plan is designed for agencies and growing teams.

## Features

- 20 active websites
- 50 pages per website
- 100,000 monthly visits
- 2,000 AI credits
- 5 team members
- 20 clients in CRM
- White label branding
- API access
- Priority support

## Limits

| Resource | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Active Websites | 20 |
| Pages per Website | 50 |
| Monthly Visits | 100,000 |
| AI Credits | 2,000 |
| Team Members | 5 |
| Clients | 20 |
