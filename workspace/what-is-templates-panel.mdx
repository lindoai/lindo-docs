description: "Overview of the right-side templates panel"
---

The Templates Panel displays pre-built website designs you can use as starting points.

## Location

The panel is on the right side of your workspace dashboard.

## Panel Components

### Category Tabs

Filter templates by industry:
- All
- Agency & Consulting
- Automotive
- Beauty & Wellness
- E-commerce
- Education
- Events
- Finance
- Food & Restaurant
- Healthcare
- Real Estate
- SaaS & Technology
- Travel & Hospitality

### Template Cards

Each card shows:
- **Thumbnail** - Preview image
- **Name** - Template name
- **Badge** - FREE or AI Agent indicator

### Expand Button

Click to view templates in a larger grid.

## Template Types

### Free Templates

Available on all plans:
- Basic designs
- Common industries
- Good starting points

### AI Agent Templates

Templates with AI-powered features:
- More sophisticated designs
- Interactive elements
- Advanced layouts

## Using Templates

1. Browse by category
2. Hover to preview
3. Click to use
4. Template loads in editor
5. Customize with AI

## Benefits of Templates

- **Faster start** - Skip initial generation
- **Proven designs** - Professionally crafted
- **Industry-specific** - Tailored for your niche
- **Fully customizable** - Change everything

## Templates vs AI Generation

| Templates | AI Generation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Start from existing design | Start from scratch |
| Faster initial setup | More unique results |
| Less credits used | More credits used |
| Customize existing | Create new |
