---
title: "How to Sign Out"
description: "Log out of your Lindo.ai account"
---

Sign out of your account when you're done working or using a shared device.

## Signing Out

1. Click your profile icon in the header
2. Select **Sign Out** from the menu
3. You're logged out and redirected to login

## When to Sign Out

Sign out when:

- Using a shared computer
- Finished working for the day
- Switching accounts
- Security concerns

## What Happens

When you sign out:

- Session ends
- Unsaved changes may be lost
- You'll need to log in again

## Tips

- Save work before signing out
- Sign out on public computers
- Use secure password practices
- Consider session timeout settings
