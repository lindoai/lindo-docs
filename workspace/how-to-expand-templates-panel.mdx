---
title: "How to Expand the Templates Panel"
description: "Maximize the panel for better browsing"
---

Expand the templates panel to see more templates at once.

## Expanding the Panel

1. Look at the templates panel on the right
2. Find the expand icon
3. Click to expand
4. Panel takes more screen space

## Benefits of Expanding

- See more templates at once
- Better browsing experience
- Easier comparison
- Larger previews

## Collapsing the Panel

To return to normal size:

1. Click the collapse icon
2. Panel returns to default size

## Tips

- Expand when browsing extensively
- Collapse when working on prompt
- Use for better template selection
- Combine with category filtering
