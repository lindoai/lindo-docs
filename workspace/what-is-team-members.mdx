---
title: "What is Team Members"
description: "Overview of team collaboration features"
---

Team Members lets you invite others to collaborate in your workspace.

## What It Does

Team collaboration enables:
- Multiple people working together
- Role-based access
- Shared workspace
- Collaborative editing

## Availability

Team members by plan:
- Pro: 1 member (just you)
- Business: 5 members
- Elite: 20 members
- Reseller: Unlimited

## Team Roles

### Owner

Full control:
- All permissions
- Billing access
- Can delete workspace

### Admin

Management access:
- Manage team
- Access settings
- Cannot delete workspace

### Editor

Content access:
- Edit websites
- Create content
- No settings access

### Viewer

Read-only access:
- View websites
- No editing
- No settings

## Accessing Team Settings

1. Click **Settings** in sidebar
2. Select **Members** tab
3. View and manage team

## Team Features

- Invite by email
- Assign roles
- Revoke access
- Track activity

## Tips

- Use appropriate roles
- Limit admin access
- Review team regularly
- Remove inactive members
