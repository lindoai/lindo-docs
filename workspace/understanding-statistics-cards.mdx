Quick overview cards at the top of analytics.

## Available Cards

### Visitors

Total unique visitors across all websites.

### Page Views

Total pages viewed across all websites.

### Response Time

Average server response time.

### Pages

Total pages across all websites.

### Blogs

Total blog posts across all websites.

## Reading the Cards

Each card shows:
- Metric name
- Current value
- Trend indicator (if available)

## Uses

- Quick health check
- Performance overview
- Content inventory
- Trend monitoring

## Tips

- Check regularly
- Compare over time
- Investigate changes
- Use for reporting
