---
title: "How to Delete White Label Domain"
description: "Remove your custom domain configuration"
---

Remove your white label domain setup if you no longer need it or want to reconfigure.

## When to Delete

Delete your white label domain when:

- Changing to a different domain
- Downgrading your plan
- No longer need custom branding
- Troubleshooting domain issues

## Deleting the Domain

1. Open **Settings** in sidebar
2. Go to **Domain** tab
3. Find your configured domain
4. Click **Delete** or **Remove**
5. Confirm the action

## What Happens

When you delete the domain:

- Custom domain stops working
- Workspace reverts to default URL
- DNS records become inactive
- Clients use default Lindo.ai URL

## After Deletion

If you want to set up a new domain:

1. Wait for deletion to complete
2. Start fresh domain setup
3. Configure new DNS records
4. Verify the new domain

## Tips

- Inform clients before changing domains
- Update any bookmarks or links
- Consider timing for minimal disruption
- Keep DNS records until fully migrated
