description: "Save an invoice as PDF"

Download invoices as PDF files for your records or to share with clients.

## Downloading an Invoice

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Select the client
3. Go to **Billing** tab
4. Find the invoice
5. Click the **Download** icon
6. PDF saves to your computer

## What's Included

The PDF invoice contains:

- Invoice number
- Date and due date
- Client information
- Service details
- Amount and currency
- Payment status

## Use Cases

Download invoices for:

- Accounting records
- Client documentation
- Tax purposes
- Dispute resolution

## Tips

- Keep organized records
- Name files consistently
- Store securely
- Back up important invoices
