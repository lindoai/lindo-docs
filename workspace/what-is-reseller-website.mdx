---

A reseller website is your branded landing page to promote your white-labeled platform.

## What It Does

Your reseller website:
- Showcases your platform
- Captures leads
- Explains features
- Drives signups

## Features

### Professional Design

- Pre-built template
- Customizable content
- Your branding
- Mobile responsive

### Lead Capture

- Sign-up forms
- Contact forms
- CTA buttons
- Lead tracking

### Custom Content

- Your messaging
- Feature highlights
- Pricing display
- Testimonials

## Accessing

1. Go to Settings → Reseller
2. Find Reseller Website section
3. Click to configure

## Customization

You can customize:
- Headlines and copy
- Feature descriptions
- Pricing information
- Call-to-action text
- Contact information

## Where It Lives

Your reseller website is hosted at:
- Your custom domain
- Or a subdomain
- Fully branded

## Tips

- Write compelling copy
- Highlight key features
- Include clear pricing
- Add social proof
