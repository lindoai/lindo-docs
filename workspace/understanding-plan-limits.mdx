Each plan has specific limits on resources.

## Limit Types

### Active Websites

Number of websites you can have active at once.
- Inactive/deleted don't count
- Can upgrade for more

### Pages per Website

Maximum pages per individual website.
- Includes all page types
- Blogs count as pages

### Monthly Visits

Total visits across all websites per month.
- Resets monthly
- Overage may throttle

### AI Credits

Credits for AI operations.
- Resets monthly
- Can purchase more

### Team Members

People who can access your workspace.
- Includes all roles
- Owner counts as one

### Clients

Number of clients in CRM.
- Business+ plans only
- For agency management

## Limits by Plan

| Limit | Pro | Business | Elite | Reseller |
|-------|-----|----------|-------|----------|
| Websites | 5 | 20 | 100 | ∞ |
| Pages | 10 | 50 | 100 | ∞ |
| Visits | 10K | 100K | 500K | ∞ |
| Credits | 500 | 2,000 | 10,000 | 50,000 |
| Team | 1 | 5 | 20 | ∞ |
| Clients | - | 20 | 100 | ∞ |

## Approaching Limits

When near limits:
- Warning notifications
- Upgrade prompts
- Usage dashboard

## Exceeding Limits

If you exceed:
- Some features may pause
- Upgrade to continue
- Contact support

## Tips

- Monitor usage regularly
- Plan for growth
- Upgrade before hitting limits
