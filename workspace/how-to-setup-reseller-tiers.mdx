---
title: "How to Set Up Reseller Tiers"
description: "Step-by-step guide to creating subscription tiers for your clients"
---

Set up subscription tiers so your clients can choose a plan and pay through Stripe Checkout.

## Prerequisites

- Reseller (Whitelabel) plan active
- Stripe connected in **Settings → Integrations**

## Steps

### 1. Open Tiers Settings

Navigate to **Settings → Tiers** tab in your workspace.

### 2. Add a Tier

Click the **Add Tier** button. A new row appears with fields for:

- **Name** — Display name (e.g., "Starter", "Pro")
- **Website Limit** — Number of websites the client can create
- **Monthly Credits** — AI credits allocated each month
- **Price** — Monthly subscription price
- **Currency** — USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, or CAD

### 3. Configure Multiple Tiers

Add up to 4 tiers. They'll be ordered from left to right in the client's billing modal. The order determines upgrade/downgrade direction — tiers later in the list are considered higher.

### 4. Save

Click **Save Tiers**. The tiers are now available for your clients.

### 5. Client Experience

When a client opens their **Plans & Billing** modal, they'll see:
- Tier cards with pricing and features
- A **Subscribe** button on each tier
- After subscribing: **Upgrade**, **Downgrade**, or **Cancel** buttons

## Editing Tiers

You can modify tier details at any time. Changes apply to new subscriptions. Existing subscribers keep their current terms until they change plans.

## Removing a Tier

Delete a tier row and save. Existing subscribers on that tier will keep their subscription until they cancel.
