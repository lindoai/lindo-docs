---
title: "How to Downgrade a Website"
description: "Reverting to free tier"
---

Remove premium features from a website.

## What Happens

When you downgrade:
- Premium features disabled
- Custom domain disconnected
- Branding may return
- Content preserved

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Website">
    Locate the website in your workspace grid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Actions Menu">
    Hover over the website card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Downgrade">
    Select "Downgrade" from the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge the changes and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What You Lose

- Custom domain connection
- Branding removal
- Premium analytics
- Priority support

## What You Keep

- All your content
- Pages and blogs
- Design and styling
- Basic functionality

## When to Downgrade

- Project completed
- No longer need premium
- Reducing costs
- Moving to different site

## Tips

- Export before downgrading
- Notify clients if applicable
- Consider keeping for active projects
