The Elite plan is designed for established agencies with high volume.

## Features

- 100 active websites
- 100 pages per website
- 500,000 monthly visits
- 10,000 AI credits
- 20 team members
- 100 clients in CRM
- Full white label
- Priority support
- Dedicated success manager

## Limits

| Resource | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Active Websites | 100 |
| Pages per Website | 100 |
| Monthly Visits | 500,000 |
| AI Credits | 10,000 |
| Team Members | 20 |
| Clients | 100 |
