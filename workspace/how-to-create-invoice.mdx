---
title: "How to Create an Invoice"
description: "Sending one-time invoice to client"
---

Bill a client through Workspace CRM.

## Prerequisites

- Stripe connected (Settings → White Label → Integrations)
- Client added to CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace CRM">
    Click **CRM** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Client">
    Click the client you want to invoice from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Billing Tab">
    Click the **Billing** tab in the client details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Invoice">
    Click the **Create Invoice** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Invoice">
    Fill in the invoice details:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | Service Name | What you're billing for (e.g., "Website Design") |
    | Price | Amount to charge |
    | Currency | USD, EUR, GBP, etc. |
    | Frequency | One-time, Monthly, Yearly, Weekly |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invoice">
    Click **Send Invoice**.

    The client receives an email with a payment link.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Invoice Flow

1. **You create invoice** - Set amount and details
2. **Client receives email** - With payment link
3. **Client pays** - Via Stripe checkout
4. **You get paid** - Funds in your Stripe account
5. **Status updates** - Invoice shows as "Paid"

## Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Open | Sent, awaiting payment |
| Paid | Payment received |
| Refunded | Payment returned to client |

## Viewing Invoices

**For one client:**
- CRM → Select client → Billing tab

**All invoices:**
- CRM → Billing tab (main)

## Invoice Actions

After creating an invoice:
- **Copy Payment Link** - Share link directly
- **Download PDF** - Save invoice document
- **Refund** - Return payment to client

## Tips

- **Clear service names** - Client knows what they're paying for
- **Consistent pricing** - Use standard rates
- **Timely invoicing** - Bill promptly after work
