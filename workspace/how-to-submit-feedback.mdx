Your feedback helps improve Lindo.ai. Here's how to share your thoughts.

## Submitting Feedback

1. Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Feedback** from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be directed to the feedback form

## What to Include

When submitting feedback, consider including:

- **Feature requests** - New capabilities you'd like to see
- **Improvements** - Ways to make existing features better
- **Use cases** - How you use the platform and what would help
- **Pain points** - Frustrations or difficulties you've encountered

## Feedback Categories

- UI/UX improvements
- AI generation quality
- New template requests
- Integration suggestions
- Performance feedback
