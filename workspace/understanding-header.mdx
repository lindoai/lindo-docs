The header is the top bar of your workspace, providing quick access to key functions.

## Header Components

### Logo (Left)

- Displays your workspace logo
- If white-labeled: Your custom logo
- If not: Lindo.ai logo
- Clicking returns to workspace home

### Workspace Info

- Shows your workspace name
- Displays current workspace context

### Language Selector (Right)

- Globe icon
- Click to change interface language
- 11 languages available
- Changes apply immediately

### User Menu (Far Right)

- Your avatar/profile picture
- Click to open dropdown menu

## User Menu Options

When you click your avatar:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Support | Access help and support |
| Feedback | Submit feedback and suggestions |
| Community | Join Slack community |
| Hire Expert | Get professional help |
| Sign Out | Log out of your account |

## Header Behavior

### On Scroll

When you scroll down:
- Header gets a subtle blur effect
- Bottom border appears
- Header stays fixed at top

### Responsive

On smaller screens:
- Logo may shrink
- Some elements collapse
- Sidebar becomes a hamburger menu

## Customizing the Header (White Label)

On Business+ plans, customize the header:

1. Go to Settings → White Label → Appearance
2. Upload your **Full Logo** (200x50px recommended)
3. Your logo replaces the Lindo.ai logo
4. Clients see your branding
