The Appearance tab controls visual branding for your white label workspace.

## Accessing

1. Click **Settings** in sidebar
2. Select **White Label**
3. Click **Appearance** tab

## Theme Mode

Choose between **Light** and **Dark** mode for your workspace and your clients' experience:

- **Light Mode** - Clean, bright interface with light backgrounds
- **Dark Mode** - Darker interface, easier on the eyes in low-light environments

The selected theme applies to:
- Your workspace dashboard
- Your white label setup pages
- The client-facing workspace experience

Clients can also switch their own theme preference from their account settings.

## Branding Elements

### Full Logo

Your main brand logo:
- Displayed in header
- Shown on login page
- Recommended: 200x50px
- Formats: PNG, JPG, SVG

### Favicon

Browser tab icon:
- Small square icon
- Shown in browser tabs
- Recommended: 32x32px
- Formats: PNG, ICO

### Login Background

Background image for login page:
- Full-screen background
- Sets the mood for clients
- Recommended: 1920x1080px
- Formats: PNG, JPG

### Default Language

Default interface language:
- For new client accounts
- Clients can change later
- 11 languages available

## Upload Guidelines

| Element | Size | Format |
|---------|------|--------|
| Full Logo | 200x50px | PNG, JPG, SVG |
| Favicon | 32x32px | PNG, ICO |
| Login Background | 1920x1080px | PNG, JPG |

## Preview

After uploading:
- Preview shows changes
- Click "Preview Client View" to see full experience
- Changes apply immediately

## Tips

- Use transparent PNG for logos
- Keep favicon simple
- High-quality background image
- Match your brand colors
