---
title: "How to View Billing History"
description: "Checking all issued invoices"
---

See all invoices for a specific client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace CRM">
    Click **CRM** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Client">
    Click the client in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Billing Tab">
    Click the Billing tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View History">
    See all invoices:
    - Invoice number
    - Date
    - Amount
    - Status
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Invoice List

Each invoice shows:
- Invoice ID
- Service name
- Amount
- Status (open/paid/refunded)
- Date created

## Actions

From the list:
- View details
- Copy payment link
- Download PDF
- Refund

## Tips

- Review regularly
- Track payment patterns
- Follow up on open invoices
- Keep records
