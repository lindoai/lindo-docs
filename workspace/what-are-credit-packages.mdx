Credit Packages let you sell one-time bundles of AI credits to your clients. Unlike tier subscriptions that provide monthly credits, credit packages are purchased credits that don't expire.

## What It Is

Credit packages are one-time purchasable bundles you define in your workspace settings. Clients can buy them to top up their credit balance beyond what their tier provides.

## How Credit Packages Work

### Creating Packages

1. Go to **Settings → Tiers** tab (below the tiers section)
2. Click **Add Package** (up to 4 packages)
3. Set the package name, credit amount, price, and currency
4. Click **Save**

Packages are automatically assigned IDs based on their order: `pkg1`, `pkg2`, `pkg3`, `pkg4`.

### Client Purchase Flow

1. Client opens the **Plans & Billing** modal
2. Clicks the **Buy Credits** button
3. A modal shows available credit packages as cards
4. Client selects a package and clicks **Buy**
5. They're redirected to Stripe Checkout for one-time payment
6. After payment, purchased credits are added to their balance

### Credit Types

Clients have two types of credits:
- **Monthly credits** — from their tier subscription, reset each month
- **Purchased credits** — from credit packages, don't expire

Both types count toward the client's total available credits.
