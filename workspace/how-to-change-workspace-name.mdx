---
title: "How to Change Workspace Name"
description: "Update your workspace name in settings"
---

Change your workspace name to reflect your brand or organization.

## Steps

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Go to **Workspace** tab
3. Find the **Workspace Name** field
4. Enter your new name
5. Click **Save**

## Where the Name Appears

Your workspace name shows in:

- Header area
- Client login page (if white label)
- Email notifications
- Invoice headers

## Tips

- Use your company or brand name
- Keep it professional
- Consider how it looks to clients
- Update related branding if needed
