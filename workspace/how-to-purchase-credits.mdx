---
title: "How to Purchase Credits"
description: "Buying additional credits"
---

Buy extra AI credits when you need more.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Credits Tab">
    Select the **Credits** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Purchase Credits">
    Click the purchase button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Amount">
    Choose how many credits to buy:
    - 500 credits
    - 1,000 credits
    - 5,000 credits
    - Custom amount
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment details and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits Added">
    Credits added to your balance immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Credit Packages

| Package | Credits | Best For |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Small | 500 | Quick top-up |
| Medium | 1,000 | Regular use |
| Large | 5,000 | Heavy projects |

## Purchased vs Monthly

| Monthly Credits | Purchased Credits |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Reset each month | Don't expire |
| Included in plan | One-time purchase |
| Use first | Use after monthly |

## Tips

- Buy before running out
- Larger packages = better value
- Purchased credits don't expire
- Plan for big projects
