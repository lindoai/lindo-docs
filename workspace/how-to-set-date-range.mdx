---
title: "How to Set Date Range"
description: "Filtering analytics by date"
---

Filter analytics data by time period.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Analytics">
    Click **Analytics** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Date Selector">
    Look for the date range picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Range">
    Choose a preset or custom range:
    - Last 7 days
    - Last 30 days
    - Last 90 days
    - Custom dates
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Data updates to selected range.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Preset Ranges

| Range | Use Case |
|-------|----------|
| 7 days | Recent activity |
| 30 days | Monthly review |
| 90 days | Quarterly analysis |
| Custom | Specific periods |

## Tips

- Use 30 days for trends
- Compare same periods
- Check before/after campaigns
- Export for reporting
