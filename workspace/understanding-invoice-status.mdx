---
title: "Understanding Invoice Status"
description: "Open, paid, refunded states"
---

Invoices have different statuses indicating their payment state.

## Status Types

### Open

- Invoice sent
- Awaiting payment
- Client has payment link
- Action: Follow up if needed

### Paid

- Payment received
- Transaction complete
- Funds in your Stripe
- No action needed

### Refunded

- Payment returned
- Client received refund
- Invoice closed
- Processed through Stripe

## Status Flow

```
Open → Paid
Open → Refunded
Paid → Refunded
```

## Viewing Status

Status shown in:
- Invoice list
- Invoice details
- Billing tab

## Tips

- Monitor open invoices
- Follow up after 7 days
- Refund promptly if needed
- Keep records
