---
title: "How to Set Invoice Frequency"
description: "Configure one-time or recurring billing"
---

Set the billing frequency when creating invoices for clients.

## Frequency Options

When creating an invoice, choose:

- **One-time**: Single payment
- **Monthly**: Recurring monthly
- **Yearly**: Recurring annually
- **Weekly**: Recurring weekly

## Setting Frequency

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Select the client
3. Go to **Billing** tab
4. Click **Create Invoice**
5. Select frequency from dropdown
6. Complete invoice details
7. Send invoice

## How Recurring Works

For recurring invoices:

- First payment charged immediately
- Subsequent charges automatic
- Creates a subscription
- Continues until canceled

## Tips

- Match frequency to service type
- Communicate billing schedule
- Monitor recurring payments
- Cancel when service ends
