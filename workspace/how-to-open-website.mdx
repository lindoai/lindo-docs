---
title: "How to Open a Website"
description: "Clicking to edit a website"
---

Open a website in the editor to make changes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Website">
    In the workspace, locate your website in the grid.

    Use search if you have many websites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Website Card">
    Click anywhere on the website card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Editor Opens">
    The website editor loads with:
    - AI chat panel on left
    - Preview on right
    - Tabs for different sections
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Alternative Methods

### From Actions Menu

1. Hover over website card
2. Click "Edit" in actions menu

### From Search

1. Type website name in search
2. Click the result

## What Loads

When you open a website:
- Home page loads in preview
- AI chat is ready
- All tabs accessible
- Your last position remembered

## Loading Time

Editor typically loads in 2-5 seconds depending on:
- Website complexity
- Number of pages
- Your connection speed

## Tips

- Click anywhere on the card
- Use search for quick access
- Editor remembers your last page
