---
title: "How to Check DNS Status"
description: "Verifying domain connection status"
---

Verify your domain is properly connected.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Domain Settings">
    Go to Settings → White Label → Domain tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Status">
    Each subdomain shows a status:
    - **Connected** (green) - Working
    - **Pending** (yellow) - Propagating
    - **Not Connected** (red) - Issue
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Each Subdomain">
    Verify status for:
    - WebApp subdomain
    - Websites subdomain
    - Email subdomain
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Connected | DNS working | None needed |
| Pending | Propagating | Wait |
| Not Connected | Issue found | Check records |

## Propagation Time

DNS changes take time:
- Usually 15 min - 4 hours
- Can take up to 48 hours
- Be patient

## If Not Connected

1. Verify records are correct
2. Check for typos
3. Ensure no extra characters
4. Wait longer for propagation
5. Contact support if stuck

## Manual Verification

Use online tools to check:
- whatsmydns.net
- dnschecker.org
- mxtoolbox.com

## Tips

- Check after 1 hour
- Verify all records
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito mode
