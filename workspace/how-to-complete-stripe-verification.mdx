title: "How to Complete Stripe Verification"
description: "Finish setting up your Stripe account for payments"
---

Complete Stripe verification to start accepting payments from clients.

## Why Verification is Required

Stripe requires verification to:

- Comply with financial regulations
- Prevent fraud
- Enable payouts to your bank

## Verification Steps

1. Connect Stripe in Settings → Integrations
2. You're redirected to Stripe
3. Complete the verification form
4. Submit required documents

## Required Information

Stripe typically asks for:

- Business information
- Personal identification
- Bank account details
- Tax information

## Document Requirements

You may need to provide:

- Government-issued ID
- Business registration documents
- Proof of address
- Bank statements

## Checking Status

1. Open **Settings** in sidebar
2. Go to **Integrations** tab
3. Check Stripe connection status

Status indicators:
- **Pending**: Verification in progress
- **Verified**: Ready to accept payments
- **Action Required**: Additional info needed

## If Verification Fails

1. Check Stripe dashboard for details
2. Provide requested information
3. Contact Stripe support if needed

## Tips

- Use accurate information
- Provide clear document scans
- Respond promptly to requests
- Keep business info updated
