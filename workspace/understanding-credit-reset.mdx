---
title: "Understanding Credit Reset"
description: "When credits refresh each month"
---

Credits refresh on a monthly cycle.

## How It Works

- Credits reset to full allocation
- Based on your billing date
- Unused credits don't roll over
- Fresh start each month

## Reset Date

Your reset date is:
- Same day each month
- Based on when you subscribed
- Shown in Settings → Credits

## Example

If you subscribed on the 15th:
- Credits reset on the 15th each month
- January 15 → February 15 → March 15

## Before Reset

Unused credits:
- Don't carry over
- Use them or lose them
- Plan accordingly

## After Reset

- Full allocation restored
- Usage counter resets
- New month begins

## Tips

- Know your reset date
- Use credits before reset
- Plan projects around reset
- Don't waste unused credits
