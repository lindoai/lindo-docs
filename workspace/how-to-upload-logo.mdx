Add your brand logo to your white-labeled workspace.

## Prerequisites

- Business, Elite, or Reseller plan
- Logo file (PNG or SVG recommended)

## Recommended Specifications

| Property | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| Dimensions | 200x50px |
| Format | PNG or SVG |
| Background | Transparent |
| File size | Under 2MB |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Appearance Settings">
    1. Go to Settings → White Label
    2. Click the **Appearance** tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Full Logo Section">
    Look for the "Full Logo" upload area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your Logo">
    1. Click the upload area
    2. Select your logo file
    3. Or drag and drop the file
  </Step>
  <Step title="Crop (If Needed)">
    If your logo needs adjustment:
    1. Use the crop tool that appears
    2. Adjust the selection
    3. Click **Save**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Where the Logo Appears

Your logo displays in:
- Workspace header (top left)
- Login page
- Client-facing areas

## Logo Tips

**Use transparent background:**
- Works on both light and dark backgrounds
- Looks more professional

**Keep it simple:**
- Avoid too much detail at small sizes
- Text should be readable

**Test both modes:**
- Check how it looks on light backgrounds
- Check how it looks on dark backgrounds

**Consider a light version:**
- If your logo is dark, it may not show on dark backgrounds
- Some brands use different versions for different backgrounds

## Removing the Logo

To remove and revert to default:
1. Click the logo area
2. Click **Remove** or delete
3. Save changes
