---
title: "How to Upgrade Your Plan"
description: "Changing to a higher tier"
---

Upgrade to access more features and higher limits.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Plans & Billing">
    Select the **Plans & Billing** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare Plans">
    Review available plans and features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Click the upgrade button on your desired plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment details and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant Activation">
    New features available immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Happens

When you upgrade:
- Immediate access to new features
- Higher limits apply instantly
- Prorated billing for current period
- New billing cycle starts

## Upgrade Paths

| From | To | Key Gains |
|------|-----|-----------|
| Pro | Business | White label, CRM, team |
| Business | Elite | More scale, priority support |
| Elite | Reseller | Unlimited everything |

## Tips

- Compare features carefully
- Consider growth plans
- Upgrade before hitting limits
- Contact sales for custom needs
