---
title: "How to Update Monthly Credits"
description: "Change a client's recurring credit allocation"
---

Update the monthly credit allocation for a client to adjust their recurring credits.

## Updating Monthly Credits

1. Open **CRM** from sidebar
2. Select the client
3. Go to **Credits** tab
4. Find **Monthly Credits** field
5. Enter new amount
6. Click **Save**

## How It Works

Monthly credits:

- Reset each billing cycle
- Automatically allocated
- Replace previous allocation
- Start from next cycle

## When to Update

Update monthly credits when:

- Client needs more/fewer credits
- Upgrading client tier
- Adjusting based on usage
- Changing service level

## Tips

- Review usage before changing
- Communicate changes to client
- Consider usage patterns
- Document allocation changes
