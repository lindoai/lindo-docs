Generate a complete website from a text description in under a minute. The AI handles layout, content, images, and styling.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the AI Prompt Builder">
    Log in to Lindo.ai. The AI Prompt Builder is front and center in your workspace dashboard with a large textarea.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your website">
    Click the textarea and write a detailed description of what you want. Include your business name, industry, sections you need, and design style.

    ```
    A modern landing page for CloudSync, a file synchronization
    tool for remote teams. Include hero with product screenshot,
    features section, pricing table, testimonials, and FAQ.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add AI tools (optional)">
    Click the **(+)** button to add refinement tools:
    - **Language** — Set content language (default: auto-detect)
    - **Mode** — Choose Dark or Light theme
    - **Color Palette** — Pick your brand colors
    - **Website URL** — Reference an existing site for inspiration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose page structure">
    Click the dropdown next to the submit button (default: "single-page"):
    - **single-page** — All content on one scrolling page
    - **multi-page** — Multiple pages with navigation menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click the **↑** (submit) button. The AI analyzes your prompt, generates business info, creates the page structure, builds each section with content and images, and applies styling. This takes 30-60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your website">
    Your new website appears in the "Your Websites" grid below the prompt builder. Click the card to open it in the Website Editor where you can make changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Better Results

**Be specific about your business:**
```
❌ Business website
✓ Website for a plumbing company in Denver with services,
   about us, testimonials, and contact form
```

**Include numbers when relevant:**
```
❌ Features section
✓ Features section with 6 feature cards
```

**Describe the design style:**
```
❌ Nice looking site
✓ Modern, minimal design with blue accents
```

## Credit Usage

Website generation uses credits based on complexity:
- Simple landing page: ~50-100 credits
- Multi-page website: ~100-200 credits

Check your balance in the toolbar before generating.
