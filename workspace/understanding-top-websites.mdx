See which websites in your workspace get the most traffic.

## What It Shows

Ranked list of websites by:
- Page views
- Visitors
- Performance

## Reading the List

Each entry shows:
- Website name
- View count
- Visitor count
- Percentage of total

## Uses

- Identify popular sites
- Focus optimization efforts
- Understand performance
- Client reporting

## Tips

- Review regularly
- Compare over time
- Investigate top performers
- Learn from success
