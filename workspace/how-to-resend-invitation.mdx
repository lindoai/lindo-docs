---
title: "How to Resend Invitation"
description: "Re-sending invitation email"
---

Resend an invitation to a team member who didn't receive it.

## When to Resend

- Email went to spam
- Invitation expired
- Member didn't see it
- Email address typo (after fixing)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Members Settings">
    Go to Settings → Members tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Pending Invitation">
    Look for invitations with "Pending" status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Resend">
    Click the resend button next to the invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Invitation email is sent again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Check spam folder first
- Verify email address
- Wait a few minutes
- Contact member directly

## If Still Not Received

1. Revoke the invitation
2. Verify correct email
3. Send new invitation
4. Ask member to check spam
