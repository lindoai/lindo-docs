---
title: "How to Enable Email Notifications"
description: "Getting notified when workflow completes"
---

Get email alerts when workflows complete.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Workflow">
    Start creating a new workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Notification Option">
    Look for email notification toggle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Notifications">
    Turn on email notifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Workflow">
    Start the workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Email">
    Get notified when complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Notification Contents

Email includes:
- Workflow status
- Website name
- Link to view
- Completion time

## Tips

- Enable for long workflows
- Check spam folder
- Useful for batch imports
- Don't need to watch progress
