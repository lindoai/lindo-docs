title: "How to Preview a Template"
description: "View a template before using it"
---

Preview templates to see the full design before starting your website.

## Previewing a Template

1. Open templates panel
2. Find a template you like
3. Hover over the template card
4. Click **Preview** button
5. Full preview opens

## What You See

The preview shows:

- Complete template design
- All pages (if multi-page)
- Layout and structure
- Color scheme and fonts

## After Previewing

If you like the template:

1. Click **Use Template**
2. Template becomes your starting point
3. Customize with AI or manually

If not:

1. Close preview
2. Browse other templates
3. Try different categories

## Tips

- Preview multiple templates
- Compare designs
- Consider your content needs
- Check mobile responsiveness
