---
title: "How to Browse Templates"
description: "Navigating template categories and scrolling"
---

Find the perfect template for your project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View the Templates Panel">
    The Templates Panel is on the right side of your workspace dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Category">
    Click category tabs to filter:
    - **All** - Show all templates
    - **Agency & Consulting** - Marketing, consulting firms
    - **E-commerce** - Online stores
    - **Food & Restaurant** - Restaurants, cafes
    - **SaaS & Technology** - Software products
    - **And more...**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll Through Templates">
    Scroll vertically to see more templates in the selected category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expand for Better View">
    Click the **expand** icon (top right of panel) to view templates in a larger grid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview a Template">
    Hover over any template card to see a larger preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Template">
    Click any template to use it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Template Badges

Look for badges on template cards:

- **FREE** - Available on all plans
- **AI Agent** - Includes AI-powered features

## Finding Specific Templates

### By Industry

Use category tabs to narrow down:
1. Click the category that matches your industry
2. Browse filtered results

### By Style

Look at thumbnails for:
- Color schemes
- Layout styles
- Design complexity

## Tips

- **Browse multiple categories** - Your perfect template might be in an unexpected category
- **Check AI Agent templates** - Often more sophisticated
- **Preview before selecting** - Make sure it fits your vision
- **Remember you can customize** - Templates are starting points
