The traffic chart visualizes website visits over time.

## What It Shows

Two metrics plotted:
- **Page Views** - Total pages viewed
- **Visitors** - Unique visitors

## Reading the Chart

- X-axis: Time period
- Y-axis: Count
- Lines: Views vs Visitors

## Page Views vs Visitors

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Page Views | Every page load |
| Visitors | Unique people |

One visitor can generate multiple page views.

## Time Periods

View data for:
- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- Last 90 days
- Custom range

## Insights

- Upward trend = growth
- Spikes = campaigns/events
- Dips = issues or seasonality
- Views >> Visitors = engagement

## Tips

- Compare views to visitors
- Look for patterns
- Identify traffic sources
- Track over time
