Lindo.ai has special scroll behaviors that enhance the user experience.

## Header Behavior

When you scroll:

- Header may blur background
- Border appears on scroll
- Header stays fixed at top

## Scroll Effects

### Blur Effect

The header background blurs slightly when scrolling, improving readability.

### Border on Scroll

A subtle border appears under the header when you scroll down, indicating scroll position.

## Modal Scrolling

In modals:

- Content scrolls independently
- Header stays fixed
- Scroll to see all content

## Tips

- Scroll to see all content
- Header remains accessible
- Use scroll for long lists
- Modals have their own scroll
