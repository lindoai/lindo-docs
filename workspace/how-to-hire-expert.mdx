title: "How to Hire an Expert"
description: "Get professional help with your project"
---

Hire a Lindo.ai expert for complex projects or custom requirements.

## Accessing Expert Hiring

1. Click your profile icon in the header
2. Select **Hire an Expert** from the menu
3. Form opens in new tab

## What Experts Can Help With

- Complex website designs
- Custom integrations
- White label setup
- Training and onboarding
- Migration assistance

## Submitting a Request

1. Describe your project
2. Specify requirements
3. Indicate timeline
4. Provide contact info
5. Submit request

## What Happens Next

After submitting:

- Request is reviewed
- Expert matches your needs
- You receive a proposal
- Discuss and agree on scope

## Tips

- Be specific about requirements
- Include examples if possible
- Set realistic timelines
- Prepare questions
