description: "Using the plan comparison table"
---

Compare features across all plans.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Plans & Billing">
    Select the **Plans & Billing** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Comparison">
    See all plans side by side with:
    - Features
    - Limits
    - Pricing
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choosing a Plan

### Pro

Best for individuals and small projects.

### Business

Best for agencies and teams.

### Elite

Best for high-volume agencies.

### Reseller

Best for white label resellers.

## Tips

- Consider growth plans
- Factor in client needs
- Compare total value
- Start smaller, upgrade later
