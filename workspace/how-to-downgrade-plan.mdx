---
title: "How to Downgrade Your Plan"
description: "Changing to a lower tier"
---

Switch to a lower tier plan.

## Before Downgrading

Consider what you'll lose:
- Features not in lower plan
- Higher limits
- Team members (may need to remove)
- Clients (may exceed new limit)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Plans & Billing">
    Select the **Plans & Billing** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Downgrade">
    Find the downgrade option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose your new plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge changes and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What Happens

When you downgrade:
- Takes effect at billing cycle end
- Features removed at that time
- Data preserved (within limits)
- Lower rate applies

## Preparing to Downgrade

1. Export important data
2. Remove excess team members
3. Archive excess websites
4. Notify affected users

## Tips

- Plan ahead
- Export before downgrading
- Consider timing
- Can upgrade again later
