## How to Use Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse & Preview">
    Find a template that matches your industry or style. Click to see the full preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remix with AI">
    Click "Remix by lindo.ai" in the preview toolbar to open it in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Use AI chat to change colors, text, images, and sections. Add your branding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Connect your domain and publish your customized website.
  </Step>
</Steps>
