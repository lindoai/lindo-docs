---
title: "Setting Up Custom Domain"
description: "DNS configuration walkthrough"
---

Connect your own domain to your website.

## What You'll Need

- Domain you own
- Access to DNS settings
- Website ready to publish

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Domain Settings">
    1. Open website in editor
    2. Go to Settings
    3. Click Domains tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Domain">
    Enter your domain without www:
    ```
    yourdomain.com
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Connection Type">
    #
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add DNS Records">
    Go to your domain registrar and add:

    **For CNAME:**
    - Type: CNAME
    - Name: www (or @)
    - Value: cname.lindo.ai

    **For TXT:**
    - Type: TXT
    - Name: _verify
    - Value: [your verification code]
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Propagation">
    DNS changes take time:
    - Usually 15 min - 4 hours
    - Can take up to 48 hours
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    1. Return to Lindo
    2. Check status
    3. Should show "Connected"
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Troubleshooting

- Double-check record values
- Wait longer for propagation
- Contact support if stuck
