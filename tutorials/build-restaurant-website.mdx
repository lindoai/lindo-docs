Create a website for a restaurant or cafe.

## What You'll Build

- Hero with ambiance photo
- Menu sections
- About/Story
- Location and hours
- Reservation/Contact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    Enter this prompt:

    > "Create a website for an Italian restaurant. Include hero with food imagery, menu with categories (appetizers, mains, desserts, drinks), about section with story, location with hours, and reservation form. Warm, inviting design."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Menu Items">
    Update menu sections:
    1. Select menu section
    2. Add your dishes
    3. Include prices
    4. Add descriptions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Photos">
    Upload images:
    - Food photography
    - Restaurant interior
    - Team/chef photos
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Info">
    Add your details:
    - Address
    - Phone number
    - Hours
    - Reservation link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    1. Set up SEO
    2. Add to Google Business
    3. Click Publish
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Appetizing food photos
- Clear menu layout
- Easy reservation
- Mobile-friendly
