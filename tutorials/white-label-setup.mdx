---
title: "Setting Up White Label Platform"
description: "Configure custom branding for your agency"
---

Configure your Lindo.ai workspace with custom branding so clients see your agency name, logo, and domain instead of Lindo.ai. This tutorial takes 30-45 minutes including DNS propagation time.

<Note>
Requires Elite or Reseller plan.
</Note>

## What You'll Achieve

- Custom logo and favicon
- Your own domain (app.youragency.com)
- Branded login page
- Client self-registration enabled
- Stripe connected for billing

## Prerequisites

- Access to your domain's DNS settings
- Logo files (200x50px for full logo, 32x32px for favicon)
- Stripe account (optional, for billing)

## Part 1: Basic Branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open White Label settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then click the **White Label** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure workspace">
    In the **Workspace** tab, enter your agency name and toggle **Agentic Mode** ON. Click **Save Changes**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload brand assets">
    Switch to the **Appearance** tab and upload:
    - **Full Logo** (200x50px) — Your agency logo, PNG or SVG with transparent background
    - **Favicon** (32x32px) — Square icon for browser tabs
    - **Login Background** (optional, 1920x1080px) — Custom background for the login page

    Select your default language and click **Save Changes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Part 2: Custom Domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start domain setup">
    Switch to the **Domain** tab and click **Setup Domain**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    Enter your main domain (e.g., `youragency.com`) and click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure subdomains">
    Set up your subdomains:
    - **WebApp**: `app` → app.youragency.com (where clients log in)
    - **Website**: `sites` → sites.youragency.com (where client sites live)
    - **Email**: `mail` → mail.youragency.com (for email sending)

    Click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add DNS records">
    Go to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap, etc.) and add the DNS records shown:

    | Type | Name | Value |
    |------|------|-------|
    | CNAME | app | cname.lindo.ai |
    | CNAME | sites | cname.lindo.ai |
    | TXT | _lindo | (verification code shown) |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify connection">
    Return to Lindo.ai and click **Check Status** for each record. Wait for green checkmarks (5-30 minutes), then click **Complete Setup**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Part 3: Client Access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable registration">
    In the **Workspace** tab, toggle **Client Register** ON and save. Clients can now self-register at app.youragency.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test client view">
    Click **Preview Client View** to see exactly what your clients will experience when they log in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Part 4: Billing Setup (Optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    Switch to the **Integrations** tab, find the **Stripe** section, and click **Connect Stripe**. Complete Stripe's onboarding process and return to Lindo.ai.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start billing clients">
    With Stripe connected, you can now create invoices and subscriptions for clients through the CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Logo appears in header
- [ ] Favicon shows in browser tab
- [ ] Custom domain loads correctly
- [ ] Login page shows your branding
- [ ] Client registration works
- [ ] Stripe connected (if using billing)

## Troubleshooting

**DNS not connecting** — Check that record values match exactly, remove conflicting A/AAAA records, and temporarily disable Cloudflare proxy if using it.

**Logo not displaying** — Verify PNG or SVG format, check file is under 2MB, and use a transparent background.

**Clients can't register** — Confirm the toggle is ON, check they're using the correct URL, and verify DNS has propagated.
