Create a stunning portfolio to showcase your work.

## What You'll Build

- Hero with introduction
- Work/Projects gallery
- About section
- Skills/Services
- Contact form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    Enter this prompt:

    > "Create a portfolio website for a graphic designer. Include hero with name and tagline, project gallery with filtering, about section, skills, and contact form. Minimal, elegant design."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Work">
    For each project:
    1. Select the project card
    2. Update with your project
    3. Add project images
    4. Write descriptions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Personalize">
    Update with your info:
    - Your name
    - Your bio
    - Your skills
    - Contact details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Style It">
    Customize the design:
    - Choose fonts
    - Set colors
    - Adjust spacing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    1. Configure SEO
    2. Connect domain (optional)
    3. Click Publish
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- High-quality images
- Brief project descriptions
- Clear contact info
- Mobile-friendly
