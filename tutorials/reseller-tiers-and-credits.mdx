Learn how to structure your reseller business with product tiers, credit packages, and free trials so clients can self-serve and scale on your platform.

## Prerequisites

- Reseller plan
- Stripe account connected
- At least one reseller website created

<Note>
Product tiers and credit packages are only available on reseller websites. Standard websites do not show these options.
</Note>

## Part 1: Creating Product Tiers with Website Limits

Product tiers let you offer different service levels to clients. Each tier controls how many websites a client can create.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your reseller website">
    1. Open your reseller website in the editor
    2. Navigate to the **Pages** tab
    3. Click **Products**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a tier product">
    1. Click **Create Product**
    2. Enter the tier name (e.g., "Starter", "Professional", "Enterprise")
    3. Set the price and billing frequency (monthly or yearly)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the website limit">
    In the product settings, find the **Website Limit** field. This controls how many websites the client can create on this tier.

    Example tiers:

    | Tier | Price | Website Limit |
    |------|-------|---------------|
    | Starter | $29/mo | 1 |
    | Professional | $79/mo | 5 |
    | Enterprise | $199/mo | 20 |

    The website limit is stored as `client_limit` in the product metadata and applied automatically when a client purchases the product.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Click **Save** to store the product. Repeat for each tier you want to offer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Part 2: Creating Credit Packages

Credit packages let you sell AI credits to clients as one-time purchases or recurring subscriptions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a credit product">
    1. In your reseller website's **Products** section, click **Create Product**
    2. Enter a name (e.g., "100 AI Credits", "500 Credit Pack")
    3. Set the price and frequency
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the credit allocation">
    In the product settings, find the **Client Credit Allocation** field. Enter the number of credits the client receives when they purchase this product.

    Example packages:

    | Package | Price | Credits |
    |---------|-------|---------|
    | Starter Pack | $10 | 100 |
    | Growth Pack | $40 | 500 |
    | Agency Pack | $150 | 2,000 |

    The credit allocation is stored as `client_credit_allocation` in the product metadata. When a client purchases the product, the specified credits are automatically added to their purchased credit balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Combine tiers with credits">
    You can set both a website limit and a credit allocation on the same product. This lets you create all-in-one plans:

    - **Website Limit**: Controls how many sites the client can build
    - **Client Credit Allocation**: Grants credits on purchase

    For example, a "Professional" plan at $79/mo could include 5 websites and 500 credits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Part 3: Setting Up Free Trials

Free trials let potential clients try your platform before committing to a paid plan.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a subscription product">
    Free trials only work with subscription products (monthly, yearly, or weekly frequency). Create a product or edit an existing tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trial period">
    In the product settings, find the **Trial Period (days)** field. Enter the number of days for the free trial.

    Common trial lengths:

    - **7 days** — Quick evaluation
    - **14 days** — Standard trial
    - **30 days** — Extended trial for complex use cases
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure trial limits">
    Set the **Website Limit** and **Client Credit Allocation** for the trial product. Clients get full access to these resources during the trial period.

    After the trial ends, the client is billed automatically at the product price. If they cancel before the trial ends, they are not charged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Part 4: Configuring Default Client Credits

Default client credits set a baseline monthly credit allocation for all new clients in your workspace, even if they haven't purchased a specific credit package.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Reseller Config tab">
    1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
    2. Select the **Reseller** tab
    3. Click the **Config** tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set default credits">
    Enter a value in the **Default Client Credits** field. This is the number of monthly credits every new client receives by default.

    This value is used as a fallback when a client does not have an individual credit limit set. If a client has a specific credit limit configured in the CRM, that value takes priority over the workspace default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save Changes** to persist the default credit value to your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How Credit Precedence Works

When a new client is created, the system determines their monthly credits in this order:

1. **Individual credit limit** — If set on the client record in the CRM, this value is used
2. **Workspace default** — If no individual limit is set, the Default Client Credits value from the Config tab is used
3. **Zero** — If neither is configured, the client starts with zero monthly credits

## Putting It All Together

A typical reseller setup might look like this:

1. **Set a workspace default** of 50 credits so every client gets a baseline allocation
2. **Create 2-3 product tiers** with increasing website limits and credit allocations
3. **Add a free trial** to your entry-level tier to attract new clients
4. **Offer standalone credit packs** for clients who need additional credits beyond their plan

This gives clients flexibility to choose a plan that fits their needs while ensuring everyone has access to credits from day one.
