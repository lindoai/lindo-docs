---
title: "Creating a Product Catalog"
description: "Set up e-commerce products with pricing and checkout"
---

Learn how to create and manage products for selling digital or physical goods through your website.

## What You Can Sell

Lindo.ai supports:

- Digital products (downloads, courses)
- Physical products
- Services
- Subscriptions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Products">
    1. Open your website in the editor
    2. Navigate to **Pages** tab
    3. Click **Products** section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Product">
    1. Click **Create Product**
    2. Fill in product details:
       - **Name**: Product title
       - **Description**: What's included
       - **Price**: Amount to charge
       - **Frequency**: One-time, monthly, yearly, weekly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Product Image">
    1. Click the image upload area
    2. Select a product photo
    3. Crop if needed
    4. Save

    Use high-quality images (recommended: 800x800px minimum).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Pricing">
    Set your pricing model:

    - **One-time**: Single purchase
    - **Monthly**: Recurring monthly charge
    - **Yearly**: Annual subscription
    - **Weekly**: Weekly billing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Digital File (Optional)">
    For digital products:

    1. Click **Upload Digital File**
    2. Select your file (PDF, ZIP, etc.)
    3. Customers receive download link after purchase
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Thank You Message">
    Customize post-purchase experience:

    1. Enter thank you message
    2. Include next steps or instructions
    3. Add support contact info
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Checkout Options">
    Enable additional fields:

    - **Discount Codes**: Allow promo codes
    - **Phone Number**: Collect customer phone
    - **Shipping Address**: For physical products
    - **Adjustable Quantity**: Let customers change quantity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Products">
    Products appear on your website automatically. Use the Block Creator to add product sections:

    ```
    Create a product grid showing all products with:
    - Product image
    - Name and price
    - Buy button
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    To accept payments:

    1. Go to Settings → Integrations
    2. Connect your Stripe account
    3. Complete verification
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Managing Orders

View orders in Website CRM:

1. Open CRM tab in editor
2. See customer purchases
3. Track payment status
4. Manage subscriptions

## Tips for Product Catalogs

- Use clear, descriptive names
- Include detailed descriptions
- Show product benefits
- Use professional images
- Test checkout flow
