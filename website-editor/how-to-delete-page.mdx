---
title: "How to Delete a Page"
description: "Removing a page permanently"
---

Permanently remove a page from your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Page">
    Locate the page to delete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Actions">
    Hover and click the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Delete">
    Click "Delete" option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Acknowledge and confirm deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Important Notes

- Cannot be undone
- Page removed permanently
- Links to page will break

## Before Deleting

- Backup if needed
- Update navigation
- Check for links

## Tips

- Duplicate first if unsure
- Update internal links
- Consider unpublishing instead
