Page cards display pages in the Pages tab.

## Card Components

### Thumbnail

Preview image of the page.

### Page Name

Title of the page.

### Status Badge

Published or Draft indicator.

### Actions

Edit, duplicate, delete options.

## Interacting with Cards

- Click to edit
- Hover for actions
- View status at glance

## Tips

- Use thumbnails to identify
- Check status badges
- Quick access to actions
