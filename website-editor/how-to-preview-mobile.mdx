---
title: "How to Preview on Mobile"
description: "Viewing mobile layout"
---

View your website as it appears on mobile phones.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Device Buttons">
    Look for device icons in preview header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Mobile">
    Click the phone icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Mobile Layout">
    Preview shows narrow mobile view.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What to Check

- Single column layout
- Readable text
- Touch-friendly buttons
- Mobile navigation
- Image sizing

## Tips

- Most important view
- Check all pages
- Test navigation
- Verify readability
