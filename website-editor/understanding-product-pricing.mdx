Set pricing for your products.

## Pricing Types

### One-Time

- Single payment
- No recurring
- For physical/digital products

### Monthly

- Recurring monthly
- Subscription model
- For services/memberships

### Yearly

- Recurring annually
- Subscription model
- Often discounted

### Weekly

- Recurring weekly
- Short-term subscriptions

## Choosing Pricing

| Product Type | Pricing |
|--------------|---------|
| Physical product | One-time |
| Digital download | One-time |
| Membership | Monthly/Yearly |
| Service | Monthly |

## Tips

- Match to product type
- Consider customer preference
- Offer yearly discounts
