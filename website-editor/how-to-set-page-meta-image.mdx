---
title: "How to Set the Page Meta Image"
description: "Use the Media Library to set your page's SEO meta image for social sharing"
---

The page meta image (Open Graph image) controls how your page looks when shared on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

## Setting the Meta Image

1. Open the page settings panel
2. Navigate to the SEO section
3. Find the meta image field
4. Click "Select Image" to open the Media Library
5. Choose an image from your uploaded assets or upload a new one
6. The selected image URL is automatically applied

## Why It Matters

- Controls the preview image when your page is shared on social media
- Improves click-through rates from social platforms
- Gives your shared links a professional appearance

## Recommended Specs

- Minimum size: 1200 x 630 pixels
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Keep important content centered

## Tips

- Upload your meta image to the Media Library first for easy reuse
- Use a clear, branded image that represents the page content
- Test your meta image with social media preview tools
