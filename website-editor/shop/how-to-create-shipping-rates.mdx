---
title: "How to Create Shipping Rates"
description: "Create Stripe shipping rates for physical-product checkout"
---

Shipping rates are created in Stripe, not in the product editor. Lindo reads your active Stripe shipping rates and adds them to checkout when at least one product requires shipping.

## Create shipping rates in Stripe

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Settings -> Shop**
3. In the **Shipping Rate** guide, click **Open Stripe**
4. Create one or more shipping rates in Stripe

Common examples:

- Standard shipping
- Express shipping
- International shipping
- Local delivery

## Make a product use shipping

1. Open the **Pages** tab
2. Edit a product
3. Enable **Shipping Address** in checkout options
4. Save the product

Once at least one item in checkout requires shipping, Lindo sends your active Stripe shipping rates to Checkout.

## How it works in cart checkout

- If all items are digital or do not require shipping, no shipping rates are shown
- If one or more items require shipping, Stripe shows your active shipping rates
- The shipping rates come directly from your connected Stripe account

## Best practices

- Create clear rate names in Stripe
- Test both desktop and mobile checkout flows
- Keep domestic and international rates separate when needed
- Disable unused rates in Stripe so customers only see valid options
