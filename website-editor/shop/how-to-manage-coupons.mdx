---
title: "How to Manage Coupons"
description: "Create coupons in Stripe and use them in Lindo checkout"
---

Lindo uses Stripe coupons and promotion codes. You create them in Stripe, then enable discount-code collection on the products that should accept them.

## Create a coupon in Stripe

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Settings -> Shop**
3. In the **Coupons** guide, click **Open Stripe**
4. Create your coupon or promotion code in Stripe

Stripe lets you configure:

- Percentage discounts
- Fixed amount discounts
- One-time or reusable offers
- Expiration dates and redemption limits

## Allow coupons on a product

1. Open the **Pages** tab
2. Open a product's settings
3. Enable **Discount Codes** in checkout options
4. Save the product

If discount codes are disabled on a product, Stripe coupons will not appear for that product's checkout.

## Best practices

- Use promotion codes for marketing campaigns
- Add expiration dates for limited offers
- Test checkout before announcing a promotion
- Keep product margins in mind before applying large discounts
