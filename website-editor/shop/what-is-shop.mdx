Shop is Lindo's commerce layer for websites. It lets you turn a regular website into a Stripe-powered storefront with products, cart checkout, coupons, shipping rates, and inventory workflows.

## What Shop includes

- Product selling through Stripe Checkout
- One-time products, digital products, services, and subscriptions
- Cart checkout for multiple items in one payment
- Coupon support through Stripe
- Shipping rate support through Stripe
- Inventory workflows through Stripe apps like Stockify

## Requirements

Before Shop appears in your website settings:

- Your workspace must have ecommerce support enabled
- Your website must have Stripe connected in **Settings -> Integrations**
- You need at least one product on the website to start selling

## Where to find it

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings**
3. Open the **Shop** tab
4. Turn on **Shop**

When Shop is enabled, your website can use Stripe-powered commerce features and your product documentation becomes the next step in setup.

## Stripe features inside Shop

### Coupons

Coupons are created and managed in Stripe, then automatically available during checkout when your product allows discount codes.

### Inventory

Inventory is managed through Stripe-compatible apps. Lindo recommends **Stockify** for stock tracking and inventory operations.

### Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are created in Stripe and then shown in checkout whenever at least one product requires shipping address collection.

## Recommended setup order

1. Connect Stripe
2. Enable Shop
3. Create shipping rates in Stripe if you sell physical goods
4. Set up coupons and inventory workflows
5. Create and publish products
