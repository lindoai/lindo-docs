---
title: "How to Enable Shop"
description: "Turn on Shop in website settings"
---

Enable Shop when you want your website to use Lindo's Stripe-powered commerce tools.

## Before you start

Make sure:

- Your workspace has ecommerce support enabled
- Your website already has Stripe connected
- You are editing the correct website

## Enable Shop

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings**
3. Open the **Shop** tab
4. Turn on the **Shop** switch
5. Click **Save Changes**

After saving, the website stores Shop activation at the website level, so you can enable it for one website without changing every website in the workspace.

## What changes after enabling Shop

- Product and cart checkout use the website's shop configuration
- Shop guidance appears for coupons, inventory, and shipping rates
- Stripe shipping rates can be shown during checkout for shippable products

## If the Shop tab does not appear

Check these first:

- Your workspace may not have ecommerce support enabled yet
- You may be on a website that does not inherit workspace ecommerce access
- Your account may still need Shop activation from the Lindo team
