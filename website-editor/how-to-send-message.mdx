---
title: "How to Send a Message to AI"
description: "Typing and submitting prompts"
---

Communicate with AI to edit your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Chat Input">
    Look at the bottom of the chat panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Your Message">
    Enter your request:
    - What you want changed
    - What to add
    - What to modify
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Message">
    Press Enter or click send button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Response">
    AI processes and responds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Good Messages

- "Add a testimonials section"
- "Change the hero headline to..."
- "Make the buttons blue"
- "Add a contact form"

## Tips

- Be specific
- One request at a time
- Clear language
- Check results
