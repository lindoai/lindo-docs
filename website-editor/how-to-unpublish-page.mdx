---
title: "How to Unpublish a Page"
description: "Removing page from live site"
---

Hide a page from public view without deleting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Page">
    Locate the published page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Actions">
    Hover and click the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Unpublish">
    Click "Unpublish" option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Page Hidden">
    Page becomes draft, hidden from public.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Unpublish vs Delete

| Unpublish | Delete |
|-----------|--------|
| Hides page | Removes page |
| Can republish | Permanent |
| Content preserved | Content lost |

## Tips

- Use for temporary hiding
- Better than deleting
- Can republish anytime
