title: "How to Edit Product Settings"
description: "Configuring product details"
---

Configure all settings for a product.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Product">
    Locate the product to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Product">
    Click to open settings modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Settings">
    Configure all product options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click save to apply changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Settings to Configure

- Name and description
- Price and currency
- Frequency
- Image
- Checkout options
- Thank you message

## Tips

- Complete all fields
- Test checkout
- Clear descriptions
