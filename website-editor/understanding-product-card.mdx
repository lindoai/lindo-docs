description: "Product display in grid"

Product cards display products in the Pages tab.

## Card Components

### Product Image

Thumbnail of the product.

### Product Name

Title of the product.

### Price

Product price display.

### Status

Published or draft indicator.

## Interacting with Cards

- Click to edit
- Hover for actions
- View price at glance

## Tips

- Use clear images
- Set accurate prices
- Check status
