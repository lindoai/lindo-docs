---
title: "How to View All Pages"
description: "Accessing the Pages tab"
---

See all pages in your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Website Editor">
    Open your website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View All Pages">
    See all pages, blogs, and products.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Page List

Shows:
- Page thumbnails
- Page names
- Status badges
- Page types

## Tips

- Use search to filter
- Check status badges
- Click to edit
