---
title: "How to Create a Blog Post"
description: "Adding a new blog to your website"
---

Add blog content to your website using AI.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Website Editor">
    Click your website from the workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click the **Pages** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Blog">
    Click **Add Blog** (not Add Page).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Blog Post">
    Enter a topic or outline:

    ```
    Blog post about 10 productivity tips for remote workers
    ```

    Or more detailed:

    ```
    Blog post: "The Ultimate Guide to Project Management"
    - Introduction to project management
    - 5 key methodologies (Agile, Scrum, Kanban, Waterfall, Lean)
    - How to choose the right one
    - Tools and software recommendations
    - Conclusion with actionable steps
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Create**. The Simple Blog Agent writes your post.

    **Credit cost:** 10 credits
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Content">
    The blog post opens with:
    - Title
    - Featured image
    - Full content in sections
    - Author info
    - Publication date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit if Needed">
    Use AI Chat to refine:

    ```
    Add more examples to the Agile section
    ```

    ```
    Make the introduction more engaging
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Blog URL">
    Edit the URL path to be SEO-friendly:
    - Default: `/blog/new-post`
    - Better: `/blog/ultimate-guide-project-management`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** to make the post live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Blog Features

### Auto-Generated Elements

The AI creates:
- SEO title and meta description
- Featured image (from Pexels)
- Structured headings
- Author name and bio
- Publication date

### Blog List Page

Your blog posts appear on `/blog` automatically with:
- Post thumbnails
- Titles and excerpts
- Publication dates

## Tips

- **Specific topics** - "10 tips for X" works better than "about X"
- **Include keywords** - Mention terms you want to rank for
- **Review for accuracy** - AI content should be fact-checked
- **Add internal links** - Link to other pages on your site
