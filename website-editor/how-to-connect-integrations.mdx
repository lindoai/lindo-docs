---
title: "How to Connect Website Integrations"
description: "Adding third-party services to your website"
---

Add analytics, chat, and other services to your website.

## Accessing Integrations

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Settings** tab
3. Click **Integrations** section

## Available Integrations

### Google Tag Manager

Container for all your marketing tags.

**To connect:**
1. Get your GTM Container ID (GTM-XXXXXX)
2. Enter in the GTM field
3. Save

### Google Analytics

Track website traffic and behavior.

**To connect:**
1. Get your GA Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX)
2. Enter in the GA field
3. Save

### Microsoft Clarity

Heatmaps and session recordings.

**To connect:**
1. Get your Clarity Project ID
2. Enter in the Clarity field
3. Save

### Crisp

Live chat widget.

**To connect:**
1. Get your Crisp Website ID
2. Enter in the Crisp field
3. Save

### Intercom

Customer messaging platform.

**To connect:**
1. Get your Intercom App ID
2. Enter in the Intercom field
3. Save

### HubSpot Chat

CRM-integrated chat.

**To connect:**
1. Get your HubSpot Portal ID
2. Enter in the HubSpot field
3. Save

## Integration Status

After connecting:
- Green indicator = Connected
- Red indicator = Error
- No indicator = Not configured

## Testing Integrations

After connecting:

1. **Publish** your website
2. **Visit** the live site
3. **Check** the integration dashboard
4. **Verify** data is flowing

### Testing Google Analytics

1. Open GA Real-Time reports
2. Visit your website
3. See yourself as active user

### Testing Chat Widgets

1. Visit your website
2. Chat widget should appear
3. Test sending a message

## Disconnecting

To remove an integration:

1. Go to Settings → Integrations
2. Find the integration
3. Clear the ID field
4. Save

## Website vs Workspace Integrations

| Website Integrations | Workspace Integrations |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| Per-website | Workspace-wide |
| Analytics, chat | Stripe, GTM |
| In website settings | In workspace settings |

## Tips

- **Use GTM** - Manage all tags in one place
- **Test after connecting** - Verify data flows
- **One chat widget** - Don't add multiple chat tools
