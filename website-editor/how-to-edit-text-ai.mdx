---
title: "How to Edit Text with AI"
description: "Selecting text and requesting changes"
---

Use AI to modify text content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Text">
    Click on text in the preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Change">
    In chat, describe what you want:
    - "Change this to..."
    - "Make it more professional"
    - "Translate to Spanish"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Request">
    Submit your message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Updates Text">
    Text changes in preview.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Example Requests

- "Change the headline to 'Welcome to Our Platform'"
- "Make this description shorter"
- "Add more detail about pricing"
- "Make the tone more friendly"

## Tips

- Select specific text
- Be clear about changes
- Review results
