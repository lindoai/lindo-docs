---
title: "What is the Pages Tab"
description: "Overview of page management"
---

The Pages tab is where you manage all pages, blogs, and products for your website.

## What's Included

### Page Types

- Pages
- Blogs
- Products

### Features

- Search pages
- Create new pages
- View page cards
- Access page actions

## How to Access

Click **Pages** tab in the editor header.

## Tips

- Use search for large sites
- Filter by page type
- Create pages as needed
- Manage from one place
