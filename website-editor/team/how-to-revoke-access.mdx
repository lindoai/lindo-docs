---
title: "How to Revoke Website Access"
description: "Removing a team member's access to your website"
---

Remove a team member who no longer needs access to your website.

## Revoking Access

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Find the team member to remove
3. Click the **Remove** or trash icon
4. Confirm the removal

## What Happens

When you revoke access:
- Team member loses immediate access
- They can no longer view or edit the website
- Their previous changes remain
- They're removed from the team list

## When to Revoke

- Project completed
- Team member left the organization
- Role change (no longer needs access)
- Security concerns

## Re-granting Access

If you need to give access back:
1. Send a new invitation
2. They'll need to accept again
3. Previous permissions don't carry over
