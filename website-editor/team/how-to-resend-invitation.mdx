---
title: "How to Resend Website Invitation"
description: "Re-sending an invitation email to a team member"
---

If a team member didn't receive their invitation, you can resend it.

## Resending an Invitation

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Find the pending team member
3. Click the **Resend** button
4. A new invitation email is sent

## When to Resend

- Original email went to spam
- Email was accidentally deleted
- Invitation link expired
- Team member can't find the email

## Invitation Status

Team members show different statuses:
- **Pending** - Invitation sent, not yet accepted
- **Active** - Invitation accepted, has access

## If Resend Doesn't Work

1. Verify the email address is correct
2. Ask them to check spam/junk folder
3. Try removing and re-inviting with correct email
4. Contact support if issues persist
