description: "Add collaborators to your website"

Invite team members to collaborate on a specific website.

## Inviting a Team Member

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **Team** section
4. Click **Invite Member**
5. Enter their email
6. Select their role
7. Send invitation

## Available Roles

- **Admin**: Full access to website
- **Editor**: Can edit content
- **Viewer**: Read-only access

## What They Can Do

Team members can:

- Access this specific website
- Edit based on their role
- View analytics
- Manage pages (if permitted)

## Tips

- Use minimum necessary permissions
- Review team access regularly
- Remove inactive members
- Communicate role expectations
