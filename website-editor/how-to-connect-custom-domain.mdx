---
title: "How to Add Custom Domain"
description: "Connect your own domain to your website"
---

Connect your own domain to your website instead of using the default lindo.site subdomain. Your site will be accessible at www.yourdomain.com.

<Note>
Requires Pro plan or higher. You need access to your domain's DNS settings.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open website settings">
    Open your website in the editor, go to the **Settings** tab, and click the **Domains** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    In the Custom Domain field, enter your domain (e.g., `www.yourdomain.com` or `yourdomain.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View DNS records">
    The system shows you the DNS records to add. You'll see either CNAME records (for subdomains like www) or A records (for root domains), plus a TXT record for verification.

    **Example CNAME record:**
    | Type | Name | Value |
    |------|------|-------|
    | CNAME | www | cname.lindo.ai |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add records to your registrar">
    Log in to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap, etc.), find the DNS settings, and add each record with the exact values shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify connection">
    Return to Lindo.ai and click **Check Status**. DNS changes take 5-30 minutes to propagate, though full global propagation can take up to 48 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="SSL is automatic">
    Once DNS connects, an SSL certificate is automatically issued. Your site will be accessible via HTTPS with no additional setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## CNAME vs A Record

**CNAME (Recommended)** — Works with subdomains like www, blog, or shop. Easier to set up and more flexible.

**A Record** — Works with root domains (yourdomain.com without www). Requires a specific IP address.

## Troubleshooting

**Not connecting?**
- Double-check record values for typos
- Remove any trailing dots from values
- If using Cloudflare, temporarily disable the proxy (orange cloud)
- Delete conflicting A records that point elsewhere

**Still pending after 48 hours?**
- Verify records are saved correctly in your registrar
- Some registrars have propagation delays—contact their support

## Website Domain vs White Label Domain

| Website Domain | White Label Domain |
|----------------|-------------------|
| For one website | For entire workspace |
| Set in website settings | Set in workspace settings |
| Example: www.client.com | Example: app.agency.com |
