---
title: "How to Set Product Frequency"
description: "One-time vs recurring"
---

Choose how often customers are billed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Product Settings">
    Click the product to open settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Frequency Field">
    Locate the frequency dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Frequency">
    Choose from:
    - One-time
    - Monthly
    - Yearly
    - Weekly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click save to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Frequency Options

| Frequency | Billing |
|-----------|---------|
| One-time | Single payment |
| Monthly | Every month |
| Yearly | Every year |
| Weekly | Every week |

## Tips

- Match to product type
- One-time for products
- Recurring for services
