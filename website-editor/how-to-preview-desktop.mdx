title: "How to Preview on Desktop"
description: "Viewing desktop layout"
---

View your website as it appears on desktop.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Device Buttons">
    Look for device icons in preview header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Desktop">
    Click the desktop/monitor icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Desktop Layout">
    Preview shows full-width desktop view.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What to Check

- Full layout
- Navigation
- Large images
- Wide sections

## Tips

- Default view
- Check all sections
- Verify spacing
