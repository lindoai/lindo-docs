---
title: "What is the Pages Tab"
description: "Overview of page management"
---

The Pages tab manages all pages in your website.

## What It Shows

- All pages
- Blog posts
- Products
- Page status

## Page Types

### Pages

Regular website pages:
- Home
- About
- Services
- Contact

### Blogs

Blog posts and articles.

### Products

E-commerce products.

## Features

- Search pages
- Add new pages
- Edit pages
- Delete pages
- Change status

## Tips

- Organize pages logically
- Use clear names
- Manage status carefully
