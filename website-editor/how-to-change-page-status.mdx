---
title: "How to Change Page Status"
description: "Switching between published/draft"
---

Toggle a page between published and draft.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Page">
    Locate the page to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Actions">
    Hover and click the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Status">
    Select publish or unpublish.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Status Options

- **Publish** - Make page live
- **Unpublish** - Hide from public

## Tips

- Unpublish to hide temporarily
- Publish when ready
- Check before going live
