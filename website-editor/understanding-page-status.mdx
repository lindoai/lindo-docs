---
title: "Understanding Page Status"
description: "Published vs Draft"
---

Pages can be published or in draft state.

## Status Types

### Published

- Visible to public
- Live on website
- Indexed by search engines

### Draft

- Not visible to public
- Work in progress
- Only you can see

## Status Indicators

- **Green badge** - Published
- **Gray badge** - Draft

## Changing Status

- Publish to make live
- Unpublish to hide
- Save as draft while working

## Tips

- Keep drafts while editing
- Publish when ready
- Unpublish to hide temporarily
