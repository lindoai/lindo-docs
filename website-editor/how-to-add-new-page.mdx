---
title: "How to Create a New Page"
description: "Adding a new page to your website"
---

Add additional pages to your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Website Editor">
    Click your website from the workspace to open the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click the **Pages** tab in the editor header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Page">
    Click the **Add Page** or **+** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the Page">
    In the prompt that appears, describe what you want:

    ```
    About page with company story, team section with 4 members,
    and our values
    ```

    Or be more specific:

    ```
    Services page with:
    - Hero: "Our Services" headline
    - 4 service cards with icons
    - Pricing comparison table
    - FAQ section
    - Contact CTA
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Create** or submit. The AI generates your new page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Edit">
    The new page opens in the editor. Use AI Chat to refine:

    ```
    Add more detail to the team section
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Page URL">
    In the address bar, edit the page path:
    - Default: `/new-page`
    - Change to: `/about` or `/services`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** to make the page live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Page Types

### Regular Pages

Standard content pages:
- About
- Services
- Contact
- Pricing

### Blog Posts

For blog content:
1. Click **Add Blog** instead of Add Page
2. AI generates blog post format
3. Includes title, content, author, date

### Product Pages

For e-commerce:
1. Create product in Products section
2. Add product to a page via AI

## Tips

- **Plan structure first** - Know what pages you need
- **Consistent navigation** - Update header with new pages
- **SEO-friendly URLs** - Use descriptive paths like `/about`
