description: "E-commerce product management"
---

Products let you sell items directly from your website with Stripe checkout.

## Purpose

Create and manage:
- Physical products
- Digital downloads
- Services
- Subscriptions

## Accessing Products

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Pages** tab
3. Click **Products** section

## Product Components

### Product Card

Each product shows:
- Product image
- Name
- Price
- Status (active/inactive)

### Product Settings

Full configuration modal with:
- Basic info (name, description)
- Pricing (amount, currency, frequency)
- Checkout options
- Digital file upload
- Thank you message

## Product Types

### One-Time Purchase

Single payment products:
- Physical goods
- Digital downloads
- One-time services

### Subscription

Recurring payment products:
- Monthly memberships
- Yearly subscriptions
- Weekly services

## Pricing Options

| Frequency | Use Case |
|-----------|----------|
| One-time | Single purchase |
| Monthly | Monthly subscription |
| Yearly | Annual subscription |
| Weekly | Weekly service |

## Checkout Options

Configure what to collect:
- **Discount Codes** - Allow promo codes
- **Phone Number** - Collect customer phone
- **Shipping Address** - For physical products
- **Adjustable Quantity** - Let customers choose quantity

## Digital Products

For downloadable products:
1. Upload the file
2. Customer receives download link after purchase
3. File is delivered automatically

## Thank You Message

Custom message shown after purchase:
- Confirmation text
- Next steps
- Additional instructions

## Requirements

- Stripe connected (Settings → Integrations)
- Product added to a page
