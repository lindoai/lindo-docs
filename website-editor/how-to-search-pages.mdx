---
title: "How to Search Pages"
description: "Filtering pages by name"
---

Find specific pages quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Search Input">
    Locate the search field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Search Term">
    Enter page name or keyword.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Results">
    List filters to matching pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Search by name
- Partial matches work
- Clear to see all
