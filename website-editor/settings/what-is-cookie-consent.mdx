---
title: "What is Cookie Consent Widget"
description: "GDPR cookie banner for your website"
---

The cookie consent widget displays a banner asking visitors to accept cookies.

## Overview

Many regions require websites to inform visitors about cookie usage and obtain consent. The cookie consent widget helps you comply with these regulations.

## When It Appears

The banner appears:
- On first visit to your website
- Until the visitor accepts or declines
- After cookies are cleared

## What It Includes

The widget displays:
- Message about cookie usage
- Link to your privacy policy
- Accept button
- Optional decline option

## Configuration Options

You can customize:
- Banner text
- Privacy policy URL
- Button text
- Appearance

## Legal Compliance

The cookie consent widget helps with:
- GDPR (European Union)
- CCPA (California)
- Other privacy regulations

Note: Consult legal advice for full compliance requirements.
