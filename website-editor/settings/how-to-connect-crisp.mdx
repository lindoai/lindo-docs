description: "Add live chat support to your website"

Connect Crisp to provide live chat support for your website visitors.

## What is Crisp?

Crisp provides:

- Live chat widget
- Chatbot capabilities
- Team inbox
- Customer support tools

## Connecting Crisp

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **Integrations** section
4. Find **Crisp**
5. Enter your Crisp Website ID
6. Save

## Finding Your Website ID

1. Log into crisp.chat
2. Go to Settings → Website Settings
3. Find your Website ID
4. Copy the ID

## After Connecting

Crisp chat widget appears on your website. Visitors can:

- Start conversations
- Get instant support
- Leave messages

## Tips

- Set up auto-responses
- Configure business hours
- Train your team
- Monitor response times
