title: "How to Configure Age Gate"
description: "Setting age limit and verification text"
---

Customize your age verification popup.

## Accessing Configuration

1. Go to **Settings** → **General**
2. Find the **Age Gate** section
3. Enable the age gate toggle
4. Configure the options

## Configuration Options

### Minimum Age
Set the required age:
- Common values: 18, 21, 25
- Depends on your content/industry

### Verification Text
Customize the message:
- Explain why verification is needed
- Include legal disclaimers if required

### Button Text
Customize the confirmation button:
- "I am [age] or older"
- "Yes, I'm of legal age"
- "Enter"

### Decline Action
What happens if visitor declines:
- Show a message
- Redirect to another page

## Example Configuration

```
Minimum Age: 21
Text: You must be 21 years or older to enter this website.
Button: I am 21 or older
```

## Best Practices

- Set appropriate age for your content
- Be clear about requirements
- Provide alternative for underage visitors
- Consult legal requirements for your industry
