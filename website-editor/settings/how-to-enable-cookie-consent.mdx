Enable a cookie consent banner to comply with privacy regulations like GDPR.

## What is Cookie Consent?

A cookie consent banner:

- Informs visitors about cookie usage
- Allows visitors to accept or decline
- Helps with GDPR compliance
- Shows your privacy policy link

## Enabling Cookie Consent

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **General** section
4. Find **Cookie Consent Widget**
5. Toggle **Enable** to on

## Configuring the Banner

### Policy URL

Enter your privacy policy page URL:

```
/privacy
```

Or a full URL:

```
https://yoursite.com/privacy
```

### Banner Text

Customize the message visitors see:

```
We use cookies to enhance your experience. By continuing to visit this site you agree to our use of cookies.
```

### Button Text

Customize the accept button:

```
Accept
```

## Banner Appearance

The cookie banner:

- Appears at bottom of page
- Shows on first visit
- Remembers visitor choice
- Matches your website theme

## Best Practices

- Link to actual privacy policy
- Keep message clear and concise
- Ensure banner is visible
- Test on mobile devices

## Compliance Notes

This banner provides basic cookie consent. For full GDPR compliance:

- Create a comprehensive privacy policy
- Document what cookies you use
- Explain data collection practices
- Consult legal advice if needed
