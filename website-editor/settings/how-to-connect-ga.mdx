---
title: "How to Connect Google Analytics"
description: "Add GA tracking to your website"
---

Connect Google Analytics to track visitor behavior and website performance.

## Connecting Google Analytics

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **Integrations** section
4. Find **Google Analytics**
5. Enter your Measurement ID
6. Save

## Finding Your Measurement ID

1. Log into analytics.google.com
2. Go to Admin → Data Streams
3. Select your web stream
4. Copy Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXX)

## After Connecting

Google Analytics will:

- Track page views
- Monitor visitor behavior
- Collect demographic data
- Report in GA dashboard

## Verifying Connection

1. Visit your published website
2. Open GA Real-time reports
3. Check for active users

## Tips

- Use GA4 (not Universal Analytics)
- Set up goals and conversions
- Review reports regularly
- Combine with GTM for advanced tracking
