The General section in Website Settings contains basic website configuration.

## What's Included

### Logo & Favicon

- Full logo upload
- Favicon upload
- Crop tools

### Social Links

- Facebook
- Instagram
- X (Twitter)
- LinkedIn
- YouTube
- TikTok
- Discord
- WhatsApp
- Support email
- Business address

### Widgets

- Cookie consent banner
- Age gate verification

### Danger Zone

- Delete website option

## Tips

- Complete logo setup first
- Add relevant social links
- Enable widgets if needed
- Use danger zone carefully
