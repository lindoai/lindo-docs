---
title: "What is Sitemap"
description: "Auto-generated sitemap for search engines"
---

A sitemap is an XML file that lists all pages on your website for search engines.

## Overview

Lindo.ai automatically generates and maintains your sitemap. It updates whenever you:
- Add new pages
- Publish or unpublish pages
- Delete pages

## Sitemap Location

Your sitemap is available at:
```
https://yoursite.com/sitemap.xml
```

## What's Included

The sitemap contains:
- All published pages
- Blog posts
- Product pages
- Last modified dates
- Page priority hints

## Benefits

### Search Engine Discovery
- Helps search engines find all your pages
- Speeds up indexing of new content
- Ensures no pages are missed

### SEO
- Improves crawl efficiency
- Signals page importance
- Provides update frequency hints

## Submitting Your Sitemap

Submit to search engines:
1. Google Search Console
2. Bing Webmaster Tools
3. Other search engine tools
