Connect HubSpot Chat to engage visitors and capture leads on your website.

## What is HubSpot Chat?

HubSpot Chat provides:

- Live chat widget
- Chatbot flows
- Lead capture
- CRM integration

## Connecting HubSpot

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **Integrations** section
4. Find **HubSpot Chat**
5. Enter your HubSpot Portal ID
6. Save

## Finding Your Portal ID

1. Log into app.hubspot.com
2. Click your account name
3. Find Account ID (Portal ID)
4. Copy the number

## After Connecting

HubSpot chat widget appears on your website. Features include:

- Live conversations
- Chatbot automation
- Lead capture forms
- CRM sync

## Tips

- Set up chatbot flows
- Configure targeting rules
- Train your team
- Review chat analytics
