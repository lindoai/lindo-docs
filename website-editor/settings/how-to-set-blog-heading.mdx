---

Set the main heading that appears on your blog listing page.

## Setting the Heading

1. Go to **Settings** → **SEO**
2. Find the **Blog Config** section
3. Locate the **Blog Heading** field
4. Enter your heading
5. Save changes

## Examples

Good headings:
- "Our Blog"
- "Latest News"
- "Insights & Articles"
- "The [Company] Blog"
- "Resources"

## Best Practices

### Keep It Clear
- Describe what visitors will find
- Match your brand voice
- Be concise

### SEO Considerations
- Include relevant keywords naturally
- Don't keyword stuff
- Make it readable

### Consistency
- Match your site's tone
- Align with navigation labels
- Be consistent across pages
