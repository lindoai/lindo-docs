Connect your own domain name to your Lindo.ai website.

## Overview

A custom domain lets you use your own domain (like `example.com`) instead of the default subdomain.

## Benefits

### Professional Branding
- Your own domain looks more professional
- Builds trust with visitors
- Matches your business identity

### SEO
- Better for search engine optimization
- Consistent branding in search results
- Easier to remember and share

### Ownership
- You own the domain
- Can move it if needed
- Full control over DNS

## Requirements

- Pro plan or higher
- Domain registered with a registrar
- Access to DNS settings

## How It Works

1. Add your domain in Lindo.ai settings
2. Configure DNS records at your registrar
3. Wait for DNS propagation
4. SSL certificate is automatically provisioned

## Connection Options

### Option A: Root Domain
Connect `example.com` directly.

### Option B: Subdomain
Connect `www.example.com` or `app.example.com`.
