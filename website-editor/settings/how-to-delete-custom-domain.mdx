---
title: "How to Delete Custom Domain"
description: "Removing a custom domain from your website"
---

Remove a custom domain connection from your website.

## Deleting the Domain

1. Go to **Settings** → **Domains**
2. Find your custom domain
3. Click the **Delete** or trash icon
4. Confirm the deletion

## What Happens

When you delete a custom domain:
- The domain is disconnected from your website
- Your website remains accessible via its subdomain
- DNS records at your registrar become inactive
- SSL certificate is removed

## After Deletion

Your website will still be available at:
- `yoursite.lindo.ai` (or your workspace subdomain)

You can:
- Add a different custom domain
- Re-add the same domain later
- Continue using the default subdomain

## Cleaning Up DNS

After removing the domain from Lindo.ai, you may want to:
1. Remove the CNAME record from your registrar
2. Remove the TXT verification record
3. Point your domain elsewhere if needed
