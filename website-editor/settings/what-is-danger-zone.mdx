---
title: "Understanding Danger Zone"
description: "Website deletion and destructive actions"
---

The danger zone contains destructive actions that cannot be undone.

## Location

Find the danger zone at the bottom of **Settings** → **General**.

## Available Actions

### Delete Website
Permanently removes the website and all its content.

## Before Deleting

Consider:
- **Export first** - Download your website as a backup
- **Check dependencies** - Ensure no critical links point to this site
- **Inform stakeholders** - Let team members know
- **Verify intent** - Make sure you're deleting the right website

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete a website:
- All pages and content
- All blog posts
- All products
- Analytics data
- Custom domain configuration
- Team member access

## What's NOT Deleted

- Your workspace
- Other websites
- Your account
- Billing history

## Recovery

Deleted websites cannot be recovered. There is no undo.
