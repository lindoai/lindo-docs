title: "What is Website Team Management"
description: "Collaborator overview for website editing"
---

Team management lets you invite others to collaborate on your website.

## Overview

You can invite team members to help edit and manage individual websites. This is separate from workspace-level team members.

## Team Roles

Website team members can:
- Edit pages and content
- Manage products
- View analytics
- Access settings (based on role)

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Enter the email address
3. Select their role
4. Send the invitation

## Invitation Process

1. Team member receives email
2. They click the invitation link
3. They create an account (if needed)
4. They gain access to the website

## Managing Team

From the Team settings, you can:
- View all team members
- See invitation status
- Resend invitations
- Revoke access

## Website Team vs Workspace Team

| Website Team | Workspace Team |
|--------------|----------------|
| Access to one website | Access to entire workspace |
| Set per website | Set at workspace level |
| Limited scope | Broader permissions |
