---
title: "What is Favicon"
description: "The browser tab icon for your website"
---

The favicon is the small icon that appears in browser tabs and bookmarks.

## Where It Appears

- Browser tabs
- Bookmarks bar
- Browser history
- Mobile home screen shortcuts

## Recommended Specifications

| Property | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| Size | 32x32 pixels |
| Format | PNG, ICO, or SVG |
| Shape | Square |
| Background | Transparent or solid |

## Best Practices

### Design
- Keep it extremely simple
- Use recognizable brand element
- Test at small sizes
- Ensure visibility on different backgrounds

### Common Approaches
- First letter of brand name
- Simplified logo mark
- Brand icon or symbol
- Distinctive shape

### What to Avoid
- Full logos (too detailed)
- Text (unreadable at small size)
- Complex images
- Low contrast colors

## Favicon vs Logo

| Favicon | Logo |
|---------|------|
| 32x32px | 200x50px |
| Square | Horizontal |
| Browser tab | Website header |
| Simplified | Full brand |
