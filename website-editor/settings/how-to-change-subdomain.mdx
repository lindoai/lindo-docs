Change your website's subdomain to customize its default URL.

## What is a Subdomain?

Your website's subdomain is the default address:

```
yoursubdomain.lindo.site
```

This is the URL visitors use before you connect a custom domain.

## Changing Your Subdomain

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **Domains** section
4. Find the **Subdomain** field
5. Enter your new subdomain
6. Click **Save**

## Subdomain Rules

Valid subdomains:

- Use lowercase letters (a-z)
- Use numbers (0-9)
- Use hyphens (-)
- Don't start or end with hyphen
- Keep it short and memorable

### Examples

Good subdomains:
- `mycompany`
- `acme-corp`
- `studio123`

Invalid subdomains:
- `-mysite` (starts with hyphen)
- `my site` (contains space)
- `MyCompany` (uppercase)

## After Changing

When you change your subdomain:

- Old URL stops working immediately
- New URL becomes active
- Update any links to your site
- Inform visitors of the change

## Tips

- Choose a subdomain that matches your brand
- Keep it short and easy to type
- Avoid numbers if possible
- Consider SEO implications

## Custom Domain Alternative

For a more professional URL, consider connecting a custom domain instead:

```
www.yourcompany.com
```

See "How to Connect Custom Domain" for setup instructions.
