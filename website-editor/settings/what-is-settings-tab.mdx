The Settings tab provides comprehensive configuration for your website.

## Sections

### General

Logo, social links, widgets, danger zone.

### Domains

Subdomain and custom domain setup.

### Theme

Typography, colors, buttons, links.

### Integrations

Third-party service connections.

### SEO

Sitemap, robots.txt, schema, blog config.

### Team

Collaborator management.

### Workflows

Background automation.

## Tips

- Configure General first
- Set up domain for professional presence
- Connect analytics early
- Review all sections
