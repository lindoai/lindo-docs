---
title: "How to Set Business Address"
description: "Adding your physical address to your website"
---

Add your business address for local SEO and customer trust.

## Setting the Address

1. Go to **Settings** → **General**
2. Find the **Business Address** field
3. Enter your full address
4. Save changes

## Address Format

Include:
- Street address
- City, State/Province
- Postal/ZIP code
- Country

Example:
```
123 Main Street
San Francisco, CA 94102
United States
```

## Where It's Used

Your address may appear in:
- Website footer (if your theme includes it)
- Schema markup for local SEO
- Contact pages

## Benefits

### Local SEO
Search engines use your address for local search results.

### Customer Trust
Physical addresses build credibility with visitors.

### Legal Requirements
Some businesses are required to display their address.
