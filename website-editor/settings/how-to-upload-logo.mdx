Upload your logo to display it on your website header and other areas.

## Uploading Logo

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **General** section
4. Find **Full Logo** area
5. Click to upload
6. Select your logo file
7. Crop if needed
8. Save

## Logo Requirements

- **Format**: PNG, JPG, or SVG
- **Recommended size**: 200x50px
- **Max file size**: 5MB
- **Transparent background**: Recommended for PNG

## Where Logo Appears

Your logo displays in:

- Website header
- Footer (if configured)
- Browser tab (favicon is separate)

## Tips

- Use high-quality image
- Ensure readable at small sizes
- Test on light and dark backgrounds
- Keep file size small for performance
