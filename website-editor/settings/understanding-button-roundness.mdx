---
title: "Understanding Button Roundness"
description: "Corner radius options for buttons"
---

Button roundness controls the corner radius of buttons throughout your website.

## Roundness Options

| Option | Description | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| None | Square corners | Corporate, minimal |
| Small | Slightly rounded | Professional, clean |
| Medium | Moderately rounded | Friendly, modern |
| Full | Pill-shaped | Playful, soft |

## Visual Impact

### Square (None)
- Sharp, professional look
- Works well with angular designs
- Corporate and serious tone

### Slightly Rounded (Small)
- Softens the design
- Most versatile option
- Works with most styles

### Moderately Rounded (Medium)
- Friendly and approachable
- Modern web standard
- Good balance

### Pill-Shaped (Full)
- Playful and soft
- Stands out visually
- Works well for CTAs

## Consistency

Button roundness applies to:
- Primary buttons
- Secondary buttons
- Form submit buttons
- Navigation buttons

## Design Considerations

- Match your brand personality
- Consider your target audience
- Maintain consistency across pages
- Test with different button sizes
