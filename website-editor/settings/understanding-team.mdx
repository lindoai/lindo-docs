---
title: "Understanding Team Section"
description: "Website collaborator management"
---

The Team section in Website Settings manages who can access and edit your website.

## What's Included

### Team Members

- List of collaborators
- Their roles
- Invitation status

### Actions

- Invite new members
- Resend invitations
- Revoke access

## Roles

- **Admin**: Full access
- **Editor**: Can edit content
- **Viewer**: Read-only

## Tips

- Use minimum necessary permissions
- Review access regularly
- Remove inactive members
- Communicate role expectations
