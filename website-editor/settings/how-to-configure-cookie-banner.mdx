title: "How to Configure Cookie Banner"
description: "Setting policy URL, text, and button for cookie consent"
---

Customize your cookie consent banner to match your brand and requirements.

## Accessing Configuration

1. Go to **Settings** → **General**
2. Find the **Cookie Consent** section
3. Enable the cookie consent toggle
4. Configure the options

## Configuration Options

### Privacy Policy URL
Link to your privacy policy page:
- Enter the full URL
- Or use a relative path (e.g., /privacy)

### Banner Text
Customize the message:
- Explain what cookies you use
- Keep it concise
- Be transparent

### Button Text
Customize the accept button:
- Default: "Accept"
- Alternatives: "I Agree", "Got it", "OK"

## Example Configuration

```
Policy URL: /privacy
Text: We use cookies to improve your experience. By continuing, you agree to our cookie policy.
Button: Accept Cookies
```

## Best Practices

- Link to an actual privacy policy
- Be clear about cookie usage
- Keep the message short
- Make the button action clear
