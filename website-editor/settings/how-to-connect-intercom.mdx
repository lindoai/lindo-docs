description: "Add Intercom messenger to your website"
---

Connect Intercom to provide customer messaging and support on your website.

## What is Intercom?

Intercom provides:

- Customer messenger
- Live chat
- Help center
- Product tours

## Connecting Intercom

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **Integrations** section
4. Find **Intercom**
5. Enter your Intercom App ID
6. Save

## Finding Your App ID

1. Log into app.intercom.com
2. Go to Settings → Installation
3. Find your App ID
4. Copy the ID

## After Connecting

Intercom messenger appears on your website. Features include:

- Live chat
- Help articles
- Automated messages
- User tracking

## Tips

- Set up welcome messages
- Create help articles
- Configure business hours
- Use automation wisely
