---
title: "What is Age Gate Widget"
description: "Age verification popup for your website"
---

The age gate widget displays an age verification popup before visitors can access your website.

## Overview

Some websites need to verify visitor age before showing content. The age gate widget provides this functionality.

## When to Use

Age gates are commonly used for:
- Alcohol-related websites
- Tobacco products
- Adult content
- Age-restricted services
- Gaming/gambling sites

## How It Works

1. Visitor arrives at your website
2. Age gate popup appears
3. Visitor confirms their age
4. If confirmed, they can access the site
5. If declined, they're blocked or redirected

## Configuration Options

You can customize:
- Minimum age requirement
- Verification message
- Button text
- What happens on decline

## Legal Considerations

Age gates provide a layer of compliance but may not satisfy all legal requirements. Consult legal advice for your specific industry and region.
