Upload a favicon to display your icon in browser tabs and bookmarks.

## Uploading Favicon

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Go to **General** section
4. Find **Favicon** area
5. Click to upload
6. Select your icon file
7. Save

## Favicon Requirements

- **Format**: PNG, ICO, or SVG
- **Size**: 32x32px (square)
- **Max file size**: 1MB

## Where Favicon Appears

Your favicon shows in:

- Browser tabs
- Bookmarks
- Browser history
- Mobile home screen icons

## Tips

- Use a simple, recognizable icon
- Test at small sizes
- Consider your logo mark
- Keep it square
