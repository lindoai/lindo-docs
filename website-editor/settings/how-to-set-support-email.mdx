Add a contact email for visitors to reach you.

## Setting the Email

1. Go to **Settings** → **General**
2. Find the **Support Email** field
3. Enter your email address
4. Save changes

## Best Practices

### Use a Professional Email
- Use your domain email (contact@yourdomain.com)
- Avoid personal email addresses
- Consider a dedicated support email

### Monitor the Inbox
- Check regularly for inquiries
- Set up notifications
- Consider auto-responders

## Where It's Used

Your support email may appear in:
- Contact forms
- Footer contact information
- Schema markup

## Multiple Contacts

If you need different emails for different purposes:
- Use the support email for general inquiries
- Add specific emails to contact forms
- Include department emails on relevant pages
