Connect your own domain to your Lindo.ai website.

## Prerequisites

- Pro plan or higher
- Access to your domain's DNS settings
- Domain registered with a domain registrar

## Adding Your Domain

1. Go to **Settings** → **Domains**
2. Find the **Custom Domain** section
3. Enter your domain (e.g., example.com or www.example.com)
4. Click **Add Domain**

## Connection Options

### Option A: Root Domain
Connect your root domain (example.com):
- Add a CNAME record pointing to Lindo.ai
- Add a TXT record for verification

### Option B: Subdomain
Connect a subdomain (www.example.com or app.example.com):
- Add a CNAME record for the subdomain
- Add a TXT record for verification

## DNS Records

After adding your domain, you'll see the required DNS records:

| Type | Name | Value |
|------|------|-------|
| CNAME | @ or www | [provided value] |
| TXT | @ | [verification code] |

## Adding DNS Records

1. Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.)
2. Find DNS settings
3. Add the CNAME record
4. Add the TXT record
5. Save changes

## Verification

1. Return to Lindo.ai settings
2. Click **Check Status**
3. Wait for DNS propagation (can take up to 48 hours)
4. Status shows "Connected" when complete

## SSL Certificate

SSL is automatically provisioned once your domain is connected. Your site will be accessible via HTTPS.
