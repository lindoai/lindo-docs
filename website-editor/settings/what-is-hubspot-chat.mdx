title: "What is HubSpot Chat"
description: "CRM chat integration for your website"
---

HubSpot Chat connects your website conversations directly to HubSpot CRM.

## Overview

HubSpot Chat provides:
- Live chat widget
- Chatbot flows
- CRM integration
- Contact management

## Features

### Live Chat
- Real-time conversations
- Team routing
- Availability scheduling

### Chatbots
- Automated qualification
- Meeting booking
- FAQ responses

### CRM Integration
- Conversations logged to contacts
- Deal creation
- Contact enrichment

### Reporting
- Chat analytics
- Team performance
- Response times

## Benefits

- **Unified data** - All conversations in CRM
- **Lead capture** - Automatic contact creation
- **Sales enablement** - Route to sales reps
- **Support** - Help desk integration

## Setup

1. Have a HubSpot account
2. Get your Hub ID
3. Add the ID to Lindo.ai
4. Chat widget appears on your site
