---
title: "How to Check Website DNS Status"
description: "Verifying your domain connection status"
---

Verify that your custom domain is properly connected.

## Checking Status

1. Go to **Settings** → **Domains**
2. Find your custom domain
3. Look at the status indicator
4. Click **Check Status** to refresh

## Status Indicators

### Connected
Your domain is properly configured and working. Your website is accessible at your custom domain.

### Not Connected
DNS records haven't propagated yet or are incorrectly configured.

### Pending
DNS changes are being processed. This can take up to 48 hours.

## Troubleshooting

If status shows "Not Connected":

1. Verify DNS records are correct
2. Check for typos in record values
3. Ensure records are saved at your registrar
4. Wait for propagation (up to 48 hours)
5. Try checking status again

## DNS Propagation

DNS changes don't happen instantly. Propagation time depends on:
- Your domain registrar
- TTL (Time To Live) settings
- Global DNS cache refresh

Typical propagation: 15 minutes to 48 hours.
