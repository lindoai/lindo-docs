---
title: "How to Send Invoice to Customer"
description: "Create and send invoices to website customers"
---

Send invoices to your website customers for products, services, or custom charges.

## Prerequisites

Before sending invoices:

1. Stripe must be connected (Settings → Integrations)
2. Customer must exist in CRM
3. Customer must have valid email

## Sending an Invoice

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Select the customer
4. Go to **Billing** tab
5. Click **Create Invoice**

## Invoice Details

### Service Name

What you're charging for:

```
Website Design Services
```

```
Monthly Maintenance
```

### Price

The amount to charge:

- Enter numeric value
- Select currency (USD, EUR, etc.)

### Frequency

How often to charge:

- **One-time**: Single payment
- **Monthly**: Recurring monthly
- **Yearly**: Recurring annually
- **Weekly**: Recurring weekly

## Sending the Invoice

1. Review invoice details
2. Click **Send Invoice**
3. Customer receives email with payment link
4. Invoice appears in billing history

## Invoice Status

Track invoice status:

- **Open**: Sent, awaiting payment
- **Paid**: Payment received
- **Refunded**: Payment returned

## Managing Invoices

After sending:

- **Copy Link**: Get payment URL
- **Download**: Save as PDF
- **Refund**: Return payment

## Tips

- Use clear service names
- Set appropriate currency
- Choose correct frequency
- Follow up on unpaid invoices
