Get the payment link to share with your customer.

## Copying the Link

1. Go to the **CRM** tab
2. Find the invoice
3. Click the **Copy** icon on the invoice row
4. The payment link is copied to your clipboard

## Using the Link

Send the link to your customer via:
- Email
- Chat/messaging
- Your client portal

When the customer clicks the link:
- They see the invoice details
- They can pay with card
- Payment is processed through Stripe

## Link Validity

- Links remain valid until the invoice is paid
- Paid invoices show a confirmation page
- Refunded invoices show refund status

## Best Practices

- Send links promptly after creating invoices
- Include context in your message
- Follow up on unpaid invoices
