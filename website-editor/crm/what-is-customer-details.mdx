---
title: "What is Customer Details Modal"
description: "Overview of the customer information modal in Website CRM"
---

The Customer Details modal shows complete information about a website customer, including their profile, billing history, and subscriptions.

## Accessing Customer Details

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Click on any customer in the list
4. The details modal opens

## Modal Sections

### Info Tab

Customer profile information:

- **Name**: Customer's full name
- **Email**: Contact email address
- **Company**: Business name (optional)
- **Notes**: Your internal notes

### Billing Tab

Financial information:

- **Billing Summary**: Total billed, outstanding, paid
- **Invoice History**: All invoices sent to this customer
- **Invoice Actions**: Copy link, download, refund

### Subscriptions Tab

Recurring billing:

- **Active Subscriptions**: Current recurring charges
- **Subscription Details**: Amount, frequency, status
- **Cancel Option**: Stop recurring billing

## Customer Actions

From the details modal, you can:

- Update customer information
- Send new invoices
- View payment history
- Manage subscriptions
- Add notes

## Billing Summary

The summary shows:

- **Total Billed**: All invoices ever sent
- **Outstanding**: Unpaid amounts
- **Paid**: Completed payments

## Tips

- Keep customer notes updated
- Review billing history regularly
- Monitor subscription status
- Use notes for communication history
