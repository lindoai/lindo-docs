---
title: "How to Cancel a Customer Subscription"
description: "Stop recurring billing for a customer"
---

Cancel a customer's subscription to stop future recurring charges.

## Canceling a Subscription

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Select the customer
4. Go to **Subscriptions** tab
5. Find the subscription
6. Click **Cancel**
7. Confirm cancellation

## What Happens

When you cancel:

- Future charges stop
- Current period may continue
- Customer loses access at period end
- Cannot be undone

## Before Canceling

Consider:

- Has customer requested this?
- Is there an alternative?
- Should you offer a discount?

## After Cancellation

- Subscription shows as canceled
- No more charges
- Customer notified (if configured)

## Tips

- Communicate with customer first
- Document reason for cancellation
- Consider win-back strategies
- Track cancellation reasons
