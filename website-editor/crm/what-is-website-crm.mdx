---
title: "What is Website CRM"
description: "Customer management for website visitors and buyers"
---

Website CRM helps you manage customers who interact with your website.

## Key Difference

- **Workspace CRM**: For agency clients
- **Website CRM**: For website customers

## Features

### Customers Tab

- Customer list
- Contact information
- Search and filter

### Billing Tab

- All invoices
- Payment status
- Invoice actions

## What You Can Do

- Add customers
- Send invoices
- Manage subscriptions
- Track billing history

## Tips

- Keep customer info updated
- Monitor billing status
- Follow up on payments
- Use for customer relationships
