title: "How to View Customer Subscriptions"
description: "Check active subscriptions for a customer"
---

View all active subscriptions for a customer in your Website CRM.

## Accessing Subscriptions

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Select the customer
4. Go to **Subscriptions** tab
5. View active subscriptions

## What You See

The subscriptions tab shows:

- Active subscriptions
- Subscription amount
- Billing frequency
- Start date
- Status

## Subscription Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Active | Currently billing |
| Canceled | Stopped billing |
| Past Due | Payment failed |

## Managing Subscriptions

From this view you can:

- See subscription details
- Cancel subscriptions
- View payment history

## Tips

- Monitor active subscriptions
- Follow up on past due
- Communicate before canceling
- Track recurring revenue
