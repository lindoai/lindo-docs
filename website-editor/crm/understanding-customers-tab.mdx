---
title: "Understanding Customers Tab"
description: "Customer list and management in Website CRM"
---

The Customers tab in Website CRM shows all customers associated with your website.

## What You See

The customers tab displays:

- Customer list
- Name and email
- Company (if provided)
- Quick actions

## Customer Information

Each customer entry shows:

- Full name
- Email address
- Company name
- Creation date

## Actions Available

From the customers tab:

- Search customers
- Add new customer
- Click to view details
- Access billing info

## Tips

- Keep customer list organized
- Update information regularly
- Use search for large lists
- Review customer details periodically
