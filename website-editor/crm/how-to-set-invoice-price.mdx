Configure the price when creating customer invoices.

## Setting the Price

When creating an invoice:

1. Open customer details
2. Go to the Billing tab
3. Click **Create Invoice**
4. Enter the **Price** field
5. Select the currency
6. Complete other details
7. Send the invoice

## Price Format

- Enter the amount in your selected currency
- Use decimal points for cents (e.g., 99.99)
- Don't include currency symbols

## Currency Selection

Choose from available currencies:
- USD (US Dollar)
- EUR (Euro)
- GBP (British Pound)
- And others based on your Stripe account

## Pricing Tips

### One-Time Projects
- Quote the full project price
- Consider milestone payments for large projects

### Recurring Services
- Set monthly/yearly rate
- Be consistent with pricing

### Discounts
- Apply discounts before creating invoice
- Note any discounts in the service name
