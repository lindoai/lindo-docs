description: "Find specific customers in your Website CRM"
---

Search through your customer list to find specific people quickly.

## Searching Customers

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Find the search input
4. Type customer name or email
5. Results filter automatically

## Search By

You can search by:

- Customer name
- Email address
- Company name

## Tips

- Use partial matches
- Search is case-insensitive
- Clear search to see all
- Combine with filters if available
