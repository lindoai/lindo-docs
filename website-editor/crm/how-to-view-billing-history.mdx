---
title: "How to View Customer Billing History"
description: "Check past invoices for a website customer"
---

View the complete billing history for any customer in your Website CRM.

## Accessing Billing History

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Select the customer
4. Go to **Billing** tab
5. View invoice history

## What You See

The billing history shows:

- All invoices sent
- Invoice amounts
- Payment status
- Invoice dates

## Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Open | Awaiting payment |
| Paid | Payment received |
| Refunded | Payment returned |

## Actions Available

From the history:

- View invoice details
- Copy payment link
- Download invoice
- Process refund

## Tips

- Review before sending new invoices
- Follow up on open invoices
- Track payment patterns
- Keep records organized
