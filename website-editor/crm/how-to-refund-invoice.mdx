---
title: "How to Refund a Customer Invoice"
description: "Process a refund for a paid invoice"
---

Refund a customer's payment when needed for returns or disputes.

## Processing a Refund

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Select the customer
4. Go to **Billing** tab
5. Find the paid invoice
6. Click **Refund**
7. Confirm the refund

## Requirements

To process a refund:

- Invoice must be paid
- Stripe must be connected
- Funds must be available

## What Happens

When you refund:

- Payment is returned to customer
- Invoice status changes to "Refunded"
- Stripe processes the return
- Customer receives notification

## Refund Timeline

Refunds typically take:

- 5-10 business days
- Depends on customer's bank
- Stripe handles processing

## Tips

- Communicate with customer
- Document refund reason
- Consider partial refunds
- Track refund patterns
