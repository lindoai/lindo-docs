---
title: "How to View Invoice Details"
description: "Checking invoice number, amount, and status"
---

Access detailed information about any invoice.

## Viewing Details

1. Go to the **CRM** tab
2. Find the invoice (in Billing tab or customer details)
3. Click on the invoice row
4. View the invoice details

## Invoice Information

Details include:
- Invoice number
- Customer name and email
- Service name
- Amount and currency
- Status
- Creation date
- Payment date (if paid)

## Available Actions

From invoice details:
- **Copy Link** - Get payment link for customer
- **Download** - Save as PDF
- **Refund** - Process refund (paid invoices only)

## Invoice Status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Open | Awaiting customer payment |
| Paid | Payment successfully processed |
| Refunded | Payment has been returned |
