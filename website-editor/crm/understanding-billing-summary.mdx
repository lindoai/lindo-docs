title: "Understanding Customer Billing Summary"
description: "Total billed, outstanding, and paid amounts"
---

The billing summary provides a quick financial overview for each customer.

## Summary Components

### Total Billed

Sum of all invoices ever sent to this customer.

### Outstanding

Amount from unpaid invoices.

### Paid

Total amount successfully collected.

## Where to Find It

1. Open CRM tab
2. Select a customer
3. Go to Billing tab
4. View summary at top

## Tips

- Monitor outstanding amounts
- Follow up on unpaid invoices
- Track customer value
- Use for financial planning
