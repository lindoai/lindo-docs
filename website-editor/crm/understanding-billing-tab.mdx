---
title: "Understanding Website Billing Tab"
description: "Invoice management in Website CRM"
---

The Billing tab in Website CRM shows all invoices for your website customers.

## What You See

The billing tab displays:

- All invoices
- Invoice status
- Amounts
- Dates

## Invoice Information

Each invoice shows:

- Invoice number
- Customer name
- Amount and currency
- Status (Open, Paid, Refunded)
- Date created

## Actions Available

From the billing tab:

- View invoice details
- Copy payment link
- Download invoice
- Process refunds

## Tips

- Monitor open invoices
- Follow up on unpaid
- Keep records organized
- Track payment patterns
