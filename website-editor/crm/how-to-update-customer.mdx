Update customer information to keep your records accurate.

## Updating Customer Info

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **CRM** tab
3. Select the customer
4. Edit the fields:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Company
   - Notes
5. Click **Save**

## Editable Fields

You can update:

- **Name**: Customer's full name
- **Email**: Contact email
- **Company**: Business name
- **Notes**: Your internal notes

## When to Update

Update customer info when:

- Contact details change
- Company name changes
- Adding notes from conversations
- Correcting errors

## Tips

- Keep information current
- Use notes for context
- Verify email addresses
- Document important interactions
