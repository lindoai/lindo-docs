---

Access comprehensive information about any customer.

## Opening Customer Details

1. Go to the **CRM** tab in the website editor
2. Find the customer in the list
3. Click on the customer row
4. The customer details modal opens

## Customer Information

The modal shows:

### Basic Info
- Customer name
- Email address
- Company (if provided)
- Notes

### Billing Summary
- Total billed amount
- Outstanding balance
- Total paid

### Billing History
- List of all invoices
- Invoice status (open, paid, refunded)
- Invoice amounts and dates

### Subscriptions
- Active subscriptions
- Subscription details
- Cancel option

## Actions Available

From the customer details modal:
- Update customer information
- Create new invoices
- View invoice details
- Cancel subscriptions
