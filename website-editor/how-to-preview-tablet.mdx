---
title: "How to Preview on Tablet"
description: "Viewing tablet layout"
---

View your website as it appears on tablet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Device Buttons">
    Look for device icons in preview header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Tablet">
    Click the tablet icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Tablet Layout">
    Preview shows medium-width tablet view.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## What to Check

- Grid layouts
- Image sizing
- Navigation
- Touch targets

## Tips

- Check between desktop and mobile
- Verify grids adapt
- Test navigation
