---
title: "How to Set Product Price"
description: "Configuring pricing"
---

Set the price for your product.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Product Settings">
    Click the product to open settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Price Field">
    Locate the price input.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Price">
    Type the price amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Currency">
    Choose currency (USD, EUR, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click save to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Use appropriate currency
- Consider market pricing
- Test checkout flow
