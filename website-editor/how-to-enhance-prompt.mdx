---
title: "How to Enhance Your Prompt"
description: "Using the enhance button"
---

Let AI improve your prompt for better results.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write Your Prompt">
    Enter your basic request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Enhance Button">
    Look for the enhance/magic wand icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Enhance">
    AI processes your prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Enhanced Prompt">
    See the improved version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or Edit">
    Send as-is or modify further.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Example

**Your prompt:**
> "Add features"

**Enhanced:**
> "Add a features section with 6 cards in a responsive grid, each containing an icon, title, and description showcasing key product benefits"

## Tips

- Use for vague requests
- Review before sending
- Learn from enhancements
