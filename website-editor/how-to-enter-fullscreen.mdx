---
title: "How to Enter Fullscreen Preview"
description: "Expanding preview area"
---

Maximize the preview for detailed viewing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Fullscreen Button">
    Look for the expand icon in preview header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to Enter">
    Click the button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview Expands">
    Full-screen preview mode activates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Tips

- Great for review
- Use for presentations
- Press Escape to exit
