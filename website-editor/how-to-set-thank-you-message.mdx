---
title: "How to Set Thank You Message"
description: "Post-purchase message"
---

Configure the message shown after purchase.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Product Settings">
    Click the product to open settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Thank You Field">
    Locate the thank you message area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Message">
    Write your post-purchase message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click save to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Good Messages

- Thank the customer
- Confirm what they bought
- Provide next steps
- Include contact info

## Example

> "Thank you for your purchase! Your download link is below. If you have any questions, contact us at support@example.com"

## Tips

- Be warm and helpful
- Include next steps
- Provide support contact
