---
title: "Understanding Website Referrers"
description: "Traffic sources for your website"
---

Referrers show where your website traffic comes from.

## Traffic Sources

Common referrer types:

- **Direct**: Typed URL or bookmarks
- **Search**: Google, Bing, etc.
- **Social**: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn
- **Other Sites**: Links from other websites

## What It Shows

For each referrer:

- Source name/URL
- Number of visitors
- Percentage of total

## Why It Matters

Referrer data helps you:

- Understand traffic sources
- Measure marketing effectiveness
- Identify partnership opportunities
- Focus promotion efforts

## Tips

- Invest in top-performing sources
- Improve underperforming channels
- Track campaign referrers
- Build relationships with referring sites
