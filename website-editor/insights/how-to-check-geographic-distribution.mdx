---
title: "How to Check Geographic Distribution"
description: "Understanding where your visitors are located"
---

See which countries your website visitors come from.

## Viewing Geographic Data

1. Go to the **Insights** tab
2. Find the **Top Countries** section
3. See visitor distribution by country

## Understanding the Data

Each entry shows:
- Country name
- Number of visitors
- Percentage of total traffic

## Using This Data

### Content Localization
If you have significant traffic from non-English countries:
- Consider translating content
- Adjust currency displays
- Localize date formats

### Marketing Focus
- Target ads to high-traffic regions
- Adjust posting times for time zones
- Create region-specific content

### Business Decisions
- Identify expansion opportunities
- Understand your market
- Plan international growth

## Common Patterns

| Pattern | Consideration |
|---------|---------------|
| Single country dominates | Local business or targeted marketing |
| Diverse distribution | Global appeal |
| Unexpected countries | Check for bot traffic or VPNs |
