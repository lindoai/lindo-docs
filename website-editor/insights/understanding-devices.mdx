The Devices section shows what types of devices visitors use to access your website.

## Device Categories

- **Desktop**: Computers and laptops
- **Mobile**: Smartphones
- **Tablet**: iPad and similar devices
- **Bot**: Automated crawlers

## What It Shows

For each device type:

- Number of visitors
- Percentage of total
- Visual breakdown

## Why It Matters

Device data helps you:

- Prioritize responsive design
- Optimize for primary devices
- Understand your audience
- Plan mobile strategy

## Tips

- If mobile is high, ensure mobile-friendly design
- Test on popular device types
- Consider device-specific features
- Monitor trends over time
