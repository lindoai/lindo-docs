---
title: "Understanding Website Top Countries"
description: "Geographic distribution of your visitors"
---

Top Countries shows where your website visitors are located geographically.

## What It Shows

The countries section displays:

- Country name
- Number of visitors
- Percentage of total

## Why It Matters

Geographic data helps you:

- Understand your audience
- Target marketing efforts
- Consider localization
- Plan content strategy

## Using the Data

If visitors are from specific regions:

- Consider local language content
- Adjust for time zones
- Target local SEO
- Customize offerings

## Tips

- Monitor for unexpected traffic
- Consider regional marketing
- Localize for top countries
- Track changes over time
