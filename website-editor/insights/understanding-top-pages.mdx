---
title: "Understanding Top Pages"
description: "Most visited pages on your website"
---

Top Pages shows which pages on your website receive the most traffic.

## What It Shows

The top pages section displays:

- Page URL/path
- Number of views
- Percentage of total traffic

## How to Use

Use top pages data to:

- Identify popular content
- Focus optimization efforts
- Understand visitor interests
- Plan content strategy

## Interpreting Data

High-traffic pages:

- Are your most valuable content
- Should be optimized first
- May need more attention

Low-traffic pages:

- May need promotion
- Could be improved
- Consider their purpose

## Tips

- Monitor changes over time
- Compare to previous periods
- Optimize top performers
- Improve underperforming pages
