---
title: "How to Identify Top Pages"
description: "Finding your best performing pages"
---

Discover which pages on your website get the most traffic.

## Viewing Top Pages

1. Go to the **Insights** tab
2. Find the **Top Pages** section
3. Pages are ranked by page views

## Understanding the List

Each entry shows:
- Page path (e.g., /about, /pricing)
- Number of page views
- Percentage of total traffic

## Using This Data

### Content Strategy
- Create more content like your top pages
- Improve underperforming pages
- Identify what resonates with visitors

### Optimization
- Focus optimization efforts on high-traffic pages
- Ensure top pages have clear CTAs
- Check mobile experience on popular pages

### Navigation
- Make top pages easy to find
- Add internal links to popular content
- Consider featuring in navigation

## Common Patterns

| Pattern | What It Means |
|---------|---------------|
| Homepage dominates | Visitors may not explore further |
| Blog posts rank high | Content marketing is working |
| Product pages rank high | Strong purchase intent |
| Even distribution | Good site navigation |
