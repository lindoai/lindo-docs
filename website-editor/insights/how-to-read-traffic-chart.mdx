Understand your website's traffic patterns using the traffic chart.

## Chart Overview

The traffic chart shows two metrics over time:
- **Page Views** - Total pages loaded
- **Visitors** - Unique visitors

## Reading the Chart

### X-Axis (Horizontal)
Shows the time period based on your selected date range:
- Daily view for short ranges
- Weekly aggregation for longer ranges

### Y-Axis (Vertical)
Shows the count of page views or visitors.

### Lines
- One line for page views
- One line for unique visitors
- Hover over points for exact values

## Interpreting Patterns

### Healthy Traffic
- Consistent or growing trend
- Page views higher than visitors (multiple pages per visit)
- Regular patterns (weekday vs weekend)

### Concerning Patterns
- Sudden drops (check for issues)
- Flat lines (no traffic)
- Page views equal to visitors (single page visits)

## Page Views vs Visitors

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| High page views, moderate visitors | Good engagement, multiple pages per visit |
| Equal page views and visitors | Single page visits, possible bounce |
| Spike in both | Successful campaign or viral content |

## Using the Data

- Identify your best performing days
- Spot trends over time
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Detect issues early
