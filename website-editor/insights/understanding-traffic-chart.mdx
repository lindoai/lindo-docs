The traffic chart visualizes your website's traffic patterns over time.

## Chart Elements

- **Blue line**: Page views
- **Green line**: Unique visitors
- **X-axis**: Time period
- **Y-axis**: Count

## Reading the Chart

- Higher lines = more traffic
- Upward trends = growth
- Spikes = traffic events
- Dips = lower activity

## Interpreting Data

### Page Views vs Visitors

- If page views >> visitors: Good engagement
- If similar: Low pages per visit
- Ratio indicates engagement

### Trends

- Consistent growth: Healthy
- Sudden spikes: Viral or campaigns
- Drops: Issues or seasonal

## Tips

- Compare time periods
- Note significant events
- Track campaign impact
- Monitor for anomalies
