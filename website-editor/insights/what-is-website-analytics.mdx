---
title: "What is Website Analytics"
description: "Overview of the Insights tab for website analytics"
---

Website Analytics (Insights tab) provides detailed traffic and visitor data for your individual website.

## Accessing Analytics

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Click **Insights** tab in the header
3. View your analytics dashboard

## Key Metrics

### Statistics Cards

At-a-glance metrics:

- **Visitors**: Unique visitors
- **Page Views**: Total page loads
- **Avg Response Time**: Server response speed
- **Pages**: Number of pages
- **Blogs**: Number of blog posts

### Traffic Chart

Visual representation of:

- Page views over time
- Visitors over time
- Trend comparison

## Analytics Sections

### Top Pages

Most visited pages on your website, showing:

- Page URL
- View count
- Percentage of total

### Operating Systems

Visitor OS breakdown:

- Windows
- macOS
- iOS
- Android
- Linux

### Browsers

Browser usage:

- Chrome
- Safari
- Firefox
- Edge
- Others

### Devices

Device categories:

- Desktop
- Mobile
- Tablet
- Bot

### Top Countries

Geographic distribution of visitors by country.

### Referrers

Traffic sources:

- Direct visits
- Search engines
- Social media
- Other websites

### UTM Campaigns

Marketing campaign tracking:

- Campaign name
- Source
- Medium
- Visitor count

## Date Range

Filter analytics by time period:

- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- Last 90 days
- Custom range

## Refreshing Data

Click the refresh button to update analytics with latest data.

## Tips

- Check analytics regularly
- Monitor traffic trends
- Identify top-performing pages
- Track campaign effectiveness
- Understand your audience
