UTM Campaigns shows traffic from your marketing campaigns with UTM parameters.

## What are UTM Parameters?

UTM parameters are tags added to URLs:

```
yoursite.com?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=summer_sale
```

## What It Shows

The UTM section displays:

- Campaign name
- Source
- Medium
- Visitor count

## Common UTM Parameters

| Parameter | Purpose | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| utm_source | Traffic source | facebook, google |
| utm_medium | Marketing medium | social, email, cpc |
| utm_campaign | Campaign name | summer_sale |

## Why It Matters

UTM tracking helps you:

- Measure campaign ROI
- Compare marketing channels
- Optimize ad spend
- Track specific promotions

## Tips

- Use consistent naming
- Tag all marketing links
- Review campaign performance
- Adjust based on data
