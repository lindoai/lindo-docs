---
title: "How to View All Products"
description: "Accessing products section"
---

See all products in your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pages Tab">
    Click "Pages" in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Products Section">
    Products appear alongside pages and blogs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Products">
    See all product cards.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Product List

Shows:
- Product images
- Product names
- Prices
- Status

## Tips

- Use search to filter
- Check prices
- Review status
