Configure what information to collect at checkout.

## Available Options

### Discount Codes

Allow customers to enter promo codes.

### Phone Number

Collect customer phone number.

### Shipping Address

Collect delivery address.

### Adjustable Quantity

Let customers change quantity.

## When to Enable

| Option | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| Discount codes | Promotions |
| Phone | Contact needed |
| Address | Physical products |
| Quantity | Multiple items |

## Tips

- Only collect what's needed
- Address for physical products
- Phone for important orders
