---
title: "How to Stop AI Generation"
description: "Canceling ongoing AI operations"
---

Cancel an AI operation in progress.

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI is Generating">
    You see the generation in progress.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Stop Button">
    Look for the stop/cancel button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Stop">
    Click to cancel the operation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generation Stops">
    AI stops processing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## When to Stop

- Going in wrong direction
- Taking too long
- Changed your mind
- Want to try different prompt

## What Happens

- Generation cancels
- Partial changes may remain
- Can undo if needed
- Try again with new prompt

## Tips

- Watch stream preview
- Stop early if wrong
- Undo partial changes
