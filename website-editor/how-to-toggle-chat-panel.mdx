---
title: "How to Toggle the Chat Panel"
description: "Show or hide the AI chat sidebar"
---

Toggle the chat panel to maximize your preview area or access AI editing features.

## Showing the Chat Panel

If the chat panel is hidden:

1. Look for the chat toggle icon in the header
2. Click to show the panel
3. Chat panel slides in from the left

## Hiding the Chat Panel

To hide the chat panel:

1. Click the chat toggle icon
2. Panel slides out
3. Preview area expands

## When to Hide

Hide the chat panel when:

- Reviewing your design
- Checking responsive layouts
- Presenting to clients
- Focusing on preview

## When to Show

Show the chat panel when:

- Making AI edits
- Switching agents
- Uploading reference images
- Sending prompts

## Tips

- Toggle based on your current task
- Use fullscreen for maximum preview
- Chat history is preserved when hidden
