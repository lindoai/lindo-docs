---
title: "How to Upload Reference Images"
description: "Attaching images to AI prompts"
---

Add images to help AI understand your request.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Image Button">
    Look for the image/attachment icon in chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to Upload">
    Click the button to open file picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Image">
    Choose an image file from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Image Attached">
    Image appears in your message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Text">
    Describe what you want with the image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Submit your message with image.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Use Cases

- Design inspiration
- Logo to include
- Style reference
- Layout example

## Tips

- Clear, high-quality images
- Describe what to do with it
- One image at a time
