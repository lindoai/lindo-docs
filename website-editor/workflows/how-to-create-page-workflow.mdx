---
title: "How to Create Page Workflow"
description: "Starting a background page creation workflow"
---

Create pages in the background while you continue working.

## Creating a Page Workflow

1. Go to the **Workflows** section (if available)
2. Click **Create Workflow**
3. Select **Page** as the workflow type
4. Enter your page prompt
5. Configure AI tools (language, mode, colors)
6. Click **Start Workflow**

## Workflow Options

### AI Tools
- **Language** - Content language
- **Mode** - Dark or light theme
- **Color Palette** - Custom colors

### Notifications
Enable email notifications to be alerted when the workflow completes.

## Monitoring Progress

1. Open the Workflows panel
2. Find your workflow in the list
3. Check the status:
   - **Running** - In progress
   - **Completed** - Finished successfully
   - **Error** - Something went wrong

## After Completion

When the workflow finishes:
- The page appears in your Pages list
- Review and make any adjustments
- Publish when ready
