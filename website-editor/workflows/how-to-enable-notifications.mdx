---
title: "How to Enable Workflow Email Notifications"
description: "Getting notified when workflows complete"
---

Receive email alerts when your workflows finish.

## Enabling Notifications

When creating a workflow:

1. Fill in the workflow details
2. Find the **Email Notification** toggle
3. Enable it
4. Start the workflow

## What You'll Receive

When the workflow completes, you'll get an email with:
- Workflow status (completed or error)
- Link to view the result
- Summary of what was created

## Benefits

- Continue working on other tasks
- No need to constantly check status
- Get alerted to errors immediately
- Know exactly when content is ready

## Notification Timing

Emails are sent when:
- Workflow completes successfully
- Workflow encounters an error
- Workflow times out (rare)
