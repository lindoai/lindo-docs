Blog Pipeline is a workflow feature that generates multiple blog posts automatically from a list of topics.

## How It Works

1. You provide a list of blog topics
2. The AI generates complete posts for each
3. Posts are created in the background
4. You receive notification when complete

## Benefits

- **Save Time**: Create many posts at once
- **Consistency**: Uniform quality and style
- **Efficiency**: Work on other tasks while generating
- **Scale**: Build content library quickly

## What Gets Created

For each topic, the AI generates:

- Blog post title
- Full article content
- Proper formatting
- SEO-friendly structure

## Credit Usage

Each blog post uses credits based on the Blog Writer agent cost (30 credits per post).

Plan your batch size according to available credits.

## Use Cases

- Building initial blog content
- Content marketing campaigns
- SEO content strategy
- Regular publishing schedule

## Workflow Status

Track your blog pipeline:

- **Running**: Currently generating posts
- **Completed**: All posts created
- **Error**: Some posts failed

## After Completion

Once the workflow completes:

1. Open Pages tab
2. Find new blog posts
3. Review and edit as needed
4. Publish when ready

## Tips

- Use specific, descriptive topics
- Review AI content before publishing
- Add personal touches
- Optimize images after generation
- Schedule publishing strategically
