---
title: "What is Fullscreen Preview"
description: "Expanded preview mode"
---

Fullscreen preview expands the preview area for better viewing.

## How to Enter

1. Click fullscreen icon in preview header
2. Preview expands to fill screen
3. Chat panel hides

## Benefits

- Larger preview area
- Better detail viewing
- Presentation mode
- Focus on design

## How to Exit

1. Click exit fullscreen icon
2. Or press Escape
3. Returns to normal view

## Tips

- Use for detailed review
- Good for presentations
- Exit to continue editing
- Combine with device preview
