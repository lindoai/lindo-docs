---
title: "What is Fullscreen Preview"
description: "Expanded preview mode"
---

Expand the preview to fill your screen.

## What It Does

- Hides chat panel
- Maximizes preview area
- Full-screen view
- Better detail view

## Uses

- Detailed review
- Client presentations
- Final checks
- Focus on design

## Entering Fullscreen

Click the fullscreen button in preview header.

## Exiting Fullscreen

Click the exit button or press Escape.

## Tips

- Use for final review
- Great for presentations
- Check all devices
