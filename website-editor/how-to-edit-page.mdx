Open a page for editing.

## Methods

### From Pages Tab

1. Go to Pages tab
2. Click the page card
3. Page opens in editor

### From Actions Menu

1. Hover over page card
2. Click "Edit" in actions

## What Opens

- Design tab activates
- Page loads in preview
- AI chat ready

## Tips

- Click anywhere on card
- Use search to find pages
- Edit with AI or manually
