Different types of content in your website.

## Page Types

### Pages

Standard website pages:
- Home page
- About page
- Services
- Contact
- Custom pages

### Blogs

Blog posts and articles:
- News
- Updates
- Articles
- Tutorials

### Products

E-commerce products:
- Physical products
- Digital products
- Services
- Subscriptions

## Differences

| Type | Purpose | Features |
|------|---------|----------|
| Pages | General content | Full design control |
| Blogs | Articles | Date, author, categories |
| Products | E-commerce | Price, checkout |

## Tips

- Use pages for static content
- Use blogs for updates
- Use products for sales
