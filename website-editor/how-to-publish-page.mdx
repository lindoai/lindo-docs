---
title: "How to Publish Page"
description: "Make your page visible to the public"
---

Publishing makes your page visible to the public. Until you publish, pages remain as drafts that only you can see.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your changes">
    Before publishing, check the preview to ensure everything looks correct. Verify that content is accurate, images load properly, links work, and the mobile view looks good.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Publish">
    Click the **Publish** button in the preview header toolbar. The button is on the right side next to the Save button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Page is live">
    Your page is now publicly accessible at its URL (e.g., yoursite.lindo.site/page-path). The status changes from "Draft" to "Published."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Draft vs Published

| State | Visibility |
|-------|------------|
| Draft | Only you can see |
| Published | Everyone can see |

## Save vs Publish

| Action | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Save | Saves changes as draft, not visible to public |
| Publish | Makes changes live immediately |

You can save drafts as many times as you want without affecting the live version. When you're ready, publish to push all changes live.

## Unpublishing a Page

To take a page offline:

1. Go to the **Pages** tab
2. Find the page you want to unpublish
3. Click the three-dot menu
4. Select **Unpublish**

The page becomes a draft again and is no longer accessible to visitors.

## Tips

- **Preview on all devices** — Check desktop, tablet, and mobile before publishing
- **Test all links** — Make sure navigation and buttons work correctly
- **Check load times** — Large images can slow down your page
