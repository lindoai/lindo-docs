---
title: "How to Upload Product Image"
description: "Adding product thumbnail"
---

Add an image to your product.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Product Settings">
    Click the product to open settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Image Upload">
    Locate the image upload area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upload">
    Click to select an image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select File">
    Choose an image from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click save to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Image Guidelines

- Clear, high-quality
- Square or 4:3 ratio
- Show the product
- Good lighting

## Tips

- Use professional images
- Consistent style
- Show product clearly
