The editor is powered by a single, website-aware AI agent. It understands your
whole site, so you describe what you want in the chat and it figures out the
right change—no need to pick a tool for each task.

## What It Handles

The same agent covers every kind of task:

- **New sections** — "Add a features section with 6 benefit cards"
- **Editing sections** — "Update the hero section with a new headline"
- **Individual elements** — "Make this button larger with rounded corners"
- **Whole pages** — "Create an About page with team and company story"
- **Blog posts** — "Write a blog post about productivity tips"
- **Header, footer & site-wide changes** — branding, colors, navigation

## How It Targets Your Change

The agent works out the scope of each request from:
- What you selected (an element, a block, or nothing)
- What you're asking for (add, edit, create)
- The context of your prompt and the rest of your site

Select an element first to scope a change to it, or name the area in your prompt
to point the agent at the right place.

## Credit Usage

There's no fixed per-action price. Credit usage scales with how much work the
agent does and the complexity of the task—small, scoped edits use far fewer
credits than generating full pages or an entire website.

## Tips

- **Be specific** — Clear requests get better results
- **Select first** — Selection helps the agent understand context
- **Scope your request** — Smaller, focused edits use fewer credits
