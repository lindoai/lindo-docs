---
title: "Understanding Product Card"
description: "Product display in the grid"
---

Product cards show your products in the Pages tab.

## Card Elements

- Product image
- Product name
- Price
- Status indicator
- Action menu

## Actions Available

Click the menu to:

- Edit product
- Duplicate product
- Delete product

## Tips

- Use clear product images
- Keep names descriptive
- Monitor product status
- Organize products logically
