title: "Understanding Checkout Options"
description: "Discount codes, phone, address, and quantity settings"
---

Checkout options customize what information you collect during purchase.

## Available Options

### Discount Codes

Allow customers to enter promo codes for discounts.

### Phone Number

Collect customer phone for follow-up or delivery.

### Shipping Address

Collect address for physical product delivery.

### Adjustable Quantity

Let customers purchase multiple units.

## Enabling Options

1. Open product settings
2. Find Checkout Options
3. Toggle desired options
4. Save changes

## Tips

- Only enable what you need
- Consider customer experience
- Test checkout flow
- Manage codes in Stripe
