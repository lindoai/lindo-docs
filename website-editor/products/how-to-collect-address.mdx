---
title: "How to Collect Shipping Address"
description: "Enable address collection for physical products"
---

Collect shipping addresses at checkout for physical product delivery.

## Enabling Address Collection

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Pages** tab
3. Click on a product
4. Open product settings
5. Find **Checkout Options**
6. Toggle **Shipping Address** on
7. Save changes

## Address Fields

When enabled, checkout collects:

- Street address
- City
- State/Province
- Postal code
- Country

## When to Enable

Enable for:

- Physical products
- Shipped merchandise
- Delivery services

## When Not Needed

Disable for:

- Digital products
- Services
- Downloads

## Tips

- Only enable when necessary
- Consider shipping costs
- Validate addresses if possible
- Store securely
