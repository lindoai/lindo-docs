Products in Lindo.ai let you sell physical goods, digital downloads, and services.

## Product Types

### Physical Products

Items that ship to customers.

### Digital Products

Downloadable files and content.

### Services

Bookings and consultations.

### Subscriptions

Recurring access or services.

## Features

- Pricing options
- Checkout customization
- Digital file delivery
- Thank you messages

## Tips

- Use clear product names
- Add quality images
- Set appropriate pricing
- Test checkout flow
