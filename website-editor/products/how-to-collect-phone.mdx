---
title: "How to Collect Customer Phone"
description: "Enable phone number collection at checkout"
---

Collect customer phone numbers during checkout for follow-up communication.

## Enabling Phone Collection

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Pages** tab
3. Click on a product
4. Open product settings
5. Find **Checkout Options**
6. Toggle **Phone Number** on
7. Save changes

## How It Works

When enabled:

- Checkout form includes phone field
- Customers enter their number
- Phone is stored with order
- Available in customer records

## When to Collect Phone

Collect phone numbers for:

- Order updates
- Delivery notifications
- Customer support
- Marketing (with consent)

## Privacy Considerations

- Only collect if needed
- Explain why you need it
- Follow privacy regulations
- Secure customer data

## Tips

- Make it optional if possible
- Explain the purpose
- Use for legitimate business needs
- Respect customer preferences
