---
title: "How to Enable Adjustable Quantity"
description: "Let customers change product quantity at checkout"
---

Allow customers to purchase multiple units of a product in a single transaction.

## Enabling Quantity Selection

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Pages** tab
3. Click on a product
4. Open product settings
5. Find **Checkout Options**
6. Toggle **Adjustable Quantity** on
7. Save changes

## How It Works

When enabled:

- Checkout shows quantity selector
- Customers can increase/decrease
- Total updates automatically
- Multiple units in one order

## When to Enable

Enable for:

- Physical products
- Bulk purchases
- Items often bought in multiples

## When to Disable

Keep disabled for:

- Services (usually one at a time)
- Subscriptions
- Unique items

## Tips

- Consider inventory limits
- Test the checkout flow
- Verify pricing calculations
- Monitor bulk orders
