description: "Full product configuration interface"
---

The Product Settings modal provides complete control over your product configuration.

## What's Included

### Basic Info

- Product name
- Description
- Price and frequency

### Media

- Product image
- Digital file (for downloads)

### Checkout

- Discount codes
- Phone collection
- Address collection
- Quantity adjustment

### Post-Purchase

- Thank you message

## Accessing Settings

1. Go to Pages tab
2. Find your product
3. Click to open settings

## Tips

- Complete all relevant fields
- Use clear descriptions
- Test checkout flow
- Update as needed
