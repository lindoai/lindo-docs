See how your product will look on your website before publishing.

## Previewing a Product

1. Go to the **Pages** tab
2. Select **Products** from the page type filter
3. Find the product you want to preview
4. Click the preview icon on the product card
5. The product page displays in the preview area

## What You'll See

The preview shows:
- Product name and description
- Price and frequency
- Product image
- Buy/checkout button
- Any configured options (quantity, etc.)

## Testing Checkout Flow

In preview mode, you can:
- See the product layout
- Check responsive design on different devices
- Verify pricing displays correctly

Note: Actual checkout testing requires publishing the page.

## Making Changes

If something doesn't look right:
1. Click **Edit** on the product
2. Update the settings
3. Preview again to verify changes
