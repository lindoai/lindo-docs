Products in Lindo.ai support various pricing models.

## Pricing Options

### One-Time

- Single payment
- Customer pays once
- No recurring charges
- Good for: Products, downloads

### Monthly

- Recurring monthly charge
- Automatic billing
- Creates subscription
- Good for: Memberships, services

### Yearly

- Recurring annual charge
- Often discounted
- Creates subscription
- Good for: Annual plans

### Weekly

- Recurring weekly charge
- Frequent billing
- Creates subscription
- Good for: Weekly services

## Setting Price

1. Open product settings
2. Enter price amount
3. Select currency
4. Choose frequency
5. Save

## Tips

- Match pricing to value
- Consider customer preferences
- Offer multiple options
- Test checkout flow
