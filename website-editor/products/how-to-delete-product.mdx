---
title: "How to Delete a Product"
description: "Remove a product from your website"
---

Delete products that are no longer available or needed.

## Deleting a Product

1. Open your website in the editor
2. Go to **Pages** tab
3. Find the product to delete
4. Click the three-dot menu
5. Select **Delete**
6. Confirm deletion

## What Happens

When you delete a product:

- Product is removed from website
- Product page is deleted
- Cannot be undone
- Existing orders unaffected

## Before Deleting

Consider:

- Are there active subscriptions?
- Should you unpublish instead?
- Do you need the product data?

## Alternatives to Deletion

Instead of deleting:

- Unpublish to hide temporarily
- Update to mark as sold out
- Archive for future use

## Tips

- Review before deleting
- Consider customer impact
- Keep records if needed
- Communicate changes to customers
