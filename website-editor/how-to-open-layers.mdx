---

Access the page structure view.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Layers Button">
    Look for the Layers icon in preview header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to Open">
    Click the button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Panel Opens">
    See the page structure tree.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Using Layers

- Expand sections
- Click to select elements
- Navigate hierarchy

## Tips

- Use for complex pages
- Find nested elements
- Quick selection
