title: "How to Create a Product"
description: "Add a product to sell on your website"
---

Add products to your website to sell physical goods, digital downloads, or services. Products integrate with Stripe for payment processing.

<Note>
Requires Stripe connected in Settings → Integrations.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Products section">
    Open your website in the editor, go to the **Pages** tab, and find the **Products** section. Click **Add Product** or the **+** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter product details">
    Fill in the basic information:
    - **Name** — What you're selling
    - **Description** — Details about the product
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set pricing">
    Configure how much to charge:
    - **Price** — The amount
    - **Currency** — USD, EUR, GBP, etc.
    - **Frequency** — One-time, Monthly, Yearly, or Weekly for subscriptions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload an image (optional)">
    Click the image upload area and select a product photo. This appears on the product card and checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure checkout options">
    Enable the options you need:
    - **Allow discount codes** — Let customers enter promo codes
    - **Collect phone number** — Require phone at checkout
    - **Collect shipping address** — For physical products
    - **Allow quantity adjustment** — Let customers buy multiple
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add digital file (optional)">
    For downloadable products, click **Upload File** and select the file. Customers receive a download link after purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set thank you message (optional)">
    Enter a custom message customers see after completing their purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the product">
    Click **Save** or **Create Product**. Your product is now available to add to pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding Products to Pages

After creating a product, add it to a page using the AI Chat:

```
Add a product section featuring [Product Name]
```

Or create a full product page:

```
Create a product page for [Product Name] with features,
pricing, and buy button
```

## Tips

- **Clear descriptions** — Help customers understand exactly what they're buying
- **Quality images** — Good photos significantly increase conversions
- **Competitive pricing** — Research your market before setting prices
