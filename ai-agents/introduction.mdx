Lindo has a single, website-aware AI agent. It understands your whole site—every
page, your design guide, and your settings—so you can just describe what you want
and it makes the change. There's no need to pick a specialized tool for each task:
the same agent creates pages, adds or edits sections, fine-tunes individual
elements, updates the header and footer, writes blog posts, and can even build or
redesign the entire website—all from one conversation.

## What It Can Do

- Build a complete multi-page website from a description
- Create and edit individual pages and sections
- Fine-tune specific elements (text, buttons, images, styling)
- Update the shared header, footer, and site-wide settings
- Write blog posts and other content
- Apply site-wide changes like branding, colors, and fonts

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe what you want">
    Type your request in the AI Chat panel. Be specific about what you want to create or change, and mention the section or area when relevant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The agent understands the context">
    It reads your page and the rest of your site, then figures out exactly what to change—whether that's one element, a section, a page, or the whole website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It makes the change">
    The agent generates or modifies the content based on your description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Result appears in preview">
    The changes show up in the live preview. You can keep refining with follow-up requests.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

- **Be specific** — Clear requests get better results
- **Mention the area** — Naming the section or element helps the agent target the right place
- **Select first** — Selecting an element before typing helps the agent understand context
- **Iterate** — Refine with follow-up requests rather than starting over
