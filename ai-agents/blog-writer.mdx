Generate complete blog posts with AI.

## What It Does

Creates blog content:
- Full articles
- Headlines
- Subheadings
- Body content
- SEO optimization

## Credit Cost

**30 credits** per blog post.

## When to Use

- Creating blog content
- Writing articles
- Content marketing
- SEO content

## How to Use

1. Create new blog post
2. Select Blog Writer agent
3. Describe the topic
4. AI generates content

## Best Prompts

Include:
- Topic
- Target audience
- Key points
- Tone/style

## Example

> "Write a blog post about 10 tips for small business websites, targeting entrepreneurs, professional tone"

## Tips

- Clear topic
- Specify length
- Review and edit
