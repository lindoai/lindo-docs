Troubleshoot credit-related problems.

## Common Issues

### Credits Not Showing

**Symptoms:**
- Balance shows 0
- Credits not updating

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Check Settings → Credits
3. Verify plan is active

### Credits Not Deducting

**Symptoms:**
- Balance unchanged after use

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh to see updated balance
2. Check transaction history

### Ran Out of Credits

**Symptoms:**
- AI features not working
- "No credits" message

**Solutions:**
1. Purchase additional credits
2. Wait for monthly reset
3. Upgrade plan

### Purchase Not Applied

**Symptoms:**
- Paid but credits not added

**Solutions:**
1. Wait a few minutes
2. Check purchase history
3. Contact support

## Prevention

- Monitor usage
- Purchase before running out
- Know your reset date

## Tips

- Check balance regularly
- Plan for large projects
- Contact support for issues
