Troubleshoot team member invitation problems.

## Common Issues

### Invitation Not Received

**Symptoms:**
- Team member didn't get email

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam folder
2. Verify email address
3. Resend invitation

### Invitation Expired

**Symptoms:**
- Link no longer works

**Solutions:**
1. Resend invitation
2. New link generated

### Wrong Email

**Symptoms:**
- Sent to wrong address

**Solutions:**
1. Revoke invitation
2. Send to correct email

### Can't Accept Invitation

**Symptoms:**
- Error when clicking link

**Solutions:**
1. Try different browser
2. Clear cookies
3. Request new invitation

## Prevention

- Verify email before sending
- Communicate with team
- Follow up on pending

## Tips

- Double-check email
- Resend if needed
- Contact support if stuck
