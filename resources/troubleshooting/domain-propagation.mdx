DNS changes don't take effect immediately. Here's what to expect.

## What is Propagation?

DNS propagation is the time it takes for DNS changes to spread across the internet.

## Typical Timing

- **Fast**: 15 minutes to 4 hours
- **Average**: 4-24 hours
- **Maximum**: Up to 48 hours

## Why It Takes Time

DNS records are cached by:

- Your ISP
- DNS servers worldwide
- Browsers
- Operating systems

Each cache has its own refresh timing.

## Checking Propagation

Use online tools:

1. Go to dnschecker.org
2. Enter your domain
3. Check propagation status globally

## What to Do

While waiting:

- Don't make additional changes
- Check status periodically
- Be patient
- Contact support if over 48 hours

## Tips

- Plan domain changes in advance
- Lower TTL before changes (if possible)
- Verify records are correct
- Wait before troubleshooting
