---
title: "Managing Client Expectations"
description: "Communication tips for agency owners working with clients"
---

Set your clients up for success by establishing clear expectations from the start.

## Setting Expectations Early

### During Onboarding
- Explain what AI-generated websites can and can't do
- Set realistic timelines for revisions
- Clarify the revision process
- Define what's included in your service

### Credit Allocation
- Explain how credits work
- Set appropriate monthly limits
- Communicate when credits are running low
- Discuss options for additional credits

## Communication Best Practices

### Be Proactive
- Send progress updates
- Notify clients before making major changes
- Alert them to any issues early

### Document Everything
- Use the notes field in client details
- Keep records of requests and changes
- Track revision history

### Set Response Times
- Define your availability
- Set expectations for turnaround
- Use email notifications for workflow completion

## Handling Revisions

### Scope Creep
- Define revision limits upfront
- Distinguish between revisions and new features
- Price additional work appropriately

### AI Limitations
- Some designs may need manual refinement
- Complex interactions may require custom code
- Set expectations for what AI can achieve

## Billing Transparency

### Clear Invoicing
- Use descriptive service names
- Break down costs when appropriate
- Send invoices promptly

### Subscription Management
- Explain recurring billing clearly
- Provide advance notice of renewals
- Make cancellation process clear

## Building Long-term Relationships

- Deliver consistent quality
- Be responsive to feedback
- Offer value beyond basic services
- Check in periodically
