As a Lindo.ai reseller, pricing your services correctly is crucial for profitability and client satisfaction.

## Pricing Models

### Project-Based Pricing

Charge per website:

- **Landing Page**: $500-1,500
- **Multi-Page Website**: $1,500-5,000
- **E-commerce Site**: $3,000-10,000

Pros: Clear scope, predictable revenue
Cons: Scope creep risk, requires accurate estimates

### Monthly Retainer

Ongoing service fee:

- **Basic**: $99-199/month (hosting, minor updates)
- **Standard**: $299-499/month (updates, support)
- **Premium**: $500-1,000/month (full management)

Pros: Recurring revenue, client retention
Cons: Requires ongoing value delivery

### Hourly Rate

Charge for time spent:

- **Junior**: $50-75/hour
- **Mid-level**: $75-125/hour
- **Senior**: $125-200/hour

Pros: Fair for variable work
Cons: Clients may resist, income unpredictable

## Calculating Your Rates

### Cost-Plus Method

1. Calculate your costs:
   - Lindo.ai subscription
   - AI credits used
   - Time invested
   - Overhead expenses

2. Add profit margin (30-50%)

3. Set minimum project price

### Value-Based Pricing

Price based on client value:

- What's the website worth to them?
- What revenue will it generate?
- What's their budget?

## Package Examples

### Starter Package - $999

- Single-page website
- Mobile responsive
- Basic SEO setup
- 1 revision round
- 30-day support

### Professional Package - $2,499

- Up to 5 pages
- Custom design
- SEO optimization
- Contact forms
- 3 revision rounds
- 90-day support

### Enterprise Package - $4,999+

- Unlimited pages
- E-commerce setup
- Advanced integrations
- Priority support
- Monthly maintenance

## Tips for Pricing

- Research competitor pricing
- Start higher, negotiate down
- Include clear deliverables
- Define revision limits
- Separate hosting/maintenance
- Offer payment plans

## Handling Price Objections

- Emphasize value, not cost
- Show portfolio examples
- Explain what's included
- Offer tiered options
- Highlight ongoing support

## Increasing Profitability

- Use templates to speed up work
- Batch similar projects
- Upsell maintenance plans
- Offer add-on services
- Automate with workflows
