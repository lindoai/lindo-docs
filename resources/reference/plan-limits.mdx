Each plan has specific limits for resources and features.

## Pricing

| Plan | Monthly | Yearly |
|------|---------|--------|
| Pro | $30/mo | $21/mo (save 30%) |
| Business | $150/mo | $105/mo (save 30%) |
| Elite | $300/mo | $210/mo (save 30%) |
| Reseller | $2,000/mo | Billed yearly only |

| Plan | Active Websites |
|------|-----------------|

## Overage Handling

When you exceed limits:
- **Websites**: Cannot create new websites until upgraded or deleted
- **Credits**: Cannot use AI features until credits refresh or purchased
