title: "Support & Help FAQ"
description: "Common questions about getting help and support"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    1. Click your profile icon in the header
    2. Select "Support" from the menu
    3. Submit your request with details about your issue
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are support hours?">
    Support is available during business hours. Response times vary by plan:
    - **Standard plans**: 24-48 hours
    - **Reseller plans**: Priority response (faster)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there live chat?">
    Currently, support is handled through the ticket system for thorough, documented assistance. This ensures your issues are properly tracked and resolved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a community forum?">
    Yes! Join our Slack community:
    1. Click your profile icon
    2. Select "Community"
    3. Join the Slack workspace
    
    Connect with other users, share tips, and get help.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request features?">
    Absolutely! Use the feedback form:
    1. Click your profile icon
    2. Select "Feedback"
    3. Submit your feature request
    
    We review all suggestions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I find tutorials?">
    - This documentation (browse the sidebar)
    - Tutorials section for step-by-step guides
    - Community discussions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hire an expert?">
    Yes, for complex projects:
    1. Click your profile icon
    2. Select "Hire an Expert"
    3. Submit your requirements
    
    Get hands-on help from professionals.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I find a bug?">
    Report bugs through the feedback form with:
    - Steps to reproduce the issue
    - Expected vs actual behavior
    - Screenshots if helpful
    - Browser and device info
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if the AI isn't working?">
    Try these steps:
    1. Refresh the page
    2. Check your credit balance
    3. Try a simpler, clearer prompt
    4. Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I can't log in?">
    Troubleshooting steps:
    1. Verify your email and password
    2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
    3. Clear browser cache and cookies
    4. Try a different browser
    5. Contact support if still stuck
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my email?">
    You can change your email directly from your workspace settings:
    1. Open Settings and go to the **Account** tab
    2. Click **Change Email** and enter your new email address
    3. A verification code will be sent to the new email
    4. Enter the verification code to confirm the change

    Your account email will be updated once the code is verified. If the code expires, you can request a new one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    You can delete your account from your workspace settings:
    1. Open Settings and go to the **Account** tab
    2. Click **Delete Account**
    3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

    Before deleting your account, you must:
    - Cancel any active subscriptions
    - Delete all websites you own

    Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where do I see my invoices?">
    1. Open Settings
    2. Go to Billing tab
    3. View invoice history and download receipts
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get a refund?">
    Contact support within 14 days of charge for refund requests. Each case is reviewed individually.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
