---
title: "White Label FAQ"
description: "Common questions about white label branding"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is white label?">
    White label lets you brand Lindo.ai as your own platform. Your clients see your brand, logo, and domain—not Lindo.ai. Perfect for agencies.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which plans include white label?">
    - **Business** ✓
    - **Elite** ✓
    - **Reseller** ✓
    
    Pro and Business plans don't include white label.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can I customize?">
    - **Logo**: Your logo in the header
    - **Favicon**: Browser tab icon
    - **Login background**: Custom login page image
    - **Domain**: app.youragency.com
    - **Workspace name**: Your agency name
    - **Default language**: Interface language for clients
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do clients see Lindo.ai anywhere?">
    No. With white label properly configured, clients only see your branding. Lindo.ai is completely hidden.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Yes, set up a custom domain like `app.youragency.com` for your workspace. Clients log in there instead of lindo.ai.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What size should my logo be?">
    - **Full logo**: 200x50px recommended
    - **Favicon**: 32x32px
    - **Login background**: 1920x1080px
    
    Use PNG or SVG with transparent background for logos.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can clients change their own language?">
    Yes, clients can change their interface language using the language selector. You set the default, but they can override it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Agentic Mode?">
    Agentic Mode is the AI-powered interface. When enabled, clients can use AI to create and edit websites. You can enable/disable this per client.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I enable Agentic Mode for all clients at once?">
    Yes, in Settings → White Label → Workspace, toggle "Enable Agentic for All Clients" to migrate existing clients.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Client Registration?">
    When enabled, clients can self-register at your workspace URL. They create their own account without you manually adding them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I preview what clients see?">
    Click "Preview Client View" in Settings → White Label → Workspace. Opens a new tab showing the exact client experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have different branding for different clients?">
    No, white label branding applies to the entire workspace. All clients see the same branding.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
