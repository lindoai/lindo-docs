---
title: "CRM & Billing FAQ"
description: "Common questions about client management and billing"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Workspace CRM and Website CRM?">
    **Workspace CRM** manages your agency clients—people you bill for website services. **Website CRM** manages your website's customers—people who buy products on your published sites.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need Stripe to use CRM?">
    You need Stripe connected to send invoices and process payments. You can still manage client information, allocate credits, and track details without Stripe—just no billing features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many clients can I have?">
    Client limits by plan:
    - **Business**: 20 clients
    - **Elite**: 100 clients
    - **Reseller**: Unlimited
    
    Pro and Business plans don't include client management.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can clients see my other clients?">
    No, clients only see their own websites and information. Each client's view is completely isolated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What currencies are supported for invoicing?">
    All currencies supported by Stripe, including USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, and many more. Set the currency per invoice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I offer discounts to clients?">
    Yes, you set custom pricing per invoice. Charge whatever amount makes sense for your services.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do refunds work?">
    Refunds are processed through Stripe. Go to the invoice, click Refund, and the amount returns to the client's original payment method.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do client credits work?">
    You allocate AI credits from your workspace pool to clients. Clients use their allocation for AI features. You control how many credits each client gets monthly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused client credits roll over?">
    No, monthly credit allocations reset each month. Unused credits don't carry forward.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I set up recurring billing?">
    Yes, create subscriptions for clients with automatic monthly or custom-frequency billing. Stripe handles the recurring charges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track client payments?">
    View all invoices and payment status in CRM → Billing tab. See paid, pending, and overdue invoices at a glance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can clients pay invoices online?">
    Yes, invoices include a payment link. Clients click the link and pay securely through Stripe.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
