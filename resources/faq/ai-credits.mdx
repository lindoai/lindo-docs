---
title: "AI Credits FAQ"
description: "Common questions about the credit system"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are AI credits?">
    Credits are the currency for AI-powered features. Each AI operation—generating content, editing blocks, creating pages—consumes credits from your balance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many credits do I get?">
    Depends on your plan:

    | Plan | Monthly Credits |
    |------|-----------------|
    | Free | 50 |
    | Pro | 500 |
    | Business | 2,000 |
    | Elite | 5,000 |
    | Reseller | 10,000+ |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does each operation cost?">
    There's no fixed per-action price. Credits are consumed based on how much work the agent does and how complex the task is—a small text or style tweak uses far fewer credits than building a full page or generating an entire multi-page website. Credits are only deducted when an operation completes successfully.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When do credits reset?">
    Credits reset on your billing date each month. Check Settings → Credits to see your reset date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused credits roll over?">
    No. Unused credits don't roll over to the next month. They reset to your plan's allocation on your billing date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I buy more credits?">
    Yes. Go to Settings → Credits → Purchase Credits. Additional credits are added to your balance immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I run out?">
    When credits reach zero, AI features become unavailable. You can still edit manually, publish existing content, and manage your websites. Purchase more credits or wait for your monthly reset.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do failed operations use credits?">
    No. Credits are only deducted when operations complete successfully. Failed or cancelled operations don't consume credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do client credits work?">
    For agencies: You allocate credits to clients from your workspace pool. Clients use their allocation for AI features. You can set monthly limits per client. All client usage comes from your workspace's total credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see credit history?">
    Yes. Settings → Credits shows your current balance, estimated monthly usage, purchase history, and reset date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I check my balance quickly?">
    Credit balance appears in the toolbar in the AI Prompt Builder, the website dropdown in the editor, and Settings → Credits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
