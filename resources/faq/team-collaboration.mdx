title: "Team & Collaboration FAQ"
description: "Common questions about team features"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many team members can I have?">
    Team limits by plan:
    - **Free**: 1 (just you)
    - **Pro**: 3 members
    - **Business**: 10 members
    - **Elite**: 25 members
    - **Reseller**: Unlimited
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What roles are available?">
    Four roles with different permissions:
    - **Owner**: Full access, billing control, can't be removed
    - **Admin**: Manage settings and team, no billing access
    - **Editor**: Create and edit websites only
    - **Viewer**: View-only access, no editing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can team members have different roles?">
    Yes, assign the appropriate role to each member based on what they need to do.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can Editors do?">
    Editors can:
    - Create new websites
    - Edit existing websites
    - Use AI features
    
    Editors cannot access settings, manage team, or view billing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can Viewers do?">
    Viewers can only view websites—no editing, creating, or settings access. Useful for stakeholders who need visibility.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change someone's role?">
    Yes, update roles anytime in Settings → Team. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I invite someone?">
    1. Go to Settings → Team Members
    2. Click "Invite Member"
    3. Enter their email and select a role
    4. They receive an invitation email
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if they don't receive the invitation?">
    - Check their spam/junk folder
    - Verify the email address is correct
    - Resend the invitation from Settings → Team
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I revoke access?">
    Yes, remove team members anytime from Settings → Team. Their access ends immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do team members share credits?">
    Yes, all team members use credits from the workspace's shared pool. Monitor usage in Settings → Credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can team members see all websites?">
    Yes, team members can see all websites in the workspace based on their role permissions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
