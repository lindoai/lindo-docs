---
title: "Reseller FAQ"
description: "Common questions about the reseller program"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the reseller program?">
    The reseller program allows you to offer Lindo.ai's website building services under your own brand, charging your own prices to clients. You become a white-label website agency.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is the reseller plan for?">
    Ideal for:
    - Digital agencies
    - Web design freelancers
    - Marketing agencies
    - White label service providers
    - Anyone building websites for clients at scale
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in the reseller plan?">
    - 100 active websites
    - 10,000+ AI credits/month
    - Full white label branding
    - Custom domain support
    - Unlimited clients
    - Built-in CRM and invoicing
    - Priority support
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I completely remove Lindo.ai branding?">
    Yes, with white label you can:
    - Use your own logo and favicon
    - Use your own domain (app.youragency.com)
    - Customize colors and appearance
    - Remove all Lindo.ai references
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do my clients know they're using Lindo.ai?">
    With proper white label setup, clients see only your branding. They interact with your domain and see your logo throughout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I set my own pricing?">
    Absolutely. You set your own prices for clients. Lindo.ai doesn't dictate what you charge—keep 100% of what you bill.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I bill my clients?">
    Use the built-in invoicing system:
    1. Connect your Stripe account
    2. Create invoices for clients
    3. Set one-time or recurring billing
    4. Clients pay directly to you
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can clients create their own websites?">
    Yes, if you enable it. You control:
    - Website limits per client
    - Credit allocations
    - Feature access (Agentic Mode)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I manage client credits?">
    In the CRM, you can:
    - Set monthly credit allocations per client
    - Add one-time credit bonuses
    - Monitor usage across all clients
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need technical knowledge?">
    No coding required. The AI handles website generation. You focus on client relationships and growing your business.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export client websites?">
    Yes, websites can be exported as ZIP files for hosting elsewhere if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What support do resellers get?">
    Resellers receive priority support with faster response times and dedicated assistance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum commitment?">
    Plans are billed monthly or annually. No long-term contracts required—cancel anytime.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade from a lower plan?">
    Yes, upgrade to reseller from any plan. Your existing websites, clients, and settings transfer automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
