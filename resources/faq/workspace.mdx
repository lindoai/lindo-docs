description: "Common questions about workspaces"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a workspace?">
    A workspace is your main dashboard where you manage websites, clients, and settings. It's the central hub for all your Lindo.ai activities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many websites can I have?">
    Website limits by plan:
    - **Free**: 1 website
    - **Pro**: 3 websites
    - **Business**: 10 websites
    - **Elite**: 25 websites
    - **Reseller**: 100 websites
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple workspaces?">
    Currently, each account has one workspace. For multiple workspaces, you would need separate accounts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my workspace name?">
    1. Open Settings in the sidebar
    2. Go to Workspace tab
    3. Edit the workspace name
    4. Click Save
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many team members can I add?">
    Depends on your plan—check Settings → Plans for your specific limit. Ranges from 1 (Free) to unlimited (Reseller).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What roles are available for team members?">
    - **Owner**: Full access, billing control
    - **Admin**: Manage settings and team
    - **Editor**: Create and edit websites
    - **Viewer**: View-only access
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can team members see all websites?">
    Yes, team members can see all websites in the workspace based on their role permissions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between team members and clients?">
    - **Team members**: Your staff who help manage the workspace
    - **Clients**: Your customers who access their own websites (Business+ plans)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can clients see other clients' websites?">
    No, clients only see websites assigned to them. Each client's view is completely isolated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I give a client access?">
    1. Open CRM in the sidebar
    2. Add the client with their email
    3. They receive an invitation to access their dashboard
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I set up white label?">
    1. Open Settings
    2. Go through Workspace, Appearance, and Domain tabs
    3. Configure your branding and custom domain
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan anytime?">
    Yes, upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Settings → Plans. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
