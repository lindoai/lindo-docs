---
title: "Pricing & Plans FAQ"
description: "Common questions about pricing and plans"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What plans are available?">
    Four paid plans to choose from:
    - **Pro** — $30/month
    - **Business** — $150/month
    - **Elite** — $300/month
    - **Reseller** — $2,000/month (billed yearly)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Pro and Business?">
    | Feature | Pro | Business |
    |---------|-----|----------|
    | Price | $30/mo | $150/mo |
    | Credits | 500 | 1,500 |
    | Websites | 1 | 10 |
    | Team | Unlimited | 3 workspace |
    | API | ✗ | ✓ |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in Elite?">
    Elite ($300/month) includes:
    - 3,000 AI credits/month
    - 20 active websites
    - White label branding
    - Client CRM
    - API access
    - Unlimited team
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?">
    Yes, changes take effect immediately:
    - **Upgrade**: New features available instantly
    - **Downgrade**: Current features remain until billing period ends
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Contact sales for trial options. You can also start with Pro plan and cancel anytime within the first 14 days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    All major credit cards via Stripe:
    - Visa
    - Mastercard
    - American Express
    - And more
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pay annually?">
    Yes! Annual billing saves 30% on Pro, Business, and Elite plans. Reseller is billed yearly by default.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my limits?">
    - **Credits**: AI features unavailable until reset or purchase more
    - **Websites**: Can't create new until you upgrade or delete existing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Contact support within 14 days of purchase for refund requests. Handled case-by-case.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a discount for nonprofits?">
    Contact sales for nonprofit pricing options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my websites if I downgrade?">
    Your websites remain but become inactive if you exceed the new plan's limits. You'll need to delete some or upgrade to reactivate them.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
