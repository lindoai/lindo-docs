---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Lindo.ai?">
    Lindo.ai is an AI-powered website builder. Describe what you want in plain text, and the AI creates it. Edit with natural language commands—no coding required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    You can start with any plan and cancel within 14 days for a full refund. Contact sales for extended trial options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create my first website?">
    Log in to your workspace, type a description in the AI Prompt Builder (e.g., "Landing page for a coffee shop with hero, menu, and contact sections"), and click submit. The AI generates your website in 30-60 seconds. Click the website card to open and edit it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to create a website?">
    Initial generation takes 30-60 seconds. Basic customization takes 15-30 minutes. A fully polished website typically takes 1-2 hours.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need coding skills?">
    No. Everything is done through natural language. Just describe what you want, and the AI handles the code.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Yes, on Pro plan and above. Connect your domain in the website's Settings → Domains section.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my website if I cancel?">
    If you cancel, your website becomes inactive. Export your website before canceling to keep a copy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my website?">
    Yes, on Pro plan and above. Export downloads a ZIP file with HTML, CSS, and images that you can host anywhere.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do AI credits work?">
    Credits are consumed when AI performs tasks. Most operations cost 10 credits. Credits reset monthly on your billing date. You can purchase more anytime.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. Lindo.ai uses industry-standard security including HTTPS encryption and secure data storage. Your data is never shared.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I collaborate with others?">
    Yes, on Business plan and above. Invite team members with different roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer) to collaborate on your workspace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I need help?">
    You have several options: this documentation, support via the user menu, the Slack community, or the "Hire an Expert" option for hands-on help.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
