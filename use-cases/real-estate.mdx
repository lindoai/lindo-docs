## Example: Real Estate Agent

**Prompt:**
```
Website for Sarah Martinez, Realtor in Miami, Florida.
Specializing in luxury waterfront properties and condos.
10 years experience, $50M+ in sales.
Fluent in English and Spanish.
Focus on first-time buyers and investors.
```

**Result:**
- Professional hero with agent photo
- Featured listings section
- About section with credentials
- Testimonials from past clients
- Neighborhood guides
- Contact form and scheduling link
- Social media integration
