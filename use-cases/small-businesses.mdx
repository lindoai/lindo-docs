## Tips for Local Businesses

<Tip>
**Include your location prominently.** City, neighborhood, service area—make it easy for local customers to find you.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Add a Google Maps embed.** Helps customers find you and improves local SEO.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Make contact easy.** Phone number in the header, contact form on every page, clear call-to-action buttons.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**List all your services.** Each service is a chance to rank in search results.
</Tip>
