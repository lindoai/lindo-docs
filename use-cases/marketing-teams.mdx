## Example: Product Launch

**Prompt:**
```
Landing page for the launch of "ProAnalytics 2.0"
New features: AI insights, real-time dashboards, Slack integration.
Target: existing customers and enterprise prospects.
Goal: drive demo requests and upgrades.
Launch date: March 15th.
```

**Result:**
- Announcement hero with product screenshot
- New features breakdown
- Comparison (old vs new)
- Customer testimonials
- Demo request form
- Upgrade CTA for existing customers

**Time to launch: 2 hours** (vs 2-3 weeks with dev team)
