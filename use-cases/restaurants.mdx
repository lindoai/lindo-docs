## Example: Italian Restaurant

**Prompt:**
```
Website for Bella Notte Italian Restaurant in Chicago.
Family-owned trattoria serving authentic Tuscan cuisine.
Known for handmade pasta and wood-fired pizza.
Romantic atmosphere, perfect for date nights.
Open for dinner Tuesday-Sunday.
```

**Result:**
- Atmospheric hero with food photography
- Menu sections (appetizers, pasta, pizza, desserts, wine)
- Reservation widget
- About section with family story
- Photo gallery of dishes and interior
- Location, hours, and contact info
