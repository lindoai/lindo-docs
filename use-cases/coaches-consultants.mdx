## Example: Executive Coach

**Prompt:**
```
Website for Michael Torres, Executive Leadership Coach.
20 years Fortune 500 experience, former VP at Google.
Specializes in C-suite transitions and leadership presence.
Offers 1:1 coaching, group programs, and keynote speaking.
Based in San Francisco, works with clients globally.
```

**Result:**
- Authority-building hero with professional photo
- Credentials and experience section
- Services overview (coaching, programs, speaking)
- Client testimonials and logos
- Featured media appearances
- Free resource download (lead magnet)
- Booking calendar integration

---

## Key Elements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Authority Building" icon="award">
    Credentials, certifications, media features, client logos—establish trust fast.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lead Magnets" icon="magnet">
    Free downloads, assessments, or mini-courses to capture email addresses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Booking Integration" icon="calendar">
    Calendly, Acuity, or similar for easy discovery call scheduling.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Testimonials" icon="quote-left">
    Client success stories and video testimonials build social proof.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
