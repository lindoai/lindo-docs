## Example: Environmental Nonprofit

**Prompt:**
```
Website for Ocean Guardians, a marine conservation nonprofit.
Mission: Protecting ocean ecosystems through research and education.
Programs: Beach cleanups, coral restoration, school education.
Based in Miami, working across the Caribbean.
Need donation integration and volunteer signup.
```

**Result:**
- Emotional hero with ocean imagery
- Mission and impact statement
- Programs overview
- Impact statistics
- Volunteer signup form
- Donation CTA
- News and updates section
- Partner logos
