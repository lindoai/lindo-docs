## Example: Online Course Creator

**Prompt:**
```
Landing page for "Master Data Science in 12 Weeks" online course.
Instructor: Dr. Sarah Kim, former Google data scientist.
Includes video lessons, projects, and career coaching.
Price: $997 or 3 payments of $397.
Target: career changers and aspiring data scientists.
```

**Result:**
- Compelling hero with course promise
- Curriculum breakdown
- Instructor credentials
- Student testimonials
- Pricing options
- FAQ section
- Enrollment CTA
