## How It Works

### Step 1: Set Up Your Branded Platform

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure White-Label">
    Add your logo, colors, and custom domain. Takes about 30 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Billing">
    Connect Stripe to handle client payments automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Client Packages">
    Define your pricing tiers and what each includes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Onboard Clients

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Client to CRM">
    Create their profile with company info and contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Limits">
    Assign website quota and monthly AI credits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Client receives branded email to access their portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Build Websites Fast

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Brief">
    Get business name, description, target audience, and any reference sites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate with AI">
    Use the prompt builder to create the initial website in 2-5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine & Polish">
    Use AI chat to adjust copy, layout, and design. Usually 30-60 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client Review">
    Share preview link, collect feedback, make final tweaks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Connect domain and go live. Total time: 2-4 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Agency Success Tips

<Tip>
**Standardize your process.** Create templates for common industries (restaurants, real estate, professional services). Reuse what works.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Set clear boundaries.** Define exactly what's included in each package. Unlimited revisions = unlimited scope creep.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Enable Agentic Mode selectively.** Not every client needs AI editing access. Offer it as a premium add-on.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Use the CRM notes.** Track client preferences, past feedback, and communication history. Your future self will thank you.
</Tip>
