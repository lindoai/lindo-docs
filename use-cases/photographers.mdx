## Example: Wedding Photographer

**Prompt:**
```
Portfolio website for Emily Rose Photography.
Wedding and engagement photographer in Napa Valley.
Light, airy editing style. Romantic and timeless.
Packages from $3,000-$8,000.
Featured in Martha Stewart Weddings.
```

**Result:**
- Full-screen hero with signature image
- Portfolio gallery with categories
- About section with photographer bio
- Pricing/packages overview
- Testimonials from couples
- Contact form with inquiry details
- Instagram feed integration
