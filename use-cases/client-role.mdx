## Welcome to Your Website Portal

Your web professional uses Lindo.ai to build and manage your website. They may have given you access to:

- **View your website** and its analytics
- **Make simple edits** using AI assistance (if enabled)
- **Review and approve** changes before they go live

---

## What You Can Do

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="View Only">
    If you have view-only access, you can:
    
    - See your website preview
    - View traffic analytics
    - Review page content
    - Request changes from your provider
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Edit Access (Agentic Mode)">
    If your provider enabled Agentic Mode, you can:
    
    - Make text and content changes
    - Update images
    - Add new sections
    - All using simple AI chat commands
    
    **Note:** Your edits use AI credits allocated by your provider.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Making Edits with AI

If you have edit access, making changes is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Website">
    Log in to the portal your provider gave you access to
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Page">
    Find the page you want to edit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Chat">
    Type what you want to change in plain English
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes">
    AI makes the edit—review and approve
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Example Edits

```
"Change the phone number to 555-123-4567"
```

```
"Update the hours to Monday-Friday 9am-6pm"
```

```
"Add a new service called 'Emergency Repairs'"
```

```
"Replace the hero image with this photo" [upload image]
```

---

## Understanding Credits

Your provider has allocated a certain number of AI credits for your use. Each edit uses some credits.

- **Simple text changes:** 1-2 credits
- **Adding sections:** 5-10 credits
- **Major redesigns:** 20-50 credits

If you run out of credits, contact your provider to request more.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I break my website?" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    No. All changes can be undone, and your provider can restore previous versions at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who pays for this?" icon="credit-card">
    Your provider handles all billing with Lindo.ai. Your access is part of their service to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get more access?" icon="key">
    Talk to your provider. They control what features you can access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I want to manage it myself?" icon="user">
    You can sign up for your own Lindo.ai account. Talk to your provider about transitioning ownership.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
