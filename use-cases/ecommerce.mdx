## Example: DTC Brand

**Prompt:**
```
Website for Evergreen Skincare, a clean beauty brand.
Sustainable, vegan skincare products.
Hero product: Vitamin C Serum.
Target audience: eco-conscious millennials.
Minimalist, natural aesthetic.
```

**Result:**
- Hero with product photography
- Product benefits section
- Ingredients transparency
- Sustainability story
- Customer reviews
- Shop now CTA (links to Shopify)

## Integration Options

Lindo.ai websites work alongside your e-commerce platform:

| Platform | Integration |
|----------|-------------|
| Shopify | Link "Shop Now" buttons to your Shopify store |
| WooCommerce | Embed product widgets or link to store |
| Gumroad | Perfect for digital products |
| Stripe | Direct checkout links for simple products |
