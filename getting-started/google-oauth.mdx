Sign up or log in to Lindo.ai instantly using your Google account.

## How to Sign In with Google

1. Go to [lindo.ai](https://lindo.ai)
2. Click **Continue with Google**
3. Select your Google account from the popup
4. If prompted, authorize Lindo.ai to access your basic profile info
5. You're signed in

## What Lindo.ai Accesses

When you sign in with Google, Lindo.ai only accesses:
- Your email address
- Your name
- Your profile picture (optional)

Lindo.ai does not access your contacts, calendar, drive, or any other Google services.

## Benefits of Google Sign-In

- **No password to remember** - Use your existing Google credentials
- **Faster sign up** - Skip email verification
- **Secure** - Google handles authentication

## Switching to Email Login

If you signed up with Google but want to use email login instead:
1. You can sign in with the same email address
2. Request a PIN code to that email
3. Both methods work with the same account

## Troubleshooting

**Google popup blocked?**
- Allow popups for lindo.ai in your browser settings
- Try a different browser

**Wrong Google account?**
- Sign out of Google first
- Then sign in to Lindo.ai and select the correct account

**Authorization failed?**
- Clear browser cookies
- Try again in an incognito window
