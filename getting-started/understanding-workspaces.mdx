A workspace is your central hub in Lindo.ai where you manage all your websites, clients, and settings. Each account has one workspace tied to your email address.

## What's in Your Workspace

Your workspace contains everything you need to build and manage websites:

- **AI Prompt Builder** — Create websites from text descriptions
- **Templates** — Pre-built designs to start from
- **Your Websites** — Grid of all sites you've created
- **CRM** — Client management for agency plans
- **Analytics** — Aggregated traffic data across all websites
- **Workflows** — Background automation for bulk operations
- **Settings** — White label, team, billing, API configuration

## Workspace Layout

When you log in, you see three main areas:

**Header** — Contains your logo (or Lindo.ai logo), workspace name, language selector, and user menu with support, feedback, and sign out options.

**Sidebar** — Navigation to CRM, Workflows, and Analytics. Also provides access to Settings. What you see depends on your role and plan.

**Main Area** — The AI Prompt Builder for creating websites, a templates panel on the right, and your websites grid below.

| Active Websites | 1 | 5 | 20 | 50 | 100+ |
| Monthly Credits | 50 | 500 | 2,000 | 5,000 | 10,000+ |
| Team Members | 1 | 3 | 10 | 25 | Unlimited |
| Clients (CRM) | — | — | 20 | 100 | Unlimited |

## White Label Your Workspace

On Elite plan and above, you can brand your workspace as your own:

- Custom domain (app.youragency.com)
- Your logo and favicon
- Custom login page background
- Clients see your brand, not Lindo.ai

## For Agencies

If you manage client websites, the workspace becomes your agency dashboard:

1. Add clients in the CRM
2. Allocate credits to each client
3. Clients log in to your branded workspace
4. They create and edit their own websites
5. You bill them through built-in invoicing
