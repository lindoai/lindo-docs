Connect with other Lindo.ai users in the Slack community.

## How to Join

1. Click your **avatar** in the header
2. Click **Community** from the dropdown
3. You'll be redirected to the Slack invite page
4. Enter your email to join
5. Check your email for the Slack invitation
6. Click the link to join the workspace

## What You'll Find

### Channels

- **#general** - General discussion and announcements
- **#help** - Ask questions, get answers from the community
- **#showcase** - Share websites you've built
- **#feature-requests** - Discuss and vote on features
- **#tips-tricks** - Share your workflows and discoveries

### Community Members

- Other Lindo.ai users
- Freelancers and agencies
- Power users with tips to share
- Lindo.ai team members

## Benefits of Joining

**Get Help Faster**
Community members often answer questions quickly, sometimes faster than support.

**Learn from Others**
See how others use Lindo.ai, discover new techniques.

**Share Your Work**
Get feedback on your websites, inspire others.

**Stay Updated**
Hear about new features and updates first.

**Network**
Connect with potential collaborators or clients.

## Community Guidelines

- **Be helpful** - Answer questions when you can
- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with kindness
- **Stay on topic** - Keep discussions relevant
- **No spam** - Don't promote unrelated services
- **Share knowledge** - Tips and tricks are welcome

## Getting the Most from Community

1. **Introduce yourself** - Share what you're building
2. **Ask specific questions** - Include details for better answers
3. **Search before asking** - Your question might be answered already
4. **Give back** - Help others when you can
5. **Share your wins** - Celebrate your launches
