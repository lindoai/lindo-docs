Create and publish a professional landing page in under 5 minutes.

## What You'll Build

A single-page landing page with:
- Hero section with headline and CTA
- Features or benefits section
- Social proof or testimonials
- Final call-to-action
- Footer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write Your Prompt (30 seconds)">
    In the AI Prompt Builder, enter:

    ```
    Landing page for [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE].
    Hero with headline "[YOUR MAIN MESSAGE]" and "Get Started" button.
    3 key benefits with icons.
    Customer testimonial.
    Final CTA section.
    [Light/Dark] mode, [COLOR] accent.
    ```

    **Example:**
    ```
    Landing page for TaskMaster, a todo app for busy professionals.
    Hero with headline "Get More Done, Stress Less" and "Try Free" button.
    3 key benefits with icons: Easy organization, Smart reminders, Team sync.
    Customer testimonial.
    Final CTA section.
    Dark mode, blue accent.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tools (15 seconds)">
    Click **(+)** and add:
    - **Mode**: Dark or Light
    - **Color Palette**: Your brand color
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate (60 seconds)">
    1. Make sure "single-page" is selected
    2. Click the **↑** submit button
    3. Wait for generation to complete
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quick Refinements (2 minutes)">
    Open your website and make quick edits:

    **Fix the headline:**
    ```
    Change the hero headline to "Your Tasks, Organized"
    ```

    **Update the CTA:**
    ```
    Change all "Get Started" buttons to "Start Free Trial"
    ```

    **Add urgency:**
    ```
    Add a badge above the hero headline that says "Now in Beta"
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish (30 seconds)">
    1. Click **Publish** in the preview header
    2. Your page is live at `yoursite.lindo.site`
    3. Share the link!
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Total Time: ~5 Minutes

## Pro Tips for Speed

1. **Be specific in your prompt** - More detail = fewer revisions
2. **Use templates** - Even faster than prompts
3. **Skip perfection** - Launch first, refine later
4. **Use keyboard shortcuts** - `Cmd/Ctrl + S` to save

## What's Next

After launching:
- Connect a custom domain
- Set up analytics tracking
- A/B test different headlines
- Add more pages
