Multiple ways to get help when you need it.

## Support Options

<Steps>
  <Step title="Documentation (You're Here!)">
    Browse articles for:
    - How-to guides
    - Feature explanations
    - Troubleshooting
    - Best practices
  </Step>
  <Step title="User Menu → Support">
    1. Click your avatar in the header
    2. Click **Support**
    3. Opens the support page with options:
       - Submit a ticket
       - Browse help articles
       - Contact information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slack Community">
    Join the Lindo.ai community:
    1. Click your avatar in the header
    2. Click **Community**
    3. Join the Slack workspace
    4. Ask questions, share tips, connect with other users
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hire an Expert">
    Need professional help?
    1. Click your avatar in the header
    2. Click **Hire Expert**
    3. Fill out the form describing your project
    4. Get matched with a Lindo.ai expert
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Submitting a Support Ticket

For account issues, bugs, or complex questions:

1. Go to Support (user menu → Support)
2. Click **Submit a Ticket**
3. Describe your issue in detail
4. Include:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Screenshots if helpful
   - Your workspace/website name
5. Submit and wait for response

## Before Contacting Support

Try these first:
1. **Search documentation** - Your answer might be here
2. **Check troubleshooting guides** - Common issues and fixes
3. **Try refreshing** - Many issues resolve with a page refresh
4. **Clear cache** - Browser cache can cause issues
5. **Try incognito mode** - Rules out extension conflicts

## What to Include in Support Requests

Help us help you faster:
- **Clear description** of the problem
- **Steps to reproduce** the issue
- **Expected vs actual** behavior
- **Screenshots or screen recordings**
- **Browser and device** information
- **Website URL** if relevant
