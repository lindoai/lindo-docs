Help improve Lindo.ai by sharing your feedback, feature requests, and suggestions.

## How to Submit Feedback

1. Click your **avatar** in the header (top right)
2. Click **Feedback** from the dropdown menu
3. A feedback form opens
4. Write your feedback
5. Submit

## Types of Feedback

### Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new feature?
- Describe what you want
- Explain why it would help
- Share your use case

**Example:**
```
I'd love a way to schedule blog posts for future publishing.
As a marketing agency, we prepare content in advance and
want to set publish dates.
```

### Bug Reports

Found something broken?
- Describe what happened
- Include steps to reproduce
- Mention your browser/device

**Example:**
```
When I try to upload a logo larger than 5MB, the upload
spinner keeps spinning forever. Expected: error message
about file size. Browser: Chrome on Mac.
```

### General Feedback

Share your experience:
- What you love
- What frustrates you
- What could be better

**Example:**
```
The AI prompt builder is amazing! Would be even better
if I could save my favorite prompts for reuse.
```

## Feedback Best Practices

**Be specific**
- "The editor is slow" → "The editor takes 5+ seconds to load on pages with 10+ sections"

**Include context**
- What were you trying to do?
- What plan are you on?
- How often does this happen?

**One topic per submission**
- Easier to track and address
- Gets faster responses

## What Happens to Your Feedback

1. **Reviewed** - Team reads every submission
2. **Categorized** - Sorted by type and priority
3. **Considered** - Evaluated for roadmap
4. **Implemented** - Popular requests get built
5. **Announced** - New features shared in updates

## Feedback vs Support

| Use Feedback For | Use Support For |
|------------------|-----------------|
| Feature requests | Account issues |
| Suggestions | Billing problems |
| General opinions | Bugs blocking your work |
| Ideas | Urgent issues |
