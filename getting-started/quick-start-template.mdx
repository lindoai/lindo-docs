Skip the blank page and start from a professionally designed template.

## Why Use Templates

- **Faster** - Skip the initial generation
- **Proven designs** - Professionally crafted layouts
- **Industry-specific** - Templates for your exact use case
- **Customizable** - Change everything with AI

## Finding Templates

Templates are in the right panel of your workspace dashboard.

### Browse by Category

Click category tabs to filter:
- All
- Agency & Consulting
- Automotive
- Beauty & Wellness
- E-commerce
- Education
- Events
- Finance
- Food & Restaurant
- Healthcare
- Real Estate
- SaaS & Technology
- Travel & Hospitality
- And more...

### Template Badges

- **FREE** - Available on all plans
- **AI Agent** - Includes AI-powered features

## Using a Template

1. **Browse** - Scroll through templates in your category
2. **Preview** - Hover to see a larger preview
3. **Select** - Click the template you want
4. **Customize** - The template opens in the editor

## Customizing Your Template

Once the template is loaded, use AI Chat to make it yours:

**Change the business name:**
```
Update all instances of the business name to "Your Company Name"
```

**Replace placeholder content:**
```
Update the hero section for a dental clinic in Chicago
```

**Change colors:**
```
Change the color scheme from blue to green
```

**Add sections:**
```
Add a FAQ section before the footer
```

## Expanding the Templates Panel

For a better view:
1. Click the **expand** icon on the templates panel
2. Browse templates in a larger grid
3. Click **collapse** to return to normal view

## Saving Your Own Templates

Created a design you love? Save it as a template:
1. In your website card, click the three-dot menu
2. Select **Save as Template**
3. Your template appears in your templates list
