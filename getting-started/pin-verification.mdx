When you sign up or log in with email, Lindo.ai sends a 6-digit PIN code to verify your identity.

## How PIN Verification Works

1. Enter your email address on the login/signup page
2. Click **Continue**
3. Check your email inbox for a message from Lindo.ai
4. Find the 6-digit PIN code in the email
5. Enter the PIN on the verification screen
6. You're signed in

## PIN Code Details

- **Length**: 6 digits
- **Expiration**: 10 minutes
- **Case**: Numbers only (0-9)

## Didn't Receive the PIN?

**Check spam/junk folder**
The email might be filtered. Look for an email from Lindo.ai.

**Wait a moment**
Email delivery can take 1-2 minutes. Be patient.

**Request a new PIN**
Click "Resend code" on the verification screen to get a fresh PIN.

**Check email address**
Make sure you entered the correct email address.

## PIN Expired?

If more than 10 minutes passed:
1. Click "Resend code"
2. A new PIN will be sent
3. Use the new PIN (old one is invalid)

## Security

- Each PIN can only be used once
- PINs expire after 10 minutes
- Failed attempts are rate-limited
- No password is stored—PIN verification is used for every login

## Why PIN Instead of Password?

Lindo.ai uses passwordless authentication:
- **More secure** - No password to steal or guess
- **No password resets** - Never forget your password
- **Fresh verification** - Each login is verified via email
