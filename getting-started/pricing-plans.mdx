Lindo.ai offers four paid plans to match your needs, from individual creators to large agencies.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Pro | Business | Elite | Reseller |
|---------|-----|----------|-------|----------|
| Price | $30/mo | $150/mo | $300/mo | $2,000/mo (yearly) |
| Monthly Credits | 500 | 1,500 | 3,000 | 25,000 |
| Active Websites | 1 | 10 | 20 | 200 |
| Pages per Website | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Custom Domain | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Team Members | Unlimited | 3 workspace | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| White Label | ✗ | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Client CRM | ✗ | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ |
| API Access | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

## Pro — $30/month

Best for business owners. Includes 500 credits/month, 1 active website, custom domains, unlimited team members, and no Lindo.ai branding.

## Business — $150/month

Best for freelancers. Includes 1,500 credits/month, 10 active websites, 3 workspace team members, and API access.

## Elite — $300/month

Best for agencies. Includes 3,000 credits/month, 20 active websites, white label branding, client CRM, and API access.

## Reseller — $2,000/month (billed yearly)

Best for resellers and SaaS builders. Includes 300,000 credits/year, 200 active websites, complete white label, unlimited clients, reseller website, and workspace embed.

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plans settings">
    Go to **Settings → Plans** in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare and select">
    Review the comparison table and click **Upgrade** on your desired plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Enter payment details and confirm. New features activate immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrading

You can downgrade anytime. Your current features remain until the billing period ends, then the new plan takes effect.

## Add-ons

Some features are available as add-ons:
- **Embed Webapp** — Embed your workspace in external sites
- **Extra Credits** — Purchase additional credits beyond your allocation
