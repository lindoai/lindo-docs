Build your first website in minutes using the AI Prompt Builder. No design skills needed—just describe what you want.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the AI Prompt Builder">
    After logging in, you're in your workspace dashboard. The AI Prompt Builder is front and center with a large textarea labeled "Build stunning websites with AI."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your website">
    Click the textarea and write a description of what you want to create. Be specific about your business, the sections you need, and the style you're going for.

    **Example prompt:**
    ```
    A modern landing page for a coffee shop called "Bean & Brew" in Portland.
    Include hero with shop interior photo, about section, menu highlights,
    location with hours, and contact info. Warm, cozy design.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add AI tools (optional)">
    Click the **(+)** button next to the textarea to add refinement tools:
    - **Language** — Set the content language (default: auto-detect)
    - **Mode** — Choose Dark or Light theme
    - **Color Palette** — Pick your brand colors
    - **Website URL** — Reference an existing site for design inspiration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose page structure">
    Click the dropdown (default: "single-page") to select:
    - **single-page** — All content on one scrolling page
    - **multi-page** — Multiple pages with navigation menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your website">
    Click the **↑** (submit) button. The AI will generate your business description, create the page structure, build each section with content and images, and apply styling. This takes 30-60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Website Editor">
    Your new website appears in the "Your Websites" grid below the prompt builder. Click the website card to open it in the Website Editor where you can make changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with AI Chat">
    In the Website Editor, use the AI Chat panel on the left to make changes:
    - "Make the hero headline larger"
    - "Add a testimonials section"
    - "Change the color scheme to blue"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your site">
    When you're happy with the result, click **Publish** in the preview header. Your site goes live at `yoursite.lindo.site` immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Better Results

- **Be specific** — Include your business name, industry, and desired sections
- **Mention style** — Words like "modern," "minimal," "playful," or "elegant" guide the design
- **List sections** — Hero, features, testimonials, pricing, FAQ, contact
- **Reference sites** — Use the Website URL tool to show examples you like
