AI credits are the currency for AI-powered features in Lindo.ai. Every AI operation—generating pages, editing sections, writing blog posts—consumes credits from your monthly allocation.

## How Credits Work

Your plan includes a monthly credit allocation. AI operations deduct credits from your balance. Credits reset on your billing date each month (unused credits don't roll over). You can purchase additional credits anytime if you run out.

## Credit Costs by Agent

| Agent | Credits | What It Does |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Website Builder | 10x (10 per page) | Generates business info and theme and all website pages |
| Page Builder | 10 | Creates page structure |
| Block Creator | 10 | Generates new section |
| Block Editor | 10 | Modifies existing section |
| Element Editor | 10 | Edits individual element |
| Simple Blog | 10 | Writes blog post |

## Monthly Allocation by Plan

| Plan | Monthly Credits |
|------|-----------------|
| Free | 50 |
| Pro | 500 |
| Business | 2,000 |
| Elite | 5,000 |
| Reseller | 10,000+ |

## Checking Your Balance

Your credit balance appears in:
- The toolbar in the AI Prompt Builder
- The website dropdown in the editor
- Settings → Credits

## Purchasing More Credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Credits settings">
    Go to **Settings → Credits** in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a package">
    Click **Purchase Credits** and choose a credit package.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Enter payment details and confirm. Credits are added immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Efficient Credit Usage

- **Plan before generating** — Clear, detailed prompts reduce the need for revisions
- **Use templates** — Start from templates instead of generating from scratch
- **Batch edits** — Make multiple changes in one session rather than many small requests
- **Review in preview** — Catch issues before publishing to avoid re-editing

## For Agencies

You can allocate credits to clients from the CRM:
- Set monthly credit limits per client
- Add one-time credits for specific projects
- Track client usage and history
