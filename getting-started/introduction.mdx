Lindo.ai is an AI-powered website builder that creates professional websites from text descriptions. Describe what you want, and the AI generates pages, content, images, and styling in seconds.

## What You Can Build

- **Landing Pages** — SaaS products, apps, services, campaigns
- **Business Websites** — Agencies, restaurants, real estate, fitness studios
- **Portfolios** — Photographers, designers, artists, freelancers
- **E-commerce** — Product pages with Stripe checkout
- **Blogs** — AI-generated posts with SEO optimization
- **Multi-page Sites** — Complete websites with navigation

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your website">
    Enter a text prompt describing what you want—your business, the sections you need, and the style you're going for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI generates it">
    The AI creates pages, sections, content, and images based on your description. This takes about 30-60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with AI">
    Chat with AI to make changes. Select a section and describe what you want different.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Go live on a free subdomain (yoursite.lindo.site) or connect your custom domain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
