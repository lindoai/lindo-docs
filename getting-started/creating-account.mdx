Get started with Lindo.ai by creating a free account. You can sign up with email or Google.

## Email Sign Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Lindo.ai">
    Open [lindo.ai](https://lindo.ai) in your browser and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up** in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type your email address in the input field and click **Continue**. We'll send you a verification code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify with PIN">
    Check your inbox for a 6-digit PIN code from Lindo.ai. Enter the code in the verification screen. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Once verified, you land in your workspace dashboard ready to create your first website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Google Sign Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Continue with Google">
    On the sign up page, click **Continue with Google** instead of entering an email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your account">
    Choose which Google account you want to use. If you're not logged into Google, you'll be prompted to sign in first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    Google will ask you to confirm that Lindo.ai can access your basic profile info. Click **Allow** to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    You're automatically logged in and land in your workspace dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Getting Started

Your new account starts with a trial period. Choose a plan to unlock all features:

| Plan | Price | Credits | Websites |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| Pro | $30/mo | 500/mo | 1 |
| Business | $150/mo | 1,500/mo | 10 |
| Elite | $300/mo | 3,000/mo | 20 |
| Reseller | $2,000/mo | 25,000/mo | 200 |

Go to **Settings → Plans** to select your plan and start building.
